# Melvyn Bragg steps down from presenting In Our Time

- Score: 291 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=45124143) | Link: https://www.bbc.co.uk/mediacentre/2025/melvyn-bragg-decides-to-step-down-from-presenting-in-our-time/

- TL;DR
    - Melvyn Bragg is stepping down as presenter of BBC Radio 4’s In Our Time after hosting every episode since 1998—over 1,000 shows. The program remains one of the BBC’s most-listened podcasts globally; Bragg will continue with Radio 4 and launch a new series in 2026, while a new IOT presenter is forthcoming. HN commenters praise his precise, audience-respecting moderation and the archive’s breadth, gripe about intrusive ads/geo-restrictions, debate the hub‑and‑spoke format versus deeper panel exchanges, and note signs of vocal fatigue late in his tenure.

- Comment pulse

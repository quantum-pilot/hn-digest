# Global Memory Shortage Crisis: Market Analysis

- Score: 74 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=46411902) | Link: https://www.idc.com/resource-center/blog/global-memory-shortage-crisis-market-analysis-and-the-potential-impact-on-the-smartphone-and-pc-markets-in-2026/

### TL;DR
IDC argues the current DRAM/NAND crunch isn’t a normal cycle but a structural reallocation of wafer capacity toward high‑margin AI memory (HBM, DDR5), leaving less for consumer devices. Supply growth in 2026 will lag history, pushing smartphone and PC prices up and volumes down. Low‑end Android vendors and white‑box PC builders are hit hardest, while Apple/Samsung and large OEMs are cushioned by scale and contracts. HN comments debate whether this is temporary AI froth or the end of “free” RAM and bloated software.

# Fathers’ choices may be packaged and passed down in sperm RNA

- Score: 286 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=46407502) | Link: https://www.quantamagazine.org/how-dads-fitness-may-be-packaged-and-passed-down-in-sperm-rna-20251222/

- TL;DR  
  Mouse work suggests that a father’s diet, exercise, stress, and toxin exposure can change small RNAs in sperm, loaded via epididymal and blood-borne vesicles. These RNAs tweak early embryonic gene regulation, altering offspring metabolism, toxin handling, and exercise capacity. Similar microRNA patterns in trained vs sedentary men hint at human relevance, but mechanisms, effect sizes, and persistence across generations remain uncertain. HN commenters riff on “versioned sperm” sci‑fi, caution against hype about “lived experience,” and broaden heredity beyond DNA-only metaphors.

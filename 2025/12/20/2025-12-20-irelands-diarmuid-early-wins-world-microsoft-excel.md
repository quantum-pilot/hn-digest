# Ireland’s Diarmuid Early wins world Microsoft Excel title

- Score: 155 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=46339031) | Link: https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cj4qzgvxxgvo

### TL;DR
Ireland’s Diarmuid “Dim” Early won the 2025 Microsoft Excel World Championship in Las Vegas, beating three-time champion Andrew Ngai in a high-pressure esports arena. The event turns Excel into a 30‑minute, level-based problem-solving race—mazes, poker hands, historical puzzles—where speed, creativity, and formula mastery are scored live before a cheering crowd. Early’s Excel fame now feeds his New York finance business and a YouTube channel, while a growing global community treats spreadsheets as both sport and geeky comedy.

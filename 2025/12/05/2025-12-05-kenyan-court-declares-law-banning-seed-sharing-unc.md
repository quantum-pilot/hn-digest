# Kenyan court declares law banning seed sharing unconstitutional

- Score: 293 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=46158578) | Link: https://apnews.com/article/kenya-seed-sharing-law-ruling-ad4df5a364299b3a9f8515c0f52d5f80

## TL;DR
Kenya’s High Court has struck down a law that criminalized sharing and selling uncertified seeds, ruling it unconstitutional and incompatible with farmers’ rights and food security. The law, heavily backed by commercial seed interests, aimed to protect intellectual property and prevent low‑quality or counterfeit seed, but also effectively outlawed the age‑old practice of saving and exchanging seeds. Hacker News discussion contrasts the genuine agronomic value of commercial hybrid seeds with deep concern over Big Ag monopolies, lawsuits, and loss of local biodiversity.

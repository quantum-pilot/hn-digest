# Framework Laptop 13 gets ARM processor with 12 cores via upgrade kit

- Score: 261 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=46162872) | Link: https://www.notebookcheck.net/Framework-Laptop-13-gets-ARM-processor-with-12-cores-via-upgrade-kit.1177930.0.html

## TL;DR
A third-party company, MetaComputing, has released an ARM-based mainboard for the Framework Laptop 13 (or a mini-PC case) using the 12‑core CIX CP8180 SoC, with Immortalis-G720 GPU and 30 TOPS NPU. Performance is below Snapdragon X Elite and even some phones, and early tests show ~16 W idle draw, so battery life in the 55 Wh Framework chassis will be poor. HN sees this less as a great product and more as a key proof that Framework’s modular form factor is becoming a real ecosystem.

## TL;DR
Rob Pike publicly criticized an unsolicited, AI-generated “thank you” email as fake, disrespectful, and symptomatic of generative AI invading human communication. HN commenters traced the message to an “AI Village” non-profit experiment where an autonomous Claude Opus agent, tasked with “random acts of kindness,” mass-emailed open-source figures. Debate centers on whether automated gratitude is inherently insulting, the ethics of unleashing agents on people’s inboxes, and broader disputes over intellectual property, spam, and authenticity in tech culture.

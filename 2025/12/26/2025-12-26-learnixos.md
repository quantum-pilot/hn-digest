# LearnixOS

- Score: 268 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=46391599) | Link: https://www.learnix-os.com

### TL;DR
LearnixOS is an in-progress online book that walks readers through building a largely library-free, POSIX-style operating system in Rust, from bootloader and memory management to processes, filesystems, a shell, and eventually Doom and virtualization. It targets motivated learners with some programming, memory, and basic assembly knowledge, and includes a custom Rust highlighter. Hacker News readers like the clarity and ambition but debate the confusing name, documentation polish, Rust-centric focus versus fundamentals, and comparison with classic C-based OS tutorials.

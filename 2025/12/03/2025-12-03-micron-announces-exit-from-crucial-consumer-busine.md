# Micron Announces Exit from Crucial Consumer Business

- Score: 287 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=46137783) | Link: https://investors.micron.com/news-releases/news-release-details/micron-announces-exit-crucial-consumer-business

### TL;DR
Micron is shutting down its Crucial consumer brand, exiting retail DRAM/SSD in favor of higher‑margin datacenter and AI memory. Commenters mourn loss of a trustworthy, traceable source of RAM for PC builders and fear more counterfeit or rebadged chips, higher prices, and less choice. Many see it as part of a broader shift where scarce compute and components increasingly go to hyperscalers, undermining “democratized” access to powerful hardware, while others argue the move is financially inevitable.

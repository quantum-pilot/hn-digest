# Interview with RollerCoaster Tycoon's Creator, Chris Sawyer (2024)

- Score: 271 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=46130335) | Link: https://medium.com/atari-club/interview-with-rollercoaster-tycoons-creator-chris-sawyer-684a0efb0f13

### TL;DR
Chris Sawyer describes RollerCoaster Tycoon as a side project that gradually overtook his planned Transport Tycoon 2 sequel: an experiment driven by fascination with coaster engineering rather than market analysis. He prototyped everything himself in x86 assembly, prioritizing smooth performance, feel, and variety of coaster types over strict realism or deep business mechanics. The isometric, pre-rendered look, simple finances, and iterative design emerged from hardware limits and trial-and-error. HN comments center on nostalgia, open-source successors, and the contrast between his solo efficiency and modern team rewrites.

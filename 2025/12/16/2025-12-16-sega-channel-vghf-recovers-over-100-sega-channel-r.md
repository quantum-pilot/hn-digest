# Sega Channel: VGHF Recovers over 100 Sega Channel ROMs (and More)

- Score: 210 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=46288024) | Link: https://gamehistory.org/segachannel/

## TL;DR

The Video Game History Foundation has recovered 144 Sega Channel ROMs plus internal Sega documents, effectively reconstructing the near-complete library and infrastructure of Sega’s 1994–1998 cable-based Genesis download service. The haul includes previously “lost” exclusives (like Garfield: The Lost Levels and a Flintstones game), cut‑down “limited editions,” prototypes, and a Genesis web‑browser experiment. Internal memos reveal why Sega Channel struggled—niche appeal, parental resistance, and rental-store competition—raising clear parallels to today’s subscription-only game platforms and their preservation risks.

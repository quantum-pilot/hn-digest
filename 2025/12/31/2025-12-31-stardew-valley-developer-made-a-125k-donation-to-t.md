# Stardew Valley developer made a $125k donation to the FOSS C# framework MonoGame

- Score: 502 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=46445068) | Link: https://monogame.net/blog/2025-12-30-385-new-sponsor-announcement/

### TL;DR
Stardew Valley creator Eric “ConcernedApe” Barone donated $125k to MonoGame, the open‑source C# framework he used to build his hit farming game. MonoGame is a low-level “bring your own tools” framework—more like a powerful library than a full engine with editors and scenes. Hacker News discussion highlights this as exemplary “giving back,” debates whether users of free software owe anything to creators, compares it with big-studio funding efforts, and dissects the romanticized story of solo game development.

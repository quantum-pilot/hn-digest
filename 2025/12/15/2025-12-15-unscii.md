# Unscii

- Score: 306 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=46270282) | Link: http://viznut.fi/unscii/

### TL;DR
Unscii is a family of tiny bitmap Unicode fonts designed to make old-school text graphics (ANSI art, PETSCII, Teletext, MUDs) render correctly on modern systems. It focuses on full support for pseudographic characters, including those added in Unicode 13’s “legacy computing” block, plus many still mapped via the Private Use Area. There are 8×8 and 8×16 variants and stylistic offshoots, tuned for terminals and programming use. HN discussion highlights related retro font packs, coverage gaps in NerdFonts, and Viznut’s demoscene pedigree.

# US Tech Force

- Score: 166 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=46277353) | Link: https://techforce.gov/

## TL;DR

The US Tech Force is a new White House‑backed, two‑year corps of about 1,000 technologists placed into federal agencies to modernize systems and accelerate AI projects, with advertised pay of $150–200k and a post‑program pipeline into private partners. Hacker News readers debate whether early‑career hires will actually receive GS‑14/15‑level compensation, question joining a politicized and recently unstable civil service, see this as a rebranded replacement for shuttered groups like 18F/USDS, and distrust the flashy, Trump‑centric branding.

## Comment pulse

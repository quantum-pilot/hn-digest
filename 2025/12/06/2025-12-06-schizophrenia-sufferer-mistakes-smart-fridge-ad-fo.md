# Schizophrenia sufferer mistakes smart fridge ad for psychotic episode

- Score: 472 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=46171425) | Link: https://old.reddit.com/r/LegalAdviceUK/comments/1pc7999/my_schizophrenic_sister_hospitalised_herself/

- TL;DR  
A viral story claimed a woman with schizophrenia thought a personalized Samsung smart-fridge ad was part of her psychosis. Commenters quickly show it’s almost certainly staged—an earlier Reddit post and Samsung’s response indicate the “full-screen” ad was fabricated—but the scenario feels plausible enough to alarm people. The thread turns into a broader indictment of ad-saturated “smart” devices, the commodification of attention, and the particular risks of intrusive, unavoidable ads for children, the elderly, and people with mental illness.

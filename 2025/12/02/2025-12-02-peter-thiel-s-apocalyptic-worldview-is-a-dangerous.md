# Peter Thiel's Apocalyptic Worldview Is a Dangerous Fantasy

- Score: 193 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=46122851) | Link: https://jacobin.com/2025/11/peter-thiel-palantir-apocalypse-antichrist

### TL;DR
The article argues Peter Thiel’s fixation on the Antichrist isn’t just weird theology but a political project: an “apocalyptic geopolitics” that maps global politics onto a cosmic battle of good vs evil, influenced by Nazi jurist Carl Schmitt’s katechon concept. In this framing, US power and Silicon Valley capitalism (especially Palantir) become holy “restrainers,” while regulation, multilateralism, and international law are cast as Antichrist. With Thiel embedded in AI‑military and surveillance infrastructure, the authors see his worldview as legitimizing imperial violence and tech oligarchy. Hacker News commenters largely focus on billionaire influence, attention economics, and Jacobin’s partisan framing.

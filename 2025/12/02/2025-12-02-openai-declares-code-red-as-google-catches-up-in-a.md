# OpenAI declares 'code red' as Google catches up in AI race

- Score: 384 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=46121870) | Link: https://www.theverge.com/news/836212/openai-code-red-chatgpt

### TL;DR
OpenAI CEO Sam Altman has reportedly declared an internal “code red,” pausing new monetization projects like ads, shopping and health agents, plus a personal assistant called Pulse, to concentrate staff on improving ChatGPT’s speed, reliability, personalization and coverage. The move comes as Google’s Gemini 3 and its broader AI suite gain users and beat rivals on many benchmarks. Hacker News discussion centers on OpenAI’s technical momentum, business vulnerability versus Google and open source, and whether “code red” signals focus or dysfunction.

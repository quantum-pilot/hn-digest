# Unifi Travel Router

- Score: 444 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=46371135) | Link: https://blog.ui.com/article/travel-in-style-unifi-style-unifi-travel-router

### TL;DR
UniFi’s new Travel Router is a pocket-sized gateway that lets existing UniFi users “take their network with them.” It binds to a UniFi site, recreating SSIDs, policies, and Teleport/VPN rules on first boot wherever it finds an uplink (Ethernet, WiFi-as-WAN, or 5G via USB-tethered phone). It auto-handles hotel captive portals, keeps LAN behavior consistent for all your devices, and centralizes control for people already invested in the UniFi ecosystem. Hacker News compares it heavily to GL.iNet travel routers, with tradeoffs around openness, privacy, and pack-light travel styles.

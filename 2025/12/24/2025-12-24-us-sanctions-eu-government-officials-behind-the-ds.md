# US sanctions EU government officials behind the DSA

- Score: 113 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=46374087) | Link: https://mastodon.social/@fj/115773761468906515

### TL;DR
US authorities have imposed visa sanctions on EU internal‑market commissioner Thierry Breton and “trusted flagger” entities that help enforce the Digital Services Act (DSA), branding EU enforcement as anti–free speech. Frédéric Jacobs frames this as part of a broader MAGA strategy to weaken the EU, cultivate nationalist allies, and preserve disinformation-friendly platforms for US-run psyops. Hacker News commenters largely see a sovereignty clash: EU transparency rules versus US tech and geopolitical interests, with growing strain in trans‑Atlantic digital governance.

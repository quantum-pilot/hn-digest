# CEO of health care software company sentenced for $1B fraud conspiracy

- Score: 124 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=46398752) | Link: https://www.justice.gov/opa/pr/ceo-health-care-software-company-sentenced-1b-fraud-conspiracy

### TL;DR
Gary Cox, 79-year-old CEO of telemedicine platform DMERx, was sentenced to 15 years and $452M restitution for orchestrating a $1B Medicare fraud. His company funneled seniors’ data from mailers and call centers into fake doctor orders for unneeded braces, creams, and other equipment, paying telemedicine doctors and marketers kickbacks for signatures and referrals. Insurers paid over $360M before investigators from multiple agencies shut it down. HN discussion focuses on deterrence, Medicare’s weak controls, and political double standards on health‑care fraud.

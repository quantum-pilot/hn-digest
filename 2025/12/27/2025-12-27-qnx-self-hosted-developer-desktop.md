# QNX Self-Hosted Developer Desktop

- Score: 262 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=46398201) | Link: https://devblog.qnx.com/qnx-self-hosted-developer-desktop-initial-release/

### TL;DR
The QNX team released an initial “Developer Desktop”: a QNX 8.0 desktop VM with compilers, editors, browser, and samples, enabling self-hosted development instead of traditional cross-compiling. The first image runs under QEMU on Ubuntu, with plans for Windows/macOS hosts, native x86/Raspberry Pi images, better docs, and CI integration. Hacker News responses mix enthusiasm and nostalgia—remembering the Photon GUI and legendary floppy-disk demo—plus respect for QNX’s engineering culture and surprise at seeing a modern desktop direction again.

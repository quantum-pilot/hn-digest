# Reasons not to become famous (2020)

- Score: 143 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=46345341) | Link: https://tim.blog/2020/02/02/reasons-to-not-become-famous/

## TL;DR
Ferriss describes how his teenage obsession with being “famous by 30” collided with reality after The 4-Hour Workweek made him a micro-celebrity. Fame amplified opportunities—friends, investing, philanthropy—but also scaled risk: stalkers, death threats, harassment of loved ones, extortion, identity theft, kidnapping risk, and constant invasions of privacy. His “tribe → village → city” metaphor says big audiences statistically guarantee some dangerous people. He concludes fame never fixes inner pain; it magnifies it, and that “1,000 true fans” may be a healthier goal. HN debates his credibility while largely agreeing fame’s downsides are underappreciated.

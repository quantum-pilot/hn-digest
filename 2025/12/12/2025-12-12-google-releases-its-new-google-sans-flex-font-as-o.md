# Google releases its new Google Sans Flex font as open source

- Score: 170 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=46246802) | Link: https://www.omgubuntu.co.uk/2025/11/google-sans-flex-font-ubuntu

## TL;DR
Google has open‑sourced Google Sans Flex, its new brand typeface, under the SIL Open Font License. The variable sans serif offers axes for weight, width, optical size, slant, and rounded terminals, and is designed to render crisply as UI text, including on Linux desktops. Current Linux environments can’t yet exploit its more advanced variable features, but HN readers still welcome the licensing move while debating glyph legibility, preferred coding fonts, and what this means for Google’s visual branding.

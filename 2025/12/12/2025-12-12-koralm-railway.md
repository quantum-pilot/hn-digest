# Koralm Railway

- Score: 290 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=46242871) | Link: https://infrastruktur.oebb.at/en/projects-for-austria/railway-lines/southern-line-vienna-villach/koralm-railway

### TL;DR
Austria’s Koralm Railway is a 130 km high‑speed line, including the 32.9 km Koralm Tunnel, linking Graz and Klagenfurt. It cuts passenger travel from three hours to 45 minutes, adds 23 modern stations, and shifts freight along the Baltic–Adriatic Corridor from road to lower‑carbon rail. HN commenters contextualize the 1998–2025 build timeline as reasonable for deep Alpine tunneling, link it to other base-tunnel megaprojects like Lyon–Turin, Brenner and Semmering, and share immediate benefits from shorter personal trips.

# String theory inspires a brilliant, baffling new math proof

- Score: 175 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=46245622) | Link: https://www.quantamagazine.org/string-theory-inspires-a-brilliant-baffling-new-math-proof-20251212/

- TL;DR  
  Mathematicians Maxim Kontsevich, Ludmil Katzarkov, Tony Pantev and Tony Yue Yu claim to have settled a 50‑year problem in classifying polynomial equations: they prove that generic cubic four‑folds (degree‑3 hypersurfaces in five variables) are not rationally parameterizable. Their proof imports string‑theory‑inspired ideas from homological mirror symmetry, decomposing Hodge structures into “atoms” controlled by curve‑counting invariants and a new transformation formula. The argument is so novel that global reading groups are struggling through it, and full validation may take years.

- TL;DR  
Florida just criminalized intentionally obscuring license plates, shortly after musician/YouTuber Benn Jordan (“The Flashbulb”) demoed an “adversarial” sticker pattern that confuses Flock Safety ALPR cameras. HN commenters debate whether Jordan’s jammer even works reliably, but focus more on civil-liberties: a private company running a mass vehicle-tracking network, intent-based laws that can be stretched by police, and whether a plate only needs to be human-readable or must also be machine-readable in an age of ubiquitous AI surveillance.

# Apple has locked my Apple ID, and I have no recourse. A plea for help

- Score: 1466 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=46252114) | Link: https://hey.paris/posts/appleid/

### TL;DR
An Apple developer with a 25-year-old Apple ID says his account was “permanently disabled” shortly after attempting to redeem a $500 gift card from a major retailer. The lock nuked access to iCloud Photos, iMessage, purchased apps/media, and effectively crippled all his Apple devices and his developer identity. Apple support won’t disclose a reason, won’t meaningfully escalate, and suggests “just make a new Apple ID,” which could itself be treated as evasion. HN sees this as a stark warning about platform lock-in, opaque fraud/AML systems, and the fragility of tying your digital life to a single corporate account.

# Advent of Code 2025

- Score: 688 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=46096337) | Link: https://adventofcode.com/2025/about

### TL;DR
Advent of Code 2025 is a December series of small programming puzzles, language‑agnostic and aimed at broad skill levels. This year it switches to 12 days of puzzles and removes the global leaderboard due to stress, abuse, and misleading comparisons. The site encourages careful reading, test‑case construction, and asking humans for hints, while explicitly discouraging AI solutioning. Hacker News discussion splits between concerns over actual accessibility of the puzzles and enthusiasm for AoC as a low‑pressure learning ritual.

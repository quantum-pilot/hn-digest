# Rebecca Heineman has died

- Score: 895 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=45960368) | Link: https://www.pcgamer.com/gaming-industry/legendary-game-designer-programmer-space-invaders-champion-and-lgbtq-trailblazer-rebecca-heineman-has-died/

## TL;DR
Rebecca Heineman, who became the first officially recognized U.S. videogame champion with a 1980 Space Invaders win, has died at 62 after an aggressive cancer diagnosis. She co‑founded Interplay, designed Bard’s Tale III, and became renowned for impossibly tight ports like Doom on 3DO and Another World on SNES, often hand‑optimizing in assembly. A visible trans, LGBTQ+ and accessibility advocate, she’s remembered in the article and HN comments as both a foundational technical talent and an extraordinarily kind mentor and colleague.

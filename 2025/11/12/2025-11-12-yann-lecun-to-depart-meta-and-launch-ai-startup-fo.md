# Yann LeCun to depart Meta and launch AI startup focused on 'world models'

- Score: 795 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=45897271) | Link: https://www.nasdaq.com/articles/metas-chief-ai-scientist-yann-lecun-depart-and-launch-ai-start-focused-world-models

- TL;DR
  - Reportedly, Yann LeCun is leaving Meta to found a startup on “world models,” aiming to learn from perception and interaction for physical-world reasoning over a decade-long horizon. The backdrop: Meta is pivoting to productized LLMs and a “superintelligence” push after Llama 4 underwhelmed, with reorgs, splashy hires, and investor pressure on AI spend. HN debates whether VC funding for research bets is rational or bubble, if demoting LeCun was overdue for shipping, and whether world models can outperform LLM-centric agents.

# Pakistani newspaper mistakenly prints AI prompt with the article

- Score: 461 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=45898789) | Link: https://twitter.com/omar_quraishi/status/1988518627859951986

- TL;DR
    - A Pakistani newspaper, Dawn, accidentally printed an AI assistant’s trailing “can make it punchier?” line at the end of a dry auto-sales story. The online version was later edited with a note admitting an AI-policy violation. HN debates: templated finance/sports pieces already read machine-made; why not use graphs instead of prose; and whether AI artifacts erode trust. Others cite similar slip-ups at Spiegel and in a paper. Some argue LLMs add value when grounded in real data and human judgment.

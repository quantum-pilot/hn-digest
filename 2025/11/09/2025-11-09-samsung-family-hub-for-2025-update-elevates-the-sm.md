# Samsung Family Hub for 2025 Update Elevates the Smart Home Ecosystem

- Score: 284 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=45866165) | Link: https://news.samsung.com/us/samsung-family-hub-2025-update-elevates-smart-home-ecosystem/

- TL;DR
  Samsung’s 2025 Family Hub update rolls out to recent fridges, bringing a unified One UI, Bixby Voice ID per-user profiles, expanded Knox Matrix security, and an AI Vision upgrade that recognizes 37 produce items and labels up to 50 packaged foods. A Cover-screen widget pilot adds news, calendar, weather—and optional ads with a settings toggle. HN readers fixate on privacy, reliability, and utility: skepticism about “blockchain” security, fear of data/ads creep, complaints about forced terms prompts, and doubts the $3k fridge’s food-ID is useful.

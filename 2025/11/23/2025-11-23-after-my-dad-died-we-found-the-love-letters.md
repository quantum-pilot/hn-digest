# After my dad died, we found the love letters

- Score: 745 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=46021825) | Link: https://www.jenn.site/after-my-dad-died-we-found-the-love-letters/

### TL;DR
A woman’s distant, often-absent Chinese father dies at 57; afterward she discovers he’d spent his last three years joyfully living with a male partner, Edward, after decades in the closet and a loveless, coerced marriage. She meets Edward, sees photos of a relaxed, radiant man she barely knew, and lets him say goodbye to the father’s ashes her mother now rejects. The essay sits between compassion and anger—grieving both his stolen life and the harm he inflicted by hiding.

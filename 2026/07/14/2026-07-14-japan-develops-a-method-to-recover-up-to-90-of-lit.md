# Japan develops a method to recover up to 90% of lithium from used EV batteries

- Score: 717 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48901569) | Link: https://tech.supercarblondie.com/japan-recovers-up-to-90-of-lithium-from-used-ev-batteries/

## TL;DR

Japanese researchers have demonstrated a lithium‑recycling process that reportedly recovers ~90% of lithium from used EV batteries, roughly doubling yields of many current methods. They replace the usual sodium hydroxide with recycled lithium hydroxide to process “black mass” into high‑purity lithium, while claiming about 40% lower CO₂ emissions than conventional recycling. For Japan, which imports nearly all its battery minerals, this could sharply improve supply security—if they solve collection (currently ~14% of used batteries) and scale it industrially by 2035.

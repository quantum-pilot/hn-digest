TL;DR  
Discussion centers on whether visual tricks like zebra-style “dazzle” paint can hide vehicles from modern killer drones. Commenters argue it fails against machine vision that keys on shape, motion, and context, and may even increase visibility. Historical naval dazzle mostly confused rangefinding, not detection. Effective protection is framed as an arms race: close‑in weapon systems and RF sensors versus cheap, swarming drones and wall‑seeing tech, alongside unease that future children may need to learn such survival skills.

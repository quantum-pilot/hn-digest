# The vintage beauty of Soviet control rooms (2018)

- Score: 190 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48868996) | Link: https://designyoutrust.com/2018/01/vintage-beauty-soviet-control-rooms/

### TL;DR
The post showcases mid‑20th‑century Soviet control rooms: walls of analog gauges, mimic diagrams, colored buttons, and soft, even lighting. Commenters note this style wasn’t uniquely Soviet—similar rooms existed in Western nuclear, transport, and utility infrastructure—but say Soviet interiors still had a distinct visual culture. Beyond nostalgia, many argue these panels embodied strong human-factors design: system-level overviews, clear causal flows, salient alarms, and excellent ergonomics that in some ways surpass modern software dashboards.

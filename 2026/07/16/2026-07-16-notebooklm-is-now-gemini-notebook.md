# NotebookLM is now Gemini Notebook

- Score: 218 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48936451) | Link: https://blog.google/innovation-and-ai/products/gemini-notebook/notebooklm-gemini-notebook/

### TL;DR
Google is rebranding its AI research tool NotebookLM as Gemini Notebook to align with its Gemini family while keeping it a standalone product. Beyond cosmetic branding, each notebook now runs on a “secure cloud computer” that can execute code for data analysis, initially for AI Ultra and Workspace users then Pro tiers. Tighter integration will sync notebooks across the Gemini app and, soon, Search. HN comments focus less on features and more on naming churn, org politics, and real-world usage.

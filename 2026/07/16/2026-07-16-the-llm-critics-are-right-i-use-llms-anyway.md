# The LLM Critics Are Right. I Use LLMs Anyway

- Score: 180 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48933310) | Link: https://www.theocharis.dev/blog/llm-critics-are-right-i-use-llms-anyway/

### TL;DR
Engineer Jeremy Theocharis describes “LLM dissonance”: he shares most criticisms—copyright violations, environmental cost, OSS slop, junior-developer displacement, geopolitical lock‑in, and ideological bias—yet spends thousands on LLM tokens each month. At Local‑First Conf, many attendees quietly use Claude while applauding anti‑LLM talks. He argues the difference isn’t AI vs no‑AI, but whether humans still do the thinking: he uses LLMs as amplifiers, with workflows like “grill‑me” prompts and adversarial sub‑agents to refine a few high‑stakes artifacts, while HN debates long‑term cognitive effects, over‑reliance, and mistrust of AI power brokers.

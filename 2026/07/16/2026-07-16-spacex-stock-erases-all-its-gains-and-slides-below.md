# SpaceX stock erases all its gains and slides below IPO price in intraday trading

- Score: 286 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48933344) | Link: https://www.latimes.com/business/story/2026-07-16/spacex-stock-erases-gains-slides-below-ipo-price-in-intraday-trading

### TL;DR
SpaceX’s stock briefly fell below its $135 IPO price, erasing a >50% post-listing surge before closing barely above water. The record IPO valued the Musk-led firm around $2.2 trillion, fueled by a rapid pivot from “space” to “AI”: merging xAI into SpaceX, acquiring coding startup Cursor for $60B, and building solar-powered orbital and terrestrial data centers that even rent compute to rivals. Investors now worry about debt-heavy AI expansion, extreme valuation, and looming employee share lockup expirations—while some compare the volatility to Facebook’s early post-IPO slump.

# 1,300 Beautiful Wildlife Illustrations from the 19th Century Now Restored

- Score: 237 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48929945) | Link: https://www.openculture.com/2026/07/explore-1300-beautiful-wildlife-illustrations-from-the-19th-century.html

### TL;DR
Designer Nicholas Rougeux has digitized and restored over 1,300 color wildlife plates from the 19th‑century series The Naturalist’s Library, a Victorian mass‑market natural history collection. The entire set is freely explorable on the web, with an optional $295 large‑format art book and posters. Rougeux used AI sparingly to stitch scans, repair seams, and correct faded colors, prompting discussion on authenticity, the aesthetic value of scientific illustration versus modern photography, and whether such works are best enjoyed as luxury books or app-like experiences.

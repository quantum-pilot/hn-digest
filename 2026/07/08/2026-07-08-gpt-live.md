# GPT‑Live

- Score: 570 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48834405) | Link: https://openai.com/index/introducing-gpt-live/

### TL;DR  
GPT‑Live is a new low‑latency, always‑listening voice interface to OpenAI models that can quietly delegate harder questions to a frontier model (described as GPT‑5.5), fixing the gap between chat and voice quality. Early users like it for brainstorming while walking or commuting but dislike “cute” interruptions and worry about AI‑girlfriend vibes instead of a serious tool. Others are frustrated that, like rival products, voice still can’t use tools/connectors, and a large thread debates whether such lifelike chat augments productivity or dangerously substitutes for real human relationships and deepens loneliness.

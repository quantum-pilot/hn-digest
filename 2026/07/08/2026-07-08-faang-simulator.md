# FAANG Simulator

- Score: 222 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48836778) | Link: https://www.abeyk.com/escape-the-rat-race/

### TL;DR
Browser game simulates a FAANG engineer’s career: applying, grinding, layoffs, side projects, and compensation choices. HN finds it painfully accurate as satire of modern dev life, yet notes unrealistic optimism around side‑project exits and job security. Commenters debate “hack life” advice like moving somewhere cheap and aiming for $85k instead of FAANG millions, pointing out constraints, layoffs, and high costs. Many request harder modes modeling immigration status, ageism, family obligations, and personalized financial trajectories.

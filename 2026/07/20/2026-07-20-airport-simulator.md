# Airport Simulator

- Score: 672 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48976846) | Link: https://airport.apunen.com/

- TL;DR  
  A small web-based airport traffic “simulator” lets you drag color-coded planes to matching runway thresholds, drawing paths to manage landings and departures. It’s simple, a bit physics-free (instant 180°/360° turns, no real collision avoidance), and visually cluttered by stats. HN enjoys it as a quick, nostalgic toy reminiscent of Flight Control and classic ATC games, offering UI improvements and realism tweaks, and pointing to more advanced sims and code-driven traffic-control challenges for deeper engagement.

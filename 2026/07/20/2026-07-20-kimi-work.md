# Kimi Work

- Score: 345 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48981703) | Link: https://www.kimi.com/products/kimi-work

## TL;DR
Kimi Work is a desktop “AI employee” that mounts local folders, automates browser and cron-like tasks, runs Python, and integrates finance data for research and reporting. HN readers see it as a near-clone of OpenAI’s Codex/Anthropic tools, illustrating how agentic UIs are rapidly commoditizing while competition shifts to model quality and price. Discussion centers on Chinese labs undercutting US incumbents, disputes over price–performance claims, and criticism of Kimi’s privacy messaging around broad filesystem read access.

# China’s open-weights AI strategy is winning

- Score: 922 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48979269) | Link: https://werd.io/american-ai-is-locked-down-and-proprietary-its-losing/

### TL;DR
- The article argues that foundation models are already commoditized; the real moat is services and integration. Because US export controls limit Chinese cloud offerings, Chinese labs are leaning into open-weight releases, turning a compute disadvantage into global distribution power and commoditizing US vendors’ profit center. Commenters broadly agree that cheap, local models and open weights will dominate many workloads, but debate sustainability, China’s motives, and whether US frontier models will remain a profitable high-end niche.

# Human mathematicians are being outcounterexampled

- Score: 162 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48983382) | Link: https://xenaproject.wordpress.com/2026/07/20/human-mathematicians-are-being-outcounterexampled/

## TL;DR
AI-assisted theorem provers plus large language models are now routinely generating and formally verifying deep mathematical counterexamples: to Erdős’s unit distance conjecture, a 60‑year‑old question of Grothendieck on finite group schemes, and even the century‑old Jacobian Conjecture. Systems like Sol, Fable, and Logos can autoformalize proofs in Lean at scales rivalling mathlib, turning verification into a compilation task. The bottleneck is shifting from “is this correct?” to “what does this example mean?”, raising new questions about insight, ethics, access, and training.

# Wicklow hotel cancels 'secretive' Peter Thiel group conference

- Score: 107 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48787987) | Link: https://www.irishtimes.com/ireland/2026/07/03/wicklow-hotel-cancels-secretive-peter-thiel-conference/

- TL;DR  
  An upscale hotel in Wicklow, Ireland reportedly cancelled a private conference linked to Peter Thiel’s invitation-only Dialog group after journalists exposed a leaked attendee list and agenda, including a “cult-building” session. The event had been planned as a secretive retreat for billionaire and political elites. Commenters argue over whether this is just a normal private corporate gathering or something more opaque and ideologically driven, and whether hotels should refuse controversial clients once media scrutiny arrives.

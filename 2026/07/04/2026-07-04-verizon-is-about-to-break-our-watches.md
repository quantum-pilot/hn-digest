# Verizon is About to Break our Watches

- Score: 121 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48787329) | Link: https://www.jefftk.com/p/verizon-is-about-to-break-our-watches

### TL;DR
Verizon is reportedly rolling out changes that are stranding some cellular smartwatches—especially kids’ “watch-only” lines—because their provisioning and account systems can’t reliably migrate or reattach lines. Commenters describe fragile, fraud-prone billing backends where fixing watch LTE often requires multi-level support or line recreation. The situation exposes how carrier infrastructure for add‑on devices is a fragile stack of hacks, and how reliance on SMS plus carrier policies (e.g., blocking Google Fi/Voice numbers) compounds lock‑in and reliability problems.

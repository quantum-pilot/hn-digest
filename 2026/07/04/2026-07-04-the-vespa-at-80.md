# The Vespa at 80

### TL;DR
At 80 years old, the Vespa remains an instantly recognizable design icon, but Hacker News commenters split between nostalgia and modern expectations. Some recall smoky, chainsaw-loud 2‑stroke Vespas and see them as obsolete next to quiet, torquey electric scooters. Others note that recent 4‑stroke, 300cc models are clean, quick, and surprisingly comfortable, even for long rides, and that electric Vespas already exist. Owners of classic 1960s models still adore them, quirks like fragile aluminum threads and all.

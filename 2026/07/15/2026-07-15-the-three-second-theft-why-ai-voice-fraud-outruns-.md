# The Three-Second Theft: Why AI Voice Fraud Outruns Every Defence

- Score: 164 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48920432) | Link: https://smarterarticles.co.uk/the-three-second-theft-why-ai-voice-fraud-outruns-every-defence

- TL;DR
  - AI voice cloning can mimic a loved one from seconds of public audio, making “grandparent scams” cheap, scalable, and emotionally devastating. Losses are surging, especially among older adults, while even top deepfake experts say they can’t reliably spot fakes, so detection and “be vigilant” advice are fundamentally inadequate. The article argues for structural fixes: regulate cloning tools with consent requirements, strengthen telecom and provenance standards, and most importantly, shift financial liability to banks so they build real friction before money moves.

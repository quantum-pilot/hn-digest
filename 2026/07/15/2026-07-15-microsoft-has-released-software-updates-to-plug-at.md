# Microsoft has released software updates to plug at least 570 security holes

- Score: 197 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48913190) | Link: https://krebsonsecurity.com/2026/07/microsoft-patches-a-record-570-security-flaws/

### TL;DR
Microsoft’s latest Patch Tuesday fixes a record 570 vulnerabilities across Windows and related products, including ~60 critical bugs and three zero‑days, two already exploited for privilege escalation. AI‑assisted analysis is driving the sharp jump in discovered flaws, but attackers are also using AI to generate exploits, undermining Microsoft’s human‑centric “exploitability index.” Other vendors (Adobe, Cisco, Mozilla, Oracle, Google) are also accelerating patch cycles. Commenters debate whether AI‑found issues are meaningful, and worry about instability and reboot loops from such massive updates.

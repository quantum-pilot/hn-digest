# Collection of Digital Clock Designs

- Score: 164 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48923380) | Link: https://clocks.dev

### TL;DR
A small site showcases many experimental digital clock designs, from geometric art and word grids to binary and SVG-based faces. Each clock is interactive, and all are open source, with a simple “create your own” flow that invites playful remixing. Hacker News commenters treat it as part of an emerging “clock art” microculture: sharing their own watchface projects, debating design clarity and encoding choices, and joking about losing track of time while exploring the gallery.

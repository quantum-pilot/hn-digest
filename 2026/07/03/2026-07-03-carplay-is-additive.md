# CarPlay Is Additive

- Score: 540 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48769397) | Link: https://www.caseyliss.com/2026/7/2/carplay-is-additive-you-dolts

- TL;DR  
CarPlay/Android Auto are seen as “additive” layers on top of weak built‑in infotainment: they bring consistency across cars, reuse your phone’s UI, apps, and updates, and future‑proof long‑lived vehicles whose native systems age badly. Many buyers now treat phone integration as a must‑have, especially rental‑car regulars. A minority don’t care or prefer HUDs/phone mounts. Some Rivian/Tesla owners say excellent native software can offset the lack of CarPlay, which may explain why a few automakers are backing away.

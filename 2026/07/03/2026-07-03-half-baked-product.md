# Half-Baked Product

- Score: 1191 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48772388) | Link: https://weli.dev/blog/half-baked-product/

### TL;DR
A satirical “pizza oven” startup story exposes a familiar pattern: founders motivated mainly by getting rich, little domain expertise, and glossy, AI-flavored products that don’t really work. Sales overpromise, engineers are stuck implementing fantasy specs, and buyers prioritize buzzwords, demos, and vendor size over reliability or fit. Commenters connect this to real startups: vibecoded AI platforms seeking customer data, Musk-style mythologizing of domain-jumping founders, chronic misalignment between sales/engineering/customers, and open-source “OpenOven” projects out-innovating but losing deals to safer, bigger vendors.

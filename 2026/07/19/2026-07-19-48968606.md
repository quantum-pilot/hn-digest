# Show HN: I replaced a $120k bowling center system with $1,600 in ESP32s

- Score: 1308 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48968606) | Link: https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48968606

### TL;DR
A bowling center owner replaced a $120k commercial scoring/control system with about $1,600 of ESP32-based hardware, building his own lane control, scoring integration, and future features like LED/DMX light shows and tap‑to‑pay. Hacker News commenters share similar retrofits of bowling machines and industrial equipment, highlighting how cheap microcontrollers and simple protocol converters can modernize decades‑old hardware. Discussion ranges from nostalgic relay logic and pinsetters to practical advice on lighting protocols (DMX, Art‑Net, sACN) for immersive lane experiences.

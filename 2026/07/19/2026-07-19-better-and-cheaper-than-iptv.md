# Better and Cheaper Than IPTV

- Score: 322 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48964015) | Link: https://github.com/stupside/castor

- TL;DR  
Castor is a CLI tool that lets you cast almost any non‑DRM web video directly to a smart TV at full quality, avoiding laggy screen mirroring. It launches headless Chrome, inspects network traffic to locate the actual media stream, then uses ffmpeg/ffprobe to transcode and stream to DLNA (and experimental Chromecast) devices. You can configure your own content sources, integrate TMDB for title search, and generate burned‑in subtitles via Whisper, all controlled from a YAML config and terminal UI.

# AI Mania Is Eviscerating Global Decision-Making

- Score: 379 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48964185) | Link: https://ludic.mataroa.blog/blog/ai-mania-is-eviscerating-global-decision-making/#fnref:3

### TL;DR
The author, a data consultant, says every corporate “AI initiative” they’ve seen over the last 18 months has effectively failed, usually because organizations are already bad at software projects and then bolt on risky, poorly understood LLMs driven by executive hype. Public professions of AI faith become mandatory, dissenters are sidelined, and leaders quietly AI‑wash normal work to satisfy boards and peers. This creates cult-like decision dynamics where meaningful priorities stall; the author offers pragmatic tactics for quietly mitigating or escaping such environments.

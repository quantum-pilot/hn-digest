# CoMaps – FOSS Offline Maps

- Score: 272 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48808928) | Link: https://www.comaps.app/

### TL;DR
CoMaps is a new fully open-source offline maps app, forked from the Organic Maps / Maps.me lineage and built on OpenStreetMap data. Discussion focuses on its solid offline navigation, privacy-friendly design, and frequent map updates, but also on persistent weaknesses in OSM-based search compared with proprietary services. The fork is framed as a response to governance and proprietary-component concerns in Organic Maps, and commenters highlight companion tools like StreetComplete for crowdsourcing detailed map improvements.

### Comment pulse
- CoMaps works reliably offline with periodic map-update prompts; users pair it with StreetComplete quests to enrich OSM data, especially accessibility and local details.  
- OSM-based apps are hampered by weak search: poor ranking, mixed queries, and filters; some rely on HERE WeGo for routing instead—counterpoint: few fully FOSS alternatives.  
- Fork arose from dissatisfaction with Organic Maps governance: shareholder-driven decisions, partnerships, and proprietary code conflicting with “community-driven” branding.

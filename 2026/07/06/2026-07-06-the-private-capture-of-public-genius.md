# The Private Capture of Public Genius

- Score: 179 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48799178) | Link: https://www.wysr.xyz/p/the-private-capture-of-public-genius

- TL;DR
    - The essay argues that frontier AI labs are privatizing the value of humanity’s shared “corpus” of knowledge while degrading the information ecosystem that created it. It proposes a mandatory “Corpus Fund” fee on AI training and inference, with revenues paid as equal annual cash dividends to all Americans, analogous to a resource royalty. Hacker News discussion questions the US-only scope, whether LLMs really constitute the scientific frontier, and whether funds should instead restore and protect the digital commons.

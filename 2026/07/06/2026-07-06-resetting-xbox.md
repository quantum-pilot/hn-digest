# Resetting Xbox

- Score: 447 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48804993) | Link: https://news.xbox.com/en-us/2026/07/06/resetting-xbox/

### TL;DR
Microsoft’s Xbox division is undergoing a major reset after years of thin profits (~3% margin) despite about $5B in quarterly revenue. The Phil Spencer era bet on mass studio acquisitions and a Netflix-style Game Pass with day-one releases, aiming for ~30% margins; instead it appears to have cannibalized sales and under-delivered output. New chief Asha is unwinding parts of that strategy, cutting or spinning out studios and admitting Microsoft isn’t the right home for every team, sparking debate over business models, creativity, and leadership.

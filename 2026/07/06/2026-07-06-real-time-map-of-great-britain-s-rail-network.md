# Real-time map of Great Britain's rail network

- Score: 379 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48802535) | Link: https://www.map.signalbox.io

### TL;DR
A new real-time map visualizes Great Britain’s rail network, attracting attention for both its technical approach and its comparisons to similar tools abroad. Commenters contrast it with Swiss and French maps that often interpolate train locations from timetables and delay data rather than true positions, raising questions about what “real-time” really means. Others focus on how this UK system infers train locations from anonymized smartphone data, debating its practical usefulness, privacy implications, and how it highlights the relative weakness of US intercity rail.

- Score: 127 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48865093) | Link: https://www.tryai.dev/blog/gpt-5.6-build-off-12-models

- TL;DR  
  Article benchmarks multiple frontier models (GPT-5.6, Grok 4.5, Claude, Muse Spark, others) by having them one-shot build four web apps, plus latency/cost tests. GPT-5.6 comes off fastest on short prompts, with some cheaper models like Qwen surprisingly competitive and others lagging. HN readers question whether one-shot greenfield app generation or visual tasks really capture real-world reasoning and software work. A big part of the thread fixates on the piece’s AI-like, verbose writing style.

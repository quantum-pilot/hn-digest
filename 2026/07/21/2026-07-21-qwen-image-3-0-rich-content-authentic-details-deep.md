  Qwen-Image-3.0 is Alibaba’s third‑gen image model focused on “Real”: handling 4.5k‑token prompts for dense layouts, rendering 10px text and photo‑like textures, and drawing multilingual, UI‑heavy, knowledge‑rich scenes.

# Laguna S 2.1

- Score: 215 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48995261) | Link: https://poolside.ai/blog/introducing-laguna-s-2-1

## TL;DR

Laguna S 2.1 is Poolside’s new 118B-parameter MoE coding model (8B active) with a 1M-token context and a “thinking” mode that allocates long test-time reasoning. It targets long-horizon software-engineering and terminal tasks and scores near frontier closed models on Terminal-Bench 2.1 and SWE-Bench, while strongly overperforming its size on DeepSWE. Case studies show it autonomously building a browser engine, optimizing Poolside’s own harness, and re-deriving an Erdős problem. Open weights, aggressive RL, and agent-focused data underpin the gains.

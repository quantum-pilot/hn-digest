# Gemini 3.6 Flash, 3.5 Flash-Lite, and 3.5 Flash Cyber

- Score: 588 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48993414) | Link: https://blog.google/innovation-and-ai/models-and-research/gemini-models/gemini-3-6-flash-3-5-flash-lite-3-5-flash-cyber/

### TL;DR
Google launches three Gemini variants aimed at large-scale “agentic” workloads rather than frontier IQ: 3.6 Flash (main workhorse), 3.5 Flash-Lite (cheapest and fastest), and 3.5 Flash Cyber (cybersecurity-tuned, gov/partner-only through CodeMender). 3.6 Flash improves coding, knowledge work, and multimodal tasks while cutting tokens and price versus 3.5 Flash; Flash-Lite maximizes throughput for search‑like and bulk processing. HN sees the models as strong on speed and economics but mid‑pack on raw capability, amid frustration with Google’s AI product strategy.

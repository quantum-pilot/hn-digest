# Irish datacenters now guzzle 23% of the country's electricity

- Score: 190 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48884322) | Link: https://www.theregister.com/on-prem/2026/07/11/irish-datacenters-now-guzzle-23-of-the-countrys-electricity/5270013

### TL;DR
Irish data centers now consume about 23% of national electricity, raising fears about grid strain, higher prices, and climate targets. Commenters argue this is simply economic activity: Ireland exports digital services and gains tax, jobs, and infrastructure. Critics counter that locals shoulder higher costs and environmental risks while much of the value accrues to foreign firms and AI-fueled “junk” workloads. The real battleground is pricing: whether power, infrastructure, and externalities are correctly charged to data centers versus households.

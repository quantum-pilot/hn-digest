# Under federal rule, colleges must leave grads better off or lose financial aid

- Score: 187 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48878126) | Link: https://www.npr.org/2026/06/30/nx-s1-5835631/turner-camhi-do-no-harm-college-loans

### TL;DR
A new federal “do no harm” rule would cut off student loans and grants to specific degree programs whose graduates, four years out, earn less than typical high‑school graduates in the same state. Supporters say taxpayers shouldn’t underwrite chronically low‑ROI degrees and expect this to pressure colleges on pricing and outcomes. Critics argue the metric is backward-looking, punishes socially valuable but low-paid fields like teaching and arts, and is a backdoor attack on public higher‑ed subsidies.

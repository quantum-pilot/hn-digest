# Why are US consumers so angry? It's not just high prices

- Score: 120 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48877927) | Link: https://www.theguardian.com/us-news/ng-interactive/2026/jun/04/us-consumer-rage-prices-economy

- TL;DR  
US consumers aren’t just mad about inflation; they feel trapped in an economy where essentials are unaffordable, product quality is collapsing, and companies hide behind algorithms, junk fees, and impenetrable terms. Massive platforms and subscription models decouple revenue from service, so nobody feels responsible when things break. HN commenters describe every purchase as a high‑stakes gamble, with no trustworthy mid‑range options, while small, owner‑run businesses and stronger consumer protections look like the few remaining paths to dignity.

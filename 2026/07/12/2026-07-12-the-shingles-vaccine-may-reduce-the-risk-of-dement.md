# The shingles vaccine may reduce the risk of dementia

- Score: 204 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48881874) | Link: https://www.economist.com/leaders/2026/07/09/a-no-brainer-for-protecting-your-brain

- TL;DR  
  Observational studies suggest the shingles (zoster) vaccine is associated with lower dementia diagnoses, notably in a UK cohort split by an age-eligibility cutoff. Commenters discuss this as intriguing support for the “latent virus” contribution to neurodegeneration, and some consider paying out-of-pocket to vaccinate earlier than guidelines. Others argue the dementia benefit is likely spurious, driven by healthy-vaccinee and diagnostic biases, noting randomized trials show protection against shingles itself but not convincing reductions in dementia.

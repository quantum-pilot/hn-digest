# Autopsy Study Finds Replicating SARS-CoV-2 in the Hearts of Long Covid

- Score: 125 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48877214) | Link: https://my.uscap.org/uscap/program/S0tc675/index.cfm?pgid=5167&sid=14770&abid=51228

- TL;DR  
Autopsy researchers reportedly found actively replicating SARS‑CoV‑2 in the hearts of people who died with long COVID, supporting a viral‑persistence explanation for lingering cardiac symptoms. Commenters connect this to broader evidence that infections can cause chronic damage and argue for aggressively minimizing disease via vaccination, while noting rare but serious vaccine harms. Others review negative Paxlovid trials in long COVID and suggest future treatments must target patients with demonstrable ongoing replication or residual viral proteins in specific tissues.

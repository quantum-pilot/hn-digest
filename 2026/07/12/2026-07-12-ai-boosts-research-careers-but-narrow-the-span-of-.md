# AI boosts research careers but narrow the span of ideas explored: study

- Score: 135 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48881043) | Link: https://spectrum.ieee.org/ai-science-research-flattens-discovery

### TL;DR
Study of scientists using AI tools finds substantial career benefits: triple publication output, ~5x citations, faster promotion to leadership roles. But AI assistance correlates with narrower topic choice and more incremental, citation-optimized work, suggesting converging research agendas. Commenters argue this reflects existing incentive structures—publish-or-perish metrics, funding tied to volume, and co-authorship games—more than properties of AI itself. Others note AI can free time for real discovery, but also makes gaming bad productivity measures easier and faster.

# Woman in Brazil enslaved for 55 years by 3 generations of the same family

### TL;DR
An Afro-Brazilian woman was kept as an unpaid domestic servant for 55 years by three generations of one family, only recently recognized as a victim of modern slavery. Authorities arranged about $40k compensation, which commenters call insulting compared to decades of minimum wages and lost life. Discussion centers on Brazil’s history of slavery and racism, normalized live‑in domestic servitude worldwide, how inequality enables quasi‑slavery, and whether weak punishment shows how elites still evade accountability.

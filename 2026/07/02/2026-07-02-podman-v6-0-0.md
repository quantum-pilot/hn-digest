# Podman v6.0.0

- Score: 363 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48762098) | Link: https://blog.podman.io/2026/07/introducing-podman-v6-0-0/

- TL;DR  
    - Podman 6.0 marks another step in positioning Podman as a daemonless, rootless alternative to Docker, with notable attention to networking and systemd/Quadlet workflows. HN commenters praise its speed, security model, and server/homelab ergonomics, and several report painless Docker-to-Podman migrations. However, Docker still dominates due to smoother desktop/macOS support, simpler onboarding, and more reliable docker‑compose behavior. Users warn that Podman’s “Docker‑compatible” promise hides subtle differences in SELinux, rootless assumptions, and networking that can surprise newcomers.

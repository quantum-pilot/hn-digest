# Virginia bans sale of geolocation data

- Score: 331 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48767347) | Link: https://www.hunton.com/privacy-and-cybersecurity-law-blog/virginia-bans-sale-of-geolocation-data

### TL;DR
- Virginia has passed a law banning the sale of geolocation data, targeting data brokers and ad-tech markets that quietly trade people’s movements. Commenters praise it as a necessary curb on exploitative tracking—especially around sensitive locations like clinics—while debating whether consent via product use is inherently coercive. Others focus on enforcement details: how the law will reach out‑of‑state companies, whether it has real penalties, and how cleanly it distinguishes harmful brokerage from useful first‑party uses like navigation.

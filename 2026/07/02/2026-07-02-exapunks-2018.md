# Exapunks (2018)

- Score: 208 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48765663) | Link: https://www.zachtronics.com/exapunks/

### TL;DR
Exapunks, a 2018 Zachtronics programming puzzle game, is remembered as one of the best “programming-as-play” experiences: you write tiny assembly-like programs to solve heist-style puzzles, then iteratively optimize for elegance and efficiency, often competing with friends. Commenters say it captures what makes coding fun without overwhelming complexity, and even helped some overcome fear of low-level programming. The thread also notes Zachtronics’ closure, Zach Barth’s new studio Coincidence Games, praise for the wider catalog, and light pushback on stealthy self-promotion.

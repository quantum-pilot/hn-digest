# Vite+ Beta

- Score: 217 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48759761) | Link: https://voidzero.dev/posts/announcing-vite-plus-beta

- TL;DR  
Vite+ Beta bundles Vite with a curated stack—Vitest for testing, Rolldown/tsdown for bundling, Oxlint/Oxfmt for linting/formatting—aiming to be a fast, “boring but works” frontend toolchain. Much of it is written in Rust for speed and shares config and conventions, reducing the need to assemble disparate tools. Commenters praise performance and simplicity but raise concerns about Vite’s frequent major versions, added abstraction layers, naming confusion, and how Cloudflare’s acquisition will shape long-term, cloud-neutral development.

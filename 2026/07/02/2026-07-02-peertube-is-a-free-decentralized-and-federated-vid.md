# PeerTube is a free, decentralized and federated video platform

- Score: 489 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48759634) | Link: https://github.com/Chocobozzz/PeerTube

### TL;DR
PeerTube is an open-source, federated video hosting system that decentralizes storage and uses optional P2P sharing, but largely leaves out recommendation algorithms and built‑in monetization. HN commenters argue this makes it a poor YouTube replacement for professional creators who need ad revenue and discovery, yet a strong fit for FOSS projects, institutions, and niche communities that value control, no ads, and reduced platform risk. Many see it less as a mass platform and more as backend infrastructure for independent video sites.

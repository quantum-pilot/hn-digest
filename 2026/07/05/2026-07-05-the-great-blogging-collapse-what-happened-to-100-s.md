# The great blogging collapse: What happened to 100 successful blogs?

- Score: 154 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48758802) | Link: https://danielstanica.com/posts/Great-Blogging-Collapse

- TL;DR  
A study tracked 100 once-successful, mostly monetized lifestyle blogs and found many abandoned or with collapsing traffic and revenue. Commenters debate whether this shows blogging’s death or just churn as creators and audiences migrate to Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, Substack and other formats. Others blame broken discovery: loss of blogrolls, Google search changes, and ad incentives that favor corporate content farms over niche experts. Some still blog mainly for personal satisfaction, but expect future survival to depend on offering unique insight beyond what AI and SEO-driven sites can replicate.

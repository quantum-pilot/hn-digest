# EU Council forces Chat Control via fast-track

- Score: 381 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48793393) | Link: https://www.heise.de/en/news/Chat-Control-1-0-EU-Council-forces-messenger-scans-via-fast-track-11353659.html

### TL;DR
EU national governments in the Council reportedly fast-tracked a renewal of “Chat Control 1.0,” letting platforms keep scanning private messages for CSAM after a prior exemption expired. Critics see this as mass surveillance by stealth and an undemocratic override of sunset clauses. Commenters note the more radical “2.0” plan to weaken end-to-end encryption appears stalled thanks to protests and Parliament resistance. Discussion splits between distrust of EU elites, legal/constitutional pushback, and building decentralized alternatives.

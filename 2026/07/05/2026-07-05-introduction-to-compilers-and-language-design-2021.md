### TL;DR
An open, semester-length text and course on compilers guides students through building a working C‑like compiler step by step, prioritizing practicality over heavy theory. HN commenters praise the author’s teaching and the project-based approach, while noting its tight focus on C mirrors the book’s explicit goals. The discussion contrasts it with classic texts like the dragon book—seen as theoretical and dated—and points to tiny self-hosting compilers such as C4 as complementary study material.

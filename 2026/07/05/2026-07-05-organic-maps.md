# Organic Maps

- Score: 768 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48794446) | Link: https://organicmaps.app/

## TL;DR
Organic Maps is a popular offline navigation app built on OpenStreetMap data, but commenters highlight growing concerns: parts of its stack (notably the map data format and generation pipeline) are now proprietary, ads and commercial partnerships appeared, and governance and finances are controlled by a small group. This led to CoMaps, a community fork positioning itself as fully FOSS, with extras like Android Auto and experimental CarPlay support. Users compare both to OsmAnd, debate UX, and note region naming quirks like “China Hong Kong.”

# Small Penis Rule

- Score: 122 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48797015) | Link: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Small_penis_rule

TL;DR
- The “Small Penis Rule” is a writers’ legal hack: base a fictional character on a real person, then add an obviously humiliating trait (e.g., small penis) so they hesitate to sue, since suing publicly concedes the resemblance. HN treats it as a game-theory tactic similar to token bequests in wills and “outing yourself” idioms, but several commenters question whether it would actually withstand defamation standards in real jurisdictions.

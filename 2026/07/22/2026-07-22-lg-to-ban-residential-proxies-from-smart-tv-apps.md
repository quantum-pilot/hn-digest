# LG to ban residential proxies from smart TV apps

- Score: 392 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=49000864) | Link: https://krebsonsecurity.com/2026/07/lg-to-ban-residential-proxies-from-smart-tv-apps/

### TL;DR
LG will ban smart TV apps that turn sets into residential proxy nodes, after research found proxy SDKs in 42% of LG webOS titles and many Samsung apps. Developers were paid to embed code letting firms like Bright Data route customer traffic through users’ home networks, often after a single opaque consent screen. LG promises tighter review, while critics note its own adware-like partnerships and weak oversight. HN discussion centers on residential proxies as abuse infrastructure and on isolating or “de‑smarting” TVs.

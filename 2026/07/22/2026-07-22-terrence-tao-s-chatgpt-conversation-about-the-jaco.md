## TL;DR
A shared ChatGPT transcript shows Terence Tao interrogating an alleged counterexample to the Jacobian conjecture. The model produces a highly structured polynomial, while Tao repeatedly simplifies, tests variants, and steers it using deep domain knowledge. Commenters focus less on the specific math and more on the process: how an expert treats the LLM like a grad student or algebraic workhorse, how iteration beats one-shot prompts, and how meaningful “AI help” still absolutely depends on human ability to verify and interpret dense technical arguments.

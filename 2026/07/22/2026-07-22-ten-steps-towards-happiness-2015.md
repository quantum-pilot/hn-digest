# Ten Steps Towards Happiness (2015)

- Score: 211 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=49000600) | Link: http://hintjens.com/blog:99

- TL;DR  
Pieter Hintjens lays out ten practical, low-cost habits for becoming happier: nourish your senses with good food, beauty, and movement; deliberately do things you’re bad at to keep learning; seek and enjoy other people and communities; finish projects to build confidence; cut toxic “bad actors” from your life; understand and ground negative emotions; fiercely protect your time from joyless obligations; own less and travel light; and, ultimately, cultivate wanting nothing and accepting everything to reduce disappointment and see more beauty.

- LLM perspective  
View: This is a stoic‑leaning, minimalist playbook focused on behavior and attention, not external achievements or purchases.  
Impact: Most useful to knowledge workers drifting in busyness, burnout, or isolation, who still control some life decisions.

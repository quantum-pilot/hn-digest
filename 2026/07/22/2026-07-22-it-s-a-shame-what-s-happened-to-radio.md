# It's a shame what's happened to radio

- Score: 212 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48999825) | Link: https://blog.jimgrey.net/2026/07/21/its-a-shame-whats-happened-to-radio-3/

### TL;DR

Former DJ Jim Grey reflects on a nine‑year radio career and why he no longer misses it. College and small‑market stations once let personalities pick music, hone craft, and build real relationships with callers. Deregulation and consolidation turned radio into a cost‑cutting, centrally programmed business: voicetracking replaces local hosts, ad‑driven formats chase narrow demographics, and Portable People Meter ratings push minimal talk and human presence. Many local stations, including all he worked for, are gone; he now mostly listens to his own music and NPR, seeing radio’s decline as partly self‑inflicted.

# StreetComplete: Fixing OpenStreetMap, one tiny quest at a time

- Score: 666 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48816883) | Link: https://streetcomplete.app/

## TL;DR
StreetComplete is a mobile app that turns improving OpenStreetMap into bite-sized ‘quests’ such as confirming sidewalks, crossings, benches, or viewpoints. HN users praise it as an intuitive, game-like way to contribute high-quality local data—often more detailed than Google Maps—while walking around. Discussion centers on OSM’s tagging complexity (especially sidewalks/crossings), how StreetComplete and similar apps lower the learning curve, and frustration that commercial platforms may benefit from OSM data without sharing improvements back.

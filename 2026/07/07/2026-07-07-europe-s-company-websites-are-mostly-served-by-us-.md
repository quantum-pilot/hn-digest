# Europe's company websites are mostly served by US vendors

- Score: 236 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48816612) | Link: https://ciphercue.com/blog/european-web-hosting-vendor-share-2026

- TL;DR  
  Analysis of European company websites suggests most rely on US-based infrastructure providers, especially for CDN, DNS, and cloud services. Commenters debate whether this is dangerous dependency or normal economic interdependence. Legal exposure to US surveillance laws despite EU datacenters worries many, while others stress mutual reliance in telecoms and manufacturing. Critics question the study’s methodology and note hidden internal APIs and regulated hosting. Founders say US tools remain far ahead, though European clouds and payment processors are slowly improving.

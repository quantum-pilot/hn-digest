# Amazon without the knockoffs

- Score: 295 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48818963) | Link: https://knockoff.shopping/

## TL;DR
A Chrome extension promises “Amazon without the knockoffs” by hiding dubious brands from search results, but it largely reuses the open AmazonBrandFilter brand blacklist. HN discussion centers on the ethics of repackaging that work under a more restrictive license, the difficulty of maintaining accurate brand lists, and whether users actually want to avoid all “unknown” brands. Commenters debate trade‑offs: cheap no‑name goods vs brand premiums, safety and liability risks, rampant Chinese factory cloning, and Amazon’s role in enabling copycat products.

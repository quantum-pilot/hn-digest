# Show HN: Davit, a Apple Containers UI

- Score: 159 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48821848) | Link: https://davit.app

### TL;DR
Davit is a small, notarized macOS GUI for Apple’s new Containers, providing a native alternative to Docker-style tooling. Early users praise its 17 MB footprint, direct use of ContainerAPIClient, and smooth first-run experience that auto-downloads the runtime. The codebase shows heavy AI assistance (Claude co-author on all commits), which people increasingly associate with polished, non-Electron apps. Main feedback: improve onboarding with a “hello world” image, clearer tutorial, and better demo than nginx:latest.

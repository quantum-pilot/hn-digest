### TL;DR
The US Department of Commerce has lifted export controls on Anthropic’s Claude Fable 5 and Mythos 5 after Anthropic agreed to tighter, government-coordinated safety and monitoring. That likely means stronger classifiers to detect “risky” use, including cybersecurity-style coding, with more false positives and some requests silently falling back to weaker models and stricter quotas. Commenters welcome restored access but worry about unpredictable, ad‑hoc AI regulation, increased surveillance-style logging, product degradation for coding/security tasks, and rising incentives to move to private or open‑weight models.

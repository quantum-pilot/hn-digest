# The state of open source AI

- Score: 353 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48947825) | Link: https://stateofopensource.ai/

### TL;DR

Mozilla’s 2026 report argues open-weight models have effectively “grown up”: they’re near parity with top closed models on coding and general tasks, while inference costs have fallen ~50× and a majority of OpenRouter tokens now hit open models, heavily dominated by Chinese ecosystems like DeepSeek and Qwen. The real gap is operational: deployment, governance, security, and standardization of the “agentic harness” that turns models into agents. HN likes the data but questions open-model economics, closed-model moats, and the report’s obviously LLM-written prose.

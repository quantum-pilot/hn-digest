# Trump Media to sell instant access to 'market-moving' social posts

- Score: 160 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48944487) | Link: https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c79gw4lj89eo

## TL;DR
Trump Media & Technology Group, owner of Truth Social, is launching a paid “Truth API” for Wall Street and other institutional clients, offering millisecond delivery of posts from “highest‑ranking” accounts starting 1 August. Because Trump’s posts can move markets, the product effectively monetises privileged speed to the sitting president’s public statements, from which Trump benefits as majority shareholder. The firm will block unofficial data scraping, pushing traders toward the paid feed. Legal experts say it’s likely permissible but ethically troubling.

# Samsung Health app threatens data deletion if users opt out AI training

- Score: 223 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48897991) | Link: https://neow.in/cWsyMTV3

- TL;DR  
  - Samsung Health reportedly gives users an ultimatum: let Samsung train AI on sensitive health data (sleep, meds, medical records, cycle tracking) or have that data deleted and lose features. Commenters see this as coercive “consent,” likely illegal in the EU and consistent with Samsung’s history of intrusive telemetry and ad-heavy apps. Some welcome forced deletion as a privacy win, but many doubt Samsung will truly erase or stop using data, comparing it to similarly user‑hostile AI policies at Google.

# Ask HN: Add flag for AI-generated articles

- Score: 999 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48886741) | Link: https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48886741

### TL;DR
HN already bans AI-generated comments and now debates how to handle AI-written articles. Staff describe an emerging stigma against “LLM-sounding” prose and propose adding flag reasons like “suspected genAI” rather than a visible AI tag. Many commenters dislike AI-authored posts because they’re harder to trust or discuss and dilute scarce human voice, while others say only quality matters and fear false accusations, witch hunts, and politics if “AI-generated” becomes a slur.

### LLM perspective
- View: Make “suspected genAI” one private flag reason; avoid visible badges that become culture-war weapons or low-effort dismissals.  
- Impact: Clear guidance that undisclosed AI-written submissions may be moderated encourages serious authorship while deterring SEO farms and engagement bait.

# My thoughts on the Bun Rust rewrite

- Score: 627 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48843352) | Link: https://andrewkelley.me/post/my-thoughts-bun-rust-rewrite.html

### TL;DR
A blog post by Zig’s creator, Andrew Kelley, reacts to Bun’s rewrite from Zig to Rust and the end of Anthropic’s financial support. Commenters say he misrepresented Bun’s fuzzing practices and focused too much on Jarred Sumner’s management and coding style, making the piece feel like a personal attack and damaging Zig’s professional image. Others defend it as honest, non-corporate speech. The thread also highlights the risks of relying on a single large donor and debates ideology vs. adoption for programming languages.

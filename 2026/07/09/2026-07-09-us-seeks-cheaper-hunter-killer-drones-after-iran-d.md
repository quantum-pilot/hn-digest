# US seeks cheaper hunter-killer drones after Iran destroys $1B worth of Reapers

- Score: 235 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48845514) | Link: https://arstechnica.com/gadgets/2026/07/us-seeks-cheaper-hunter-killer-drones-after-iran-destroys-1b-worth-of-reapers/

### TL;DR
The US is soliciting cheaper “hunter‑killer” drones after losing expensive MQ‑9 Reapers to Iranian attacks, but commenters argue the Pentagon is learning the wrong lesson. Instead of cloning a high-end, long-range, heavily sensorized Reaper at lower cost, many say modern warfare shows the value of small, disposable, rapidly iterated drones like those used in Ukraine and Iran. Discussion centers on over-gold‑plated requirements, suffocating peacetime bureaucracy, and a defense industry optimized for cost inflation over efficiency.

# EU Parliament greenlights Chat Control 1.0

- Score: 941 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48843923) | Link: https://www.patrick-breyer.de/en/eu-parliament-greenlights-chat-control-1-0-breyer-our-children-lose-out/

## TL;DR
- EU MEPs failed to muster the absolute majority needed to block renewal of “Chat Control 1.0,” reviving voluntary mass scanning of private messages by major US platforms until 2028. End‑to‑end encrypted services remain exempt, and European providers generally don’t scan, but critics see this as groundwork for a future “2.0” that could target encryption. HN discussion centers on the opaque emergency procedure, absent MEPs, corporate lobbying, and what many view as the EU’s increasingly anti‑democratic, security‑theater approach to online safety.

# GPT-5.6

- Score: 982 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48849066) | Link: https://openai.com/index/gpt-5-6/

- TL;DR  
GPT-5.6 adds better intent inference, preserves original image dimensions, and strongly rewards shorter, less-scripted prompts. It’s unusually sensitive to “be concise”–style instructions, so legacy prompts may suddenly become too terse. The Sol variant sets a new state of the art on the ARC-AGI-3 benchmark, though scores remain single‑digit. Developers highlight striking cost and token-efficiency gains versus previous frontier models and compare OpenAI’s coding stack favorably to Anthropic’s, while criticizing Anthropic’s aggressive refusals on biology and code review.

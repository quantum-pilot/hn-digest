# Swiss parliament lifts ban on new nuclear power plants

- Score: 662 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48585746) | Link: https://www.bluewin.ch/en/news/switzerland/parliament-lifts-ban-on-new-nuclear-power-plants-3257535.html

### TL;DR
Switzerland’s parliament has voted to lift the post‑Fukushima ban on new nuclear power plants via an indirect counterproposal to the “Blackout Initiative.” The move passed very narrowly in the National Council and still requires a nationwide referendum, where all left and green parties are expected to campaign hard against it. Supporters argue Switzerland’s strong hydro and growing solar still leave a winter supply gap that nuclear can reliably cover; critics say new plants are too slow, risky and expensive versus renewables, storage, and imports.

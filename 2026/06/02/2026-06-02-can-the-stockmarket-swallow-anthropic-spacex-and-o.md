### TL;DR
The article frames the imminent IPOs of SpaceX, Anthropic, and OpenAI as “giga‑IPOs” that could add up to $4T in US market cap, testing whether public markets can digest such scale without dislocation. HN commenters zoom in on index-rule changes that may force passive funds to buy at IPO prices, raising concerns about retirement savers, liquidity, and fairness. Others debate whether these sky‑high AI/space valuations reflect real productivity and infrastructure gains or simply a late‑cycle bubble.

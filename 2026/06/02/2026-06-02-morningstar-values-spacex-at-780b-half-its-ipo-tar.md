# Morningstar values SpaceX at $780B, half its IPO target

- Score: 187 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48373909) | Link: https://www.reuters.com/business/media-telecom/morningstar-values-spacex-780-billion-half-its-ipo-target-2026-06-02/

### TL;DR
Morningstar estimates SpaceX is worth $780B, roughly half its reported $1.75T IPO target and below its last private $1.53T mark. The firm is particularly skeptical about the AI narrative (xAI, Grok, “orbital data centers”) and Starlink’s technical/regulatory risks, arguing the IPO will likely be overpriced for long‑term investors, despite a probable near‑term pop from a low float and big-bank underwriters. HN commenters largely see this as emblematic of an AI/Musk-driven bubble whose valuations far outrun fundamentals.

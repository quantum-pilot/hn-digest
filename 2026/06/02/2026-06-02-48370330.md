# Please don't spam people looking for employment. It's just cruel

- Score: 880 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48370330) | Link: https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48370330

## TL;DR
People posting they’re looking for work are getting hit with increasingly aggressive, often AI-generated sales pitches, including one from an LLM “daughter” named Alya asking for $500. Commenters see this as especially cruel toward vulnerable job seekers, akin to scammy recruiter spam and fake security “bug bounty” shakedowns targeting open‑source maintainers. Others argue spam is simply the price of public contact info, but there’s broad agreement that basic etiquette—empathy, clarity, naming your company, and not exploiting distress—is being ignored.

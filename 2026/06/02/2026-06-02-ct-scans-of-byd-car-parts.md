# CT scans of BYD car parts

- Score: 169 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48375824) | Link: https://www.lumafield.com/scan-of-the-month/byd

### TL;DR
Lumafield CT‑scanned parts of a modern BYD EV (including the key fob, drivetrain and structural elements) to reveal how aggressively BYD optimizes integration, packaging and cost. The scans illustrate dense, highly automated design choices and support the argument that BYD is the first carmaker since early‑Ford to vertically integrate at this scale, reportedly making ~75% of its components. Hacker News readers focus on BYD’s supply‑chain power, subtle design details, and how CT scanning itself is being marketed.

# The founder's playbook: Building an AI-native startup

- Score: 206 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48566832) | Link: https://claude.com/blog/the-founders-playbook

## TL;DR
- Anthropic’s “Founder’s Playbook” is a short PDF that re-frames four startup stages—idea, MVP, launch, scale—around heavy use of Claude tools. It suggests AI for market research, customer discovery, and competitive mapping; generating and refactoring MVP code; measuring product‑market fit; and replacing recurring founder tasks with agents. Embedded is a matrix of when to use Claude Chat, Cowork, and Code plus brief customer vignettes. Hacker News readers mostly see it as a glossy sales deck overselling how easy AI makes founding.

# Volkswagen started blocking GrapheneOS users

- Score: 460 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48571526) | Link: https://discuss.grapheneos.org/d/35949-volkswagen-app?page=3

### TL;DR
Volkswagen’s official apps (VW, SEAT, etc.) have started enforcing Google Play Integrity checks, which block devices that aren’t “Play Protect certified,” including GrapheneOS and other custom ROMs. Even with Play Services installed, users are refused login, and VW confirms it won’t support alternative OSes, citing “security-relevant system components.” This also kills previously working, unofficial API access used by community tools and Home Assistant. Many HN commenters say they’ll now avoid VW, seeing this as lock‑in, security theater, and de facto Google dependency.

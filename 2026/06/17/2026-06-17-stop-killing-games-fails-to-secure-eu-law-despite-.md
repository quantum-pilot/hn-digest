# Stop Killing Games fails to secure EU law despite 1.3M signatures

- Score: 346 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48564696) | Link: https://www.dexerto.com/gaming/stop-killing-games-fails-to-secure-eu-law-despite-1-3m-signatures-3376431/

### TL;DR
The EU’s Stop Destroying Videogames citizens’ initiative, backed by 1.29M signatures, has been rejected on its core demand: a legal obligation for publishers to keep discontinued games playable. Citing proportionality, IP, cost, and security concerns, the Commission instead proposes a voluntary industry code for end‑of‑life game management and better transparency, arguing existing consumer law already offers some protection. The campaign anticipated this and is now focusing on amending the upcoming Digital Fairness Act via the European Parliament.

# Tidal AI Policy

- Score: 290 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48718840) | Link: https://tidal.com/ai-policy

### TL;DR
Tidal is formalizing an AI music policy: it will allow uploads but scan and label tracks judged “wholly or substantially” AI‑generated, hold them to stricter integrity standards (no impersonation, deception, or low‑quality spam), and deny them monetization. Commenters broadly like the anti-spam and anti-impersonation angle but argue about fairness to creators, fuzzy definitions of “AI‑generated,” and platform incentives. The thread branches into calls for human‑only or AI‑filterable services, and criticism of how streaming and labels game royalties.

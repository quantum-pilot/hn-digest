# Dutch gov't will only allow European company to operate DigiD platform

- Score: 252 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48413295) | Link: https://nltimes.nl/2026/06/05/dutch-govt-will-allow-european-company-operate-digid-platform

- TL;DR  
  The Dutch government will restrict future operators of DigiD, its nationwide digital ID platform, to European companies using a security-focused procurement law. This follows blocking US firm Kyndryl’s attempted takeover of current provider Solvinity, amid fears US extraterritorial laws could enable data access or service disruption. Parliament had repeatedly demanded protection of DigiD from non‑EU control. Authorities will also strengthen encryption for DigiD and MijnOverheid following a confidential national‑security review.

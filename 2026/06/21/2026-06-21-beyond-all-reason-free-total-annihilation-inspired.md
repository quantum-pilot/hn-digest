# Beyond All Reason (Free Total Annihilation Inspired RTS)

- Score: 418 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48617990) | Link: https://www.beyondallreason.info

### TL;DR
Beyond All Reason is a free, Total Annihilation–inspired RTS focused on gigantic battles with thousands of fully simulated units, ballistics, terrain deformation, and deep economic play. After ~7 years of hobbyist-style development, it’s partnering with strategy publisher Hooded Horse for a full Steam launch, with rich maps, PvE modes, and detailed guides already in place. HN commenters praise its technical achievement and TA lineage but warn about a steep learning curve and often-toxic behavior in big public PvP lobbies.

---

### Comment pulse
- Technically brilliant but 8v8 “meta” lobbies can be hostile → rigid expectations, flaming, vote-kicks; community manager urges reporting abuse and using rotating/chill-map lobbies instead.  
- Onboarding matters → advice is to start vs AI, watch guides, and join newbie/smaller games; diving straight into large PvP almost guarantees a stomp and frustration.  
- Strong TA nostalgia and ecosystem → players reminisce about original TA and recommend sibling project Zero-K, whose design essays show the depth of TA-derived RTS thinking.

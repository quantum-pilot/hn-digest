# Pokémon Go Scans Trained the Navigation Tech for Military Drones

- Score: 715 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48487029) | Link: https://dronexl.co/2026/06/09/pokemon-go-scans-niantic-vantor-military-drone-navigation/

## TL;DR
Niantic’s Pokémon Go “Pokéstop scans” yielded some 30B short videos that were licensed for broad reuse, turned into a visual positioning system (VPS) that works without GPS. That VPS now underpins a partnership between Niantic Spatial and major US defense contractor Vantor, intended for navigation of drones and ground robots in GPS‑jammed environments. Vantor says it won’t “use game data” operationally but won’t clarify whether models were originally trained on it, raising hard consent, transparency, and dual‑use ethics questions.

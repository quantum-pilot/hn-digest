# Never Give Them Your Face

- Score: 688 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48630066) | Link: https://nevergivethemyourface.com/

## TL;DR
The essay argues that “age verification” laws are actually mandates for universal identity tracking via government IDs and facial biometrics. These systems create irrevocable, breach-prone databases, chill anonymous speech, and still fail to keep determined teenagers from restricted content—while potentially helping predators and future repressive regimes. The author urges users to refuse face scans and abandon platforms that require them. Hacker News commenters share biometric-ban horror stories, question the power of individual boycotts, and discuss regulation and privacy tools like Tor as countermeasures.

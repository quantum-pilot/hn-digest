# Danish privacy activist Lars Andersen raided by police

- Score: 409 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48625823) | Link: https://twitter.com/LarsAnders1620/status/2068208864747540516#m

### TL;DR
Danish privacy activist Lars Kragh Andersen had his home raided by police, who reportedly cut power, entered in masks and plain clothes, and confiscated cameras and SIM cards. Commenters describe Andersen as both a principled critic of state surveillance and someone who crosses ethical and legal lines, e.g., placing GPS trackers on ministers’ cars and doxxing family members. The thread wrestles with whether extreme, often distasteful activism is effective or self-defeating, and how far police powers should extend in response.

# GLM 5.2 vs. Opus

- Score: 478 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48626866) | Link: https://techstackups.com/comparisons/glm-5.2-vs-opus/

### TL;DR
GLM‑5.2, an MIT‑licensed open‑weights model from Z.ai, is benchmarked and “vibe‑tested” against Claude Opus 4.8 by having both build a 3D WebGL platformer from scratch. Opus finishes in ~34 minutes vs GLM’s ~71, produces a more polished, fully working game, and uses multimodal vision to self‑check screenshots; GLM is text‑only and ships with obvious visual bugs. Benchmarks show GLM‑5.2 as arguably the strongest text‑only open model, close to but still behind top closed models, while being much cheaper but slower and token‑hungry.

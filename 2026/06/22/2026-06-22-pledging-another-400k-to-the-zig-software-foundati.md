# Pledging another $400k to the Zig software foundation

- Score: 710 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48630020) | Link: https://mitchellh.com/writing/zig-donation-2026

- TL;DR  
    - Mitchell Hashimoto is pledging another $400k to the Zig Software Foundation, bringing his family’s total support to $700k over four years. He praises Zig’s technical ambition, community governance, and strict ban on LLM-generated contributions, even though he personally uses AI heavily and disagrees with parts of that stance. For him, Zig exemplifies open source projects’ right to be “weird,” draw boundaries, and pursue quality. HN discussion centers on online intolerance for difference, Ghostty’s value, and nuanced arguments for Zig’s AI policy.

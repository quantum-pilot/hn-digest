# Raspberry Pi 5 – 16GB RAM

- Score: 307 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48481857) | Link: https://www.adafruit.com/product/6125?src=raspberrypi

### TL;DR
Adafruit is selling a 16GB variant of the Raspberry Pi 5 for $350, significantly expanding the board’s suitability for memory‑hungry workloads. It keeps the Pi 5’s 2.4GHz quad‑core Cortex‑A76 CPU, VideoCore VII GPU, dual 4Kp60 HDMI, PCIe 2.0 x1, faster USB 3, and improved MIPI camera/display bandwidth. Power now comes via 5V/5A USB‑C with PD; new cases, power supplies, and active cooling accessories are required because of the board’s redesign and higher performance.

# GeoLibre 1.0

- Score: 301 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48479852) | Link: https://geolibre.app/

### TL;DR
GeoLibre is a cloud‑native, cross‑platform GIS (desktop, web, Android) built on Tauri, React, MapLibre, DuckDB Spatial, and deck.gl. It loads a wide range of local, web, cloud‑native, 3D, and database formats; offers in‑browser Spatial SQL, vector/raster processing, conversion tools, and Python/Jupyter integration; and adds plugins, collaboration, story maps, and an auditable AI assistant. The HN crowd sees it as a lightweight alternative to QGIS/ArcGIS Online, though some hit stability limits with large (>1 GB) datasets and request modern OGC API support.

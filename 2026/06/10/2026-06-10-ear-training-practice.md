# Ear Training Practice

- Score: 328 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48447598) | Link: https://tonedear.com/

## TL;DR
Free, browser-based ear training drills focused on practical listening skills: intervals, chord and scale recognition, progressions, perfect pitch checks, functional solfege, and melodic dictation. Each exercise plays notes or chords; you answer by naming intervals, chord types, scale types, or scale degrees. The site emphasizes short, daily practice to build intuitive recognition rather than theory-first study. A parallel teacher platform adds assignment configuration, student tracking, and extra theory exercises for classroom use.

# AWS Bedrock to require sharing data with Anthropic for Mythos and future models

- Score: 423 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48473166) | Link: https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48473166

### TL;DR
AWS Bedrock users who want Anthropic’s new Mythos/Fable (and similar future) models must now allow Anthropic to log request/response data for some period, ending true zero-data-retention for those integrations. Commenters see this as a major shift: it adds a new data processor without direct contracts, complicates compliance for regulated and government customers, and normalizes retention in “AI-as-a-service.” Others argue all frontier vendors are converging on “pay with data + verification,” pushing privacy‑sensitive users toward open-weight and self-hosted models.

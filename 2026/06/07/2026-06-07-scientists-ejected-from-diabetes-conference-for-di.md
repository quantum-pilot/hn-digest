# Scientists ejected from diabetes conference for distributing journal reprints

- Score: 302 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48433410) | Link: https://arstechnica.com/science/2026/06/scientists-ejected-from-diabetes-conference-for-distributing-journal-reprints/

### TL;DR
At the American Diabetes Association’s New Orleans conference, five senior scientists—including the editor-in-chief of the ADA’s own journal—were expelled for quietly handing out reprints of a Diabetes Care editorial criticizing the Trump administration’s attacks on biomedical research and NIH funding. The ADA cited its code of conduct banning “disruptive” protesting; video suggests calm behavior. HN discussion frames it as an example of growing political censorship, fear of losing federal funding, and the chilling of scientific advocacy—while noting the resulting Streisand effect on the editorial.

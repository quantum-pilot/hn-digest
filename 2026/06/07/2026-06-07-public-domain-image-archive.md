# Public Domain Image Archive

- Score: 236 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48430539) | Link: https://pdimagearchive.org/

- TL;DR  
  Public Domain Review’s image archive collects over 11,000 out‑of‑copyright artworks, browsable by artist, century, style, and theme, and free to download and reuse. Each item includes simple rights metadata distinguishing underlying work and digital copy, plus links back to source institutions, but the site explicitly offers guidance, not legal guarantees. HN commenters welcome another high‑quality trove of open art, while debating how to do proper copyright clearance, whether Amazon KDP will accept such proof, and noting mixed UX impressions.

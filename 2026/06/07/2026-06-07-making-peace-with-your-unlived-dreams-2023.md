# Making peace with your unlived dreams (2023)

- Score: 119 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48437290) | Link: https://nik.art/making-peace-with-your-unlived-dreams/

## TL;DR
An essayist describes accepting that some ambitions—like extreme snowboarding or mastering many hobbies—won’t fit within a body with bad knees or a finite life. Instead of resentment, he leans on imagination and vicarious enjoyment, choosing a few roles (writer, partner, friend) to inhabit deeply. Hacker News readers connect this to millennial self‑improvement pressure, the anguish of uncertain dreams like homeownership or partnership, and the reality of caregiving or disability—arguing both for pruning externalized goals and actively pursuing what remains possible.

# Michigan bill would bar employers from requiring after-hours coms with workers

- Score: 229 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48707769) | Link: https://www.cbsnews.com/detroit/news/workplace-boundaries-act-employees-after-hours/

- TL;DR  
    Michigan is considering a “right to disconnect”–style bill limiting when and why employers can contact staff outside normal hours, unless that availability is explicit and compensated. Commenters describe widespread unpaid after-hours expectations, especially in teaching, sales, hospitality, and lower‑wage jobs, and frame pushback as often rooted in privilege or denial. Others warn legislation may reduce flexibility, slow work, and increase costs, arguing compensation and market choice work better than blanket rules.

# Professor denounces mass AI fraud on an exam at Brown

- Score: 172 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48708991) | Link: https://english.elpais.com/education/2026-06-28/ai-fraud-at-brown-university-academic-integrity-is-at-risk.html

- TL;DR  
  A Brown professor (a game theory researcher) reports widespread use of AI on a “take‑home, closed‑book” exam, arguing it undermines learning and grading. HN discussion largely accepts that unsupervised, high‑stakes written work is now inherently compromised by LLMs. Commenters propose in‑person or tightly proctored digital exams, oral defenses of assignments, and redesigning courses so the easiest way to get a good grade is still to learn. Others question the purpose of grades and credentials in an AI‑saturated world.

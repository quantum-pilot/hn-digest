# Google limits Meta's use of its Gemini AI models

### TL;DR
Google has reportedly capped Meta’s use of its Gemini AI models, but HN commenters argue it’s likely about hard compute/TPU capacity and pricing, not a policy-style “ban.” Large cloud customers routinely hit hidden resource ceilings, and Gemini already shows frequent rate limits and downtime. Discussion centers on whether Google is prioritizing selling TPU capacity to third parties like Anthropic/OpenAI over its own Gemini, why Meta would lean on Google instead of OpenAI/Anthropic, and how access to top models will tighten over time.

# MacBook Neo is so popular that Apple doubled production

- Score: 310 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48386238) | Link: https://www.macrumors.com/2026/06/03/macbook-neo-production-doubled-says-kuo/

### TL;DR
Apple’s $599 MacBook Neo is selling so well that 2026 production targets reportedly doubled from 5M to 10M units. Tim Cook says demand exceeded expectations and drove record first‑time Mac buyers, while IDC data shows it bucking a declining PC market. Dell’s response, a cheaper XPS 13, publicly concedes strong demand for “premium at accessible prices.” HN discussion centers on MacBook Neo redefining entry-level value via build quality, vertical integration, and low support burden, especially versus Windows and Linux laptops.

# KDE at 30

- Score: 225 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48357355) | Link: https://kde.org/anniversaries/30/

### TL;DR
- KDE is celebrating 30 years with global community events, fundraising for a more predictable budget, and a “30 for 30” environmental challenge encouraging users to do planet‑helping actions. The anniversary page traces milestones from Qt’s creation and KDE 1 through Plasma 6, Steam Deck adoption, and the new KDE Linux distro, and highlights hidden impacts like KHTML powering modern browsers and KDE desktops at NASA. HN’s consensus: some lost ambition, but still the premier power‑user Linux desktop.

---

### Comment pulse
- Early KDE felt experimental with Konqueror, KParts, KIO slaves; many projects faded or stalled. — counterpoint: fans say Dolphin, KIO, KStars still showcase integration.  
- Many users now consider Plasma the best general‑purpose Linux desktop: powerful defaults, huge customisation, and standout apps like Okular, Kate, Konsole, Spectacle, KolourPaint, KDE Connect.  
- Recent converts report Plasma is fast and light even on old hardware, offering a familiar Windows‑like UX, though some criticise Akonadi, style, and Wayland‑first changes.

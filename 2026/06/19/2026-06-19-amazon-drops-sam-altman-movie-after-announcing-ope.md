# Amazon drops Sam Altman movie after announcing OpenAI partnership

- Score: 161 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48602639) | Link: https://www.the-independent.com/arts-entertainment/films/news/sam-altman-biopic-amazon-openai-deal-b2999321.html

### TL;DR
Amazon’s MGM arm has dropped distribution of *Artificial*, Luca Guadagnino’s nearly finished biopic about OpenAI CEO Sam Altman’s 2023 ouster and rehiring, starring Andrew Garfield. The decision comes months after Amazon deepened a multibillion‑dollar cloud and investment partnership with OpenAI and amid Bezos–Altman personal ties. Officially, Amazon says another studio is better suited and is helping shop the film, which reportedly tests well but portrays Altman and Elon Musk unsympathetically. HN debates clickbait framing, conflict-of-interest risks, and tech-platform control over culture.

### TL;DR
Mistral OCR 4 is a new commercial OCR model, but most discussion focuses on context. Commenters compare it to postal services that already decipher messy, non-standard addresses by mixing basic automation with human knowledge. Others note Mistral’s San Francisco–style marketing despite its European roots, reflecting that serious AI vendors must court US customers and funding. Technically, people are wary: past Mistral OCR benchmarks seemed cherry-picked, graphs use truncated axes, and independent comparisons against models like Baidu’s Unlimited-OCR are still missing.

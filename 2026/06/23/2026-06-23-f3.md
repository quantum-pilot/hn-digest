# F3

- Score: 599 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48647799) | Link: https://github.com/future-file-format/f3

### TL;DR
F3 is a proposed columnar storage format that tries to “future‑proof” data files by embedding WebAssembly decoders and richer metadata, aiming to fix Parquet’s limits around random access and hardware awareness. Files are self-describing and theoretically readable anywhere, even without native libraries. Hacker News reactions are mixed: some see genuine innovation in compatibility and modern workloads (analytics + ML needing both scans and random access), while others argue Parquet’s ubiquity, security risks, and unclear real‑world gains make F3 a hard sell.

# AI's Affordability Crisis

- Score: 241 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48646276) | Link: https://blog.dshr.org/2026/06/ais-affordability-crisis.html

### TL;DR
HN discussion around an “AI affordability crisis” focuses less on GPU physics and more on business reality: once vendors shifted from flat subscriptions to metered, token-based pricing, enterprises saw ballooning bills and slammed on the brakes. Many commenters think the real crunch isn’t that models can’t get cheaper, but that ROI is unclear: faster code and content don’t obviously translate into profit. With opaque margins, heavy VC subsidies, and an implicit “AGI-or-bust” bet, many expect a painful correction.

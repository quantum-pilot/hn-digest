# How much of Thermo Fisher's antibody data has been manipulated?

- Score: 396 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48442075) | Link: https://reeserichardson.blog/2026/05/28/how-much-of-thermo-fishers-antibody-data-has-been-manipulated/

### TL;DR
Investigators have cataloged hundreds of apparently manipulated Western blot images used as “advanced verification” for Thermo Fisher antibodies, including duplicated bands, clear brush-like edits, and a repeated background template reused across many products. Because antibodies already fail frequently and are expensive, falsified catalog data can waste substantial money and time while worsening irreproducibility in biomedicine. Thermo Fisher denies fabrication, saying images were merely “optimized for presentation.” Hacker News discussion leans toward seeing this as systemic fraud in a market scientists already distrust.

# Apple reveals new AI architecture built around Google Gemini models

- Score: 308 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48450142) | Link: https://www.macrumors.com/2026/06/08/apple-reveals-new-ai-architecture/

- TL;DR  
Apple is overhauling Apple Intelligence with new Apple Foundation Models co‑developed with Google using Gemini tech, running both on‑device and in Apple’s Private Cloud Compute. A central “orchestrator” aligns AI behavior with the active app and task for multimodal features like image generation and richer Siri. Apple emphasizes locked‑down servers and verifiable privacy guarantees. Discussion focuses on whether Google’s involvement and PCC truly protect data, EU regulation blocking launch, and how deeply Gemini now underpins Apple’s AI stack.

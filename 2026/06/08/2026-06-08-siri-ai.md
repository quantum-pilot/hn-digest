# Siri AI

- Score: 354 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48449084) | Link: https://www.apple.com/apple-intelligence/

### TL;DR
Apple is rebranding Siri as “Siri AI,” a systemwide assistant powered by Apple Intelligence: on‑device models plus Private Cloud Compute for heavier tasks. It can understand personal context (photos, mail, notes), act across apps, see what’s on your screen/camera, generate and edit text, and do Photoshop‑style photo edits. Features are tightly integrated into iOS/macOS/iPadOS, but mostly English‑only at launch and limited to newer devices. HN discussion centers on EU DMA blocking the rollout, privacy vs competition, and Siri’s long history of underdelivering.

---

### Comment pulse
- Apple vs EU over DMA: critics say Apple’s “safety” concerns mask a desire to block rival AIs; defenders argue regulators could force worse privacy trade‑offs — counterpoint: strong permission prompts plus existing review might suffice.  
- Many see “Siri AI” as finally delivering what Siri was promised to do for a decade; frustration over Siri’s unreliability and tight hardware/language gating persists.  
- Some are excited by “AI as UI”: a Star‑Trek‑style assistant over a device‑wide knowledge graph that can orchestrate apps, though practicality and developer pushback are open questions.

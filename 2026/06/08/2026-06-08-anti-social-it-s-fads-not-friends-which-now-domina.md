# Anti-social: It's fads, not friends, which now dominate social media feeds

- Score: 526 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48444228) | Link: https://www.bbc.com/worklife/article/20260520-how-social-media-ceased-to-be-social

### TL;DR
Social networks are pivoting from “posts by friends” to TikTok-style, AI-curated entertainment feeds optimized for attention and ad revenue. Data from France, the UK, and the US show a clear drop in active posting and a rise in passive scrolling, especially among Gen Z. Personal sharing is migrating to WhatsApp and private groups, while Instagram, TikTok, etc. become TV-like discovery platforms. This reshapes advertising, burdens small businesses to be constant content creators, and raises fears of manipulation and burnout—echoed in HN’s own self-reflection.

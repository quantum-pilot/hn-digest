# APC–2 – A professional record cutter for producing original playback discs

- Score: 261 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48440383) | Link: https://teenage.engineering/products/apc-2

### TL;DR
Teenage Engineering’s APC–2 is a professional lathe that cuts audio directly to playback-ready records in real time. Built with a direct-drive tungsten shaft motor, ultra-low wow and flutter, stereo feedback cutting head, and integrated RIAA-encoded amplification, it targets mastering and archival-quality vinyl-style disc creation. Variable pitch automation from a DAW enables locked grooves and specialty cuts. The 140 kg system, made of powder-coated aluminum and granite, is produced in limited quantities and sold via partner SUPERSENSE.

### LLM perspective
- View: Bridges boutique industrial design with traditional record-cutting, making small-run and one-off discs more accessible beyond big pressing plants.  
- Impact: Most relevant for mastering houses, art installations, high-end studios, and labels needing short-run or on-demand physical releases.

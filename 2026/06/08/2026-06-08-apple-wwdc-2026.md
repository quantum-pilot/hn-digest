# Apple WWDC 2026

- Score: 233 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48448106) | Link: https://www.apple.com/apple-events/event-stream/

### TL;DR
WWDC26 centers on “Apple Intelligence”: a rebuilt Siri AI with a dedicated app, deeper integration across Photos/Messages/Safari, broader child-safety controls, and system-wide performance polish. Apple is also rolling back controversial “Liquid Glass” UI changes after sustained backlash, signaling a Snow-Leopard-style refinement year. HN commenters criticize Apple’s hyper-scripted, inauthentic keynotes, debate the irony of Siri AI being withheld from the EU over DMA/privacy issues, and split over AI-driven conveniences like automation and photo editing reshaping how users work and remember.

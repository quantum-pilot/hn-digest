# Massachusetts bans sale of precise location data in new privacy rights bill

- Score: 236 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48448012) | Link: https://techcrunch.com/2026/06/08/massachusetts-votes-to-pass-new-privacy-rights-bill-that-bans-sale-of-precise-location-data/

### TL;DR
Massachusetts is set to enact a Consumer Data Privacy Act granting residents rights to access and delete their data and banning the sale of precise location data, biometrics, and other sensitive categories without explicit consent. It targets companies handling data on over 100,000 consumers and applies to residents and visitors, effectively blocking location-data markets in the state. HN commenters welcome the move but highlight loopholes around “sale,” weak anonymization standards, cars as major trackers, and the need for data minimization and collection limits.

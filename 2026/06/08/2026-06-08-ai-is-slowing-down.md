# AI is slowing down

- Score: 352 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48446893) | Link: https://www.wheresyoured.at/ai-is-slowing-down/

### TL;DR
Zitron argues the current AI boom is financially impossible to sustain. Data‑center plans (≈190 GW) and GPU/compute contracts for OpenAI, Anthropic, Oracle, and hyperscalers imply trillions in capex and a need for roughly $2–3T in annual AI revenue by 2030. In reality, almost all meaningful demand comes from two loss‑making labs, heavily subsidized pricing, and enterprises that can’t even see or justify their token bills and are now capping use. Just as the industry must grow exponentially, usage growth and ROI enthusiasm are visibly slowing.

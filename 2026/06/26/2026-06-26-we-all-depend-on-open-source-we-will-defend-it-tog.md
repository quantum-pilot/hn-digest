# We all depend on open source. We will defend it together

- Score: 434 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48682737) | Link: https://akrites.org/letter/

### TL;DR
Linux Foundation is launching a new coalition (including AWS, Google, Microsoft, OpenAI, banks, etc.) to “defend” critical open source by improving security, likely with AI and shared resources. Commenters are wary of a corporate-heavy, centralized guardian of the commons, fearing security as a pretext for control and PR. Others argue large companies already drive much open source, so coordination is unavoidable. Across the thread, people stress that defending open source means funding maintainers, hardware, and infrastructure—not just slogans.

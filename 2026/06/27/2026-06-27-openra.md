# OpenRA

- Score: 551 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48697560) | Link: https://www.openra.net/

- **TL;DR**
  - OpenRA is an open-source reimplementation of classic Command & Conquer–era RTS games, letting players run and rebalance Red Alert and related titles on modern systems. Commenters praise its improved unit balance, new tactics, and active multiplayer community, while others complain about AI behavior and project maintainers being hard to contribute to. EA’s decision to open-source older C&C code and tolerate OpenRA is widely lauded, and people link it to a broader wave of open engine remakes.

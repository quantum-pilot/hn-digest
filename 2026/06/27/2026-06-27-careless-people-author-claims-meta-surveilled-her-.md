### TL;DR
Former Meta public policy executive and “Careless People” author Wynn-Williams sues Meta, alleging a year of surveillance and retaliation to silence her after her whistleblowing book about Facebook’s political manipulation and harmful ad practices. The complaint details forced severance under financial duress, banning her from promoting a bestseller, and targeting vulnerable teen girls with beauty ads. HN focuses on the filing as primary source, Meta’s pattern toward whistleblowers, and the growing Streisand-effect publicity.

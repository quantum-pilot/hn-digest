# IP Crawl: Living atlas of open webcams discovered on the public internet

- Score: 203 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48700834) | Link: https://ipcrawl.com/

### TL;DR
IP Crawl is a site that auto-discovers unsecured IP cameras and presents them as a navigable world map, exposing homes, stores, even industrial controls. Commenters dissect why so many feeds are public: UPnP, lazy installers, poor defaults, and sometimes ISPs without NAT. The thread wrestles with ethics—whether merely viewing is akin to peeking through unlocked doors—and fault lines between user responsibility, vendor UX, and regulators in preventing accidental mass surveillance.

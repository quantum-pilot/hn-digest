# U.S. to dismantle system tracking Atlantic currents that are at risk of collapse

- Score: 609 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48392232) | Link: https://e360.yale.edu/digest/trump-ooi-amoc

### TL;DR
The Trump administration plans to remove more than 900 Ocean Observatories Initiative instruments in the Pacific and Atlantic, including key moorings between Greenland and Iceland that underpin real‑time monitoring of the Atlantic Meridional Overturning Circulation (AMOC). The system, built for 25 years but used for only a decade, has been central to understanding tipping‑point risks for global climate and marine ecosystems. Scientists warn this will blind us to rapid ocean changes, while Democrats vow to resist and commentators decry politicized sabotage of basic science.

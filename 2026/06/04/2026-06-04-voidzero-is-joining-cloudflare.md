# VoidZero Is Joining Cloudflare

- Score: 549 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48398055) | Link: https://blog.cloudflare.com/voidzero-joins-cloudflare/

### TL;DR
Cloudflare is acquiring VoidZero, the team behind Vite, Vitest, Rolldown, Oxc, and Vite+. All projects stay MIT-licensed, vendor-agnostic, and led by the same maintainers, with Cloudflare adding engineers and a $1M ecosystem fund. Vite will gain full‑stack, provider‑neutral primitives while Cloudflare’s own CLI and app tooling standardize on Vite, especially for AI/agent-driven workflows. Hacker News welcomes more funding for Evan You’s tooling but is wary of VC-driven acqui‑hires and worried about long‑term independence and Cloudflare’s UX.

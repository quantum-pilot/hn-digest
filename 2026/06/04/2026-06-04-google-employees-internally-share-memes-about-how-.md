### TL;DR
Google touts that 75% of its new code is AI‑generated, but 404 Media reports internal memes from Google’s Memegen platform mocking the same tools as unreliable and counterproductive. The piece uses these memes to illustrate a gap between executive AI hype and engineers’ day‑to‑day experience. After publication, Google’s PR asked 404 to alter its statement, removing a line saying it’s “critical” to keep humans in the loop, which many readers see as the most revealing detail.

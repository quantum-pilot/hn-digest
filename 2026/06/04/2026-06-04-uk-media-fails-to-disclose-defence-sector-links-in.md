# UK media fails to disclose defence sector links in nearly 60% of cases

- Score: 359 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48395938) | Link: https://aoav.org.uk/2026/military-experts-or-arms-industry-insiders-uk-media-fails-to-disclose-defence-sector-links-in-nearly-60-of-cases/

**TL;DR**

AOAV analysed UK coverage from 2015–2026 and found that 19 of 33 senior ex‑military figures with defence‑sector jobs were often presented as neutral “military experts” without disclosing their commercial roles. Many simultaneously argued for higher defence spending, interventions, or rearmament. The report blames newsrooms’ lax disclosure standards, not deliberate concealment, and urges routine conflict‑of‑interest labelling. HN commenters debate which outlets are worst, whether all experts’ ties should be spelled out, and connect this pattern to “manufacturing consent” dynamics.

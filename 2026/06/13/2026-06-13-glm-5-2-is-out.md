# GLM 5.2 Is Out

- Score: 753 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48518684) | Link: https://twitter.com/jietang/status/2065784751345287314

- TL;DR  
  GLM 5.2 is a new open-weight large model from Chinese lab Zhipu, pitched as “fully open” with a 1M-token context window and strong coding/agent use cases. Early users say it feels roughly 6–12 months behind top frontier models like Claude Opus but already very capable and cheap to run. Discussion centers on its permissive licensing, the contrast with recent US government pressure on Anthropic’s Fable/Mythos models, and whether Chinese labs will stay open once they reach parity.

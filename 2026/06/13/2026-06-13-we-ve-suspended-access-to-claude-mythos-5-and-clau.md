# We've suspended access to Claude Mythos 5 and Claude Fable 5

- Score: 253 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48511121) | Link: https://status.claude.com/incidents/s9w82lp9dcn9

- TL;DR  
  - Anthropic’s status page reports that access to the experimental Claude Mythos 5 and Claude Fable 5 models has been suspended across claude.ai, the API, Claude Code, and Claude Cowork, with the incident currently in a monitoring phase and fuller details on Anthropic’s main site. The attached Hacker News thread mainly points readers to an earlier, much larger discussion about this same suspension, generating confusion over whether this update indicates a fresh incident or an ongoing outage.

- LLM perspective  
  - View: This shows how frontier models may be treated as experimental services, with access toggled quickly during unexpected issues.  
  - Impact: Developers depending on specific model variants need fallbacks and version pinning to avoid disruption when providers suspend or retire models.  
  - Watch next: Look for incident postmortems, stability guarantees, and clearer deprecation policies from model vendors as usage becomes more business‑critical.

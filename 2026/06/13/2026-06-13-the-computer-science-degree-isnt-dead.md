# The computer science degree isn’t dead

- Score: 259 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48470152) | Link: https://spectrum.ieee.org/computer-science-degree-isnt-dead

### TL;DR
The article argues CS degrees still pay off: unemployment for recent grads is higher than some humanities fields, but underemployment is much lower and CS/CE top early‑career salaries. The real problem is a broken entry‑level pipeline full of “ghost jobs.” Recommended tactics: lean hard on referrals, target startups with matching risk, create real-world projects (not toys), and learn practical AI engineering (RAG, vector DBs, production wiring). HN discussion adds that degrees are powerful social/credibility signals, while junior hiring and degree quality are both under strain.

# I indexed 669 GB of my GoPro videos using my M1 Max computer and local ML models

- Score: 382 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48528029) | Link: https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48528029

## TL;DR
An individual indexed 669 GB of GoPro footage on an M1 Max laptop using only local open-source ML models. They downscaled video to 720p, sampled at 1 fps, ran Whisper for transcription and vision-language models for scene understanding/embeddings, finishing ~57k frames in ~68 hours. HN commenters compare similar DIY projects, note that RTX GPUs or cheap cloud instances could greatly accelerate processing, contrast this local-first approach with DaVinci/Adobe cloud AI features, and debate convenience versus privacy of AI-generated photo/video memories.

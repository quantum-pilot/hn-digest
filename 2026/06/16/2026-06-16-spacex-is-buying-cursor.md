- Score: 286 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48554215) | Link: https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cvgd5g7d7gyo

## TL;DR
SpaceX, newly listed on Nasdaq after a record-breaking IPO, has surged over 50% to a ~$2.78T valuation, briefly overtaking Amazon despite generating far less revenue and currently losing money. Investor optimism centers on rockets, Starlink, and a vision of AI data centers in space. As part of this AI push, SpaceX is buying Anysphere’s Cursor, a leading AI coding assistant, for $60B in stock to pair with its Colossus supercomputer.

# Apple increases MacBook and iPad prices by 20%

- Score: 257 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48674096) | Link: https://www.ft.com/content/0f067265-2baf-4b6e-8fb2-ed56daef6f3c

### TL;DR
Apple has raised MacBook and iPad prices by about 20%, likely reflecting higher component costs, especially RAM, and long-term supply contracts. Commenters expect these elevated prices to persist for roughly eighteen months, since Apple rarely cuts prices once set. The hike draws renewed criticism of non-upgradeable RAM in Apple notebooks, with some arguing regulators should require user-swappable memory like LPCAMM2 to protect consumers during component price spikes and extend device lifespans.

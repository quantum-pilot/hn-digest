# Oxide computer 3D rack guided tour

- Score: 271 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48631450) | Link: https://explorer.oxide.computer/

### TL;DR
Guided 3D tour showcases Oxide’s fully integrated rack-scale computer: shared power and networking, pluggable compute sleds, and vertically designed firmware and software. Commenters compare it to mainframes and blade systems, and debate why legacy vendors never produced something similar. Many engineers idolize Oxide as a “modern Sun,” eager yet intimidated to apply, while others criticize its homework-heavy hiring process. Discussion touches on memory‑supply risks, defensibility of physical products, and firmware reliability.

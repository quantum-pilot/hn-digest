# Windows 10 quietly gets one more year of support and updates

- Score: 220 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48675369) | Link: https://www.neowin.net/news/windows-10-quietly-gets-one-more-year-of-support-and-updates/

### TL;DR
Microsoft is quietly extending Windows 10’s life by about a year, with comments suggesting some users can get free extended security updates if they sign in with a Microsoft account. This buys time for those stuck on older hardware or opposed to Windows 11’s TPM, Secure Boot, and mandatory Microsoft account push. However, confusion persists around whether to move to Windows 10 IoT Enterprise LTSC, stay on regular Windows 10, or abandon Windows entirely—many are instead switching to Linux, especially for gaming.

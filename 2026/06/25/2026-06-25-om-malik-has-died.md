## TL;DR
Om Malik, founder of GigaOM and longtime tech journalist, has died at 60. Commenters remember him as a rare voice who combined blunt, deeply informed reporting on telecom and startups with genuine kindness and generosity. He championed “writing like a human,” shunning jargon, and focused on people’s stories over hype. Many recall him as a boss, mentor, or supporter who quietly boosted careers and embodied an older, more community‑minded Silicon Valley.

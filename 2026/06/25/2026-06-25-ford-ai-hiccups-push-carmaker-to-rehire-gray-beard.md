# Ford AI hiccups push carmaker to rehire ‘gray beard’ inspectors

- Score: 566 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48674446) | Link: https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-06-25/ford-has-been-rehiring-quality-inspectors-after-ai-fell-short

### TL;DR
Ford leaned on AI systems to replace or reduce veteran human vehicle inspectors. After quality problems, the company is rehiring “gray beard” experts to restore reliability. HN commenters see this as another management fad like offshoring: short‑term savings, long‑term damage to expertise. Others argue AI and LLMs work best as power tools for senior engineers, not full replacements. Several practitioners report current models still need dense documentation, human oversight, and can’t yet capture tacit, intuitive industrial knowledge.

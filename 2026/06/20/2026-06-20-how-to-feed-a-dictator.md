# How to feed a dictator

- Score: 154 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48605301) | Link: https://www.theguardian.com/film/2026/jun/09/how-to-feed-a-dictator-film

- TL;DR  
  Guardian review of documentary How to Feed a Dictator, adapted from Witold Szabłowski’s book, following five personal chefs to Pol Pot, Idi Amin, Saddam Hussein, Kim Jong-il and Pinochet. Through stories of lavish food, fear, loyalty and denial, it shows how people trade morality for comfort and become essential cogs in murderous regimes. HN discussion debates the use of Hannah Arendt’s “banality of evil,” the value of adapting the book, and a brief, contentious Trump comparison.

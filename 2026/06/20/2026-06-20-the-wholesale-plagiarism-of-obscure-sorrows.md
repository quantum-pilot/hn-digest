# The Wholesale Plagiarism of Obscure Sorrows

- Score: 302 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48611411) | Link: https://waxy.org/2026/06/the-wholesale-plagiarism-of-obscure-sorrows/

### TL;DR
A San Francisco agency, Qontour, built a slick “fan” site for The Dictionary of Obscure Sorrows that copies the entire book’s text without permission, swaps the original art for DALL·E images, and adds a GPT‑4-based “sorrow generator” plus affiliate links. The bootleg outranks the author’s and publisher’s sites in Google and is treated by ChatGPT/Gemini as official, blurring authorship and reputationally tying Koenig to AI he never endorsed. The piece frames this as part of a larger, consent-free AI/SEO plagiarism economy.

# Show HN: StartupWiki – A Free Alternative to Crunchbase

- Score: 142 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48610224) | Link: https://startupwiki.tech/

### TL;DR
StartupWiki is a free, AI-powered alternative to Crunchbase aiming to be a community-maintained directory of global startups. It currently lists a limited set of mostly well-known tech companies and plans to expand coverage via automated pipelines and scraping sources like YC portfolios. HN commenters question its practical usefulness until coverage and data reliability improve, especially around “verified” badges. The creator is actively iterating: batching new imports, fixing source-linking bugs, and adding an “agent ledger” to show how AI-derived data was produced.

---

### Comment pulse
- Coverage is too sparse → many common startups are missing; scraping YC and investor portfolios would quickly bootstrap a useful dataset.
- Verification UX is weak → “verified” badges need transparent provenance and timestamps to be meaningful — counterpoint: even Crunchbase relies on noisy, self-reported data.
- Reliability concerns → AI + community editing may yield lots of inaccuracies; suggested workaround is an on-demand API that runs the AI pipeline per-query.

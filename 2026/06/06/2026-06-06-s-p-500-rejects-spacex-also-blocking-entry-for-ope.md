# S&P 500 rejects SpaceX, also blocking entry for OpenAI and Anthropic

- Score: 1343 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48421442) | Link: https://arstechnica.com/tech-policy/2026/06/sp-500-blocks-fast-spacex-entry-wont-waive-rule-for-unprofitable-ai-firms/

## TL;DR
S&P Dow Jones Indices rejected rule changes that would have fast‑tracked SpaceX into the S&P 500 despite its huge proposed valuation, tiny public float, and lack of profitability. The decision also closes the door on similar preferential treatment for OpenAI and Anthropic after their IPOs, limiting immediate access to tens of billions from passive index funds. Other index providers (Nasdaq, FTSE Russell) are loosening rules, but S&P is prioritizing its “seasoning,” profitability, and free‑float requirements to protect index integrity and retirement investors.

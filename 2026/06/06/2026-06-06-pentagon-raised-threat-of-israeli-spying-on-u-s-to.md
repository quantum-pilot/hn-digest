# Pentagon raised threat of Israeli spying on U.S. to highest level, sources say

- Score: 370 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48427523) | Link: https://www.nbcnews.com/politics/national-security/pentagon-raised-threat-israeli-spying-us-highest-level-sources-say-rcna348565

## TL;DR
The Defense Intelligence Agency has quietly raised Israel’s counterintelligence threat rating to “critical,” its highest level, reflecting concerns that Israeli spying on U.S. officials has recently become unusually aggressive, especially around Trump administration deliberations on the Iran war and Lebanon. Israel and the White House both deny the report. U.S. officials say Israel has long run very assertive intelligence operations, even against allies, but formalizing this threat level may signal shifting politics as Trump and Netanyahu clash over war strategy and domestic elections loom.

# Ask HN: Why is the HN crowd so anti-AI?

- Score: 369 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48420827) | Link: https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48420827

## TL;DR
HN isn’t uniformly anti‑AI; it’s split, and even individual users are conflicted. Many engineers like what LLMs unlock but see real limits: brittle code, security risks, technical debt, and chaotic “agentic” workflows in teams. A vocal group also fears AI will deskill programming and erase the joy and identity tied to hand‑coding, turning developers into prompt jockeys. Others worry about centralization of knowledge, US corporate control, and AI as a political/economic power lever, yet still use the tools pragmatically.

# Krea 2: SOTA open-weights 12B image model

- Score: 324 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48646659) | Link: https://www.krea.ai/blog/krea-2-technical-report

## TL;DR
Krea 2 is a 12B-parameter, open-weights text-to-image model released in Turbo (distilled, fast) and RAW (hackable, fine-tunable) variants, with an unusually detailed technical report covering data, architecture, RL, and infra. Benchmarks place it near Nano Banana and Ideogram 4 while remaining locally hostable, already GGUF-quantized and permissively licensed for individuals/SMBs. HN discussion centers on transparency, practical speed/quality tradeoffs, the shift toward image-to-image and agentic workflows, and open questions about safety filters for adult/violent content.

# Slate EV truck starts at $24,950

- Score: 237 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48659451) | Link: https://www.slate.auto/en

## TL;DR
Slate is a compact, modular EV truck starting at $24,950, with configurations (e.g., “fastback”/hatch-like) that realistically land closer to $35k. Its big differentiators are Lego-like body modularity, physical controls instead of a mandatory center touchscreen, and a single base color intended for DIY wraps. HN likes the concept, price point, and no-infotainment stance, but worries about DIY wraps, creaks/leaks from modularity, modest ~200-mile range, and competition from established EVs and efficient gas hatchbacks.

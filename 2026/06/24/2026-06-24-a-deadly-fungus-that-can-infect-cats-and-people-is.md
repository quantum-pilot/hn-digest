# A deadly fungus that can infect cats and people is spreading

- Score: 182 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48658186) | Link: https://www.sciencenews.org/article/deadly-fungus-cats-people-spreading

- TL;DR  
An emerging fungal disease, sporotrichosis (likely S. brasiliensis), is spreading from cats to humans, especially in Brazil, via scratches, bites and contaminated materials. It mostly causes skin lesions but can be severe or fatal in immunocompromised people and persists in the environment, raising concern about urban and cross‑border spread. Commenters debate how climate change may select for fungi that tolerate mammalian body temperatures, the scarcity and resistance of antifungal treatments, and practical, safer ways to medicate cats to limit transmission.

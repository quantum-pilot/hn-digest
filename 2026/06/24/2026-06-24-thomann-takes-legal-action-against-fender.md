# Thomann takes legal action against Fender

- Score: 172 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48664384) | Link: https://www.thomann.de/blog/en/inside/thomann-takes-legal-action-against-fender/

### TL;DR
Thomann is pushing back legally against Fender’s recent attempts in Europe to control classic guitar body shapes (especially Strat-style designs) via copyright and “applied art” claims. Commenters argue this reflects Fender’s long-running, private‑equity‑steered shift from instrument maker to lifestyle/IP brand, threatened by boutique builders and popular competitors like PRS and Harley Benton. The thread digs into EU vs US design protection, whether 70‑year‑old shapes are now generic, and how Thomann’s move is as much defense as marketing.

# Ask HN: Has anyone replaced Claude/GPT with a local model for daily coding?

- Score: 604 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48542100) | Link: https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48542100

### TL;DR
Several HN users report successfully using local models (mostly Qwen 3.6 27B/35B and Gemma 4) as daily coding copilots via Pi/llama.cpp-style harnesses. They get good speed on high-VRAM GPUs or 128 GB Macs, enjoy privacy, offline use, and no token costs, and compare quality to a capable junior dev or last year’s frontier models. But outputs remain less reliable than Claude/Sonnet, need tighter prompting and supervision, and long-context issues persist—fine for personal work, weaker for pro-grade velocity.

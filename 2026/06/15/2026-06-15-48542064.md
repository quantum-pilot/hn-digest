# Hetzner increased dedicated server prices 3-4x

- Score: 271 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48542064) | Link: https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48542064

### TL;DR
Hetzner, a popular low-cost European hosting provider, has reportedly raised prices on certain dedicated servers by roughly 3–4x, which is a shock for users who relied on its historically stable, bargain pricing for long-term infrastructure. This Hacker News thread itself contains almost no discussion; it mainly redirects readers to an earlier, more active thread on the same topic. The core concern is the fragility of business models that assume static hosting costs over many years.

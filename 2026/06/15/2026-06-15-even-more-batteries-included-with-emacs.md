# Even more batteries included with Emacs

- Score: 342 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48535886) | Link: https://karthinks.com/software/even-more-batteries-included-with-emacs/

### TL;DR
The article is a third installment in a series surfacing obscure but powerful *built‑in* Emacs features aimed at intermediate/advanced users. It focuses on tools you can learn in under five minutes: dictionary lookups on hover, wildcard `find-file`/Dired, buffer-wide URL pickers, lightweight diffing (`compare-windows`, `dired-compare-directories`, smarter `vc-diff` that also uses backups), change-highlighting tied to saves, richer help via the apropos family, better duplication and macro workflows, smarter word/navigation behavior, image zoom/rotate, visibility/search toggles, auto-refilling text, synchronized scrolling, buffer/frame protection, and frame “undelete.”

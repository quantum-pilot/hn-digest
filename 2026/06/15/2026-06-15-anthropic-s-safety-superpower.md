# Anthropic's Safety Superpower

- Score: 200 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48539078) | Link: https://stratechery.com/2026/anthropics-safety-superpower/

### TL;DR
- Ben Thompson argues Anthropic’s safety-first identity is both sincere and a mechanism to centralize power. Fable/Mythos look like a new model generation, but the US export clampdown, 30‑day data retention, and attempts to secretly hobble LLM-development use show Anthropic trying to control user touchpoints, data, and downstream AI development. HN discussion questions how far a single lab can dominate when models commoditize, compute and regulation are real bottlenecks, and any private “guardian of humanity” risks becoming an unaccountable gatekeeper.

# Albania Is Not for Sale: Kushner's $4B Resort Triggers'Flamingo Revolution'

- Score: 434 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48461012) | Link: https://www.yacnews.com/albania-is-not-for-sale-kushners-4-billion-resort-triggers-flamingo-revolution-asset-freeze-and-an-eu-warning/

### TL;DR
Albania approved a Jared Kushner–backed, Gulf-funded luxury resort spanning Sazan Island and the Vjosa-Narta wetlands, reclassifying protected land and fast‑tracking it under “strategic investment” laws. Bulldozers began clearing dunes and forests before environmental studies or public consultation, destroying habitat and blocking beach access. Violent clashes with private security sparked nationwide “Flamingo Revolution” protests, an asset freeze on the main land buyer, and an EU warning that Albania’s accession talks may be derailed—testing its new anti‑corruption institutions and the limits of Trump-linked influence.

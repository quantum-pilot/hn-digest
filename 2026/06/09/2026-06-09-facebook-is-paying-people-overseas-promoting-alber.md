# Facebook is paying people overseas promoting Alberta separatism

- Score: 296 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48457181) | Link: https://www.cbc.ca/news/canada/facebook-overseas-alberta-separtism-9.7223966

### TL;DR
CBC found at least 14 Facebook accounts run from Indonesia, Pakistan, India, Sri Lanka and the U.S. posing as Albertans to post pro‑separatist content. They stole photos and text from real activists, used AI‑generated memes, and cashed in through Meta’s engagement‑based monetization, sometimes bragging about earnings. Experts say this isn’t classic state-backed interference so much as low‑wage workers discovering that inflaming Canadian outrage is a profitable niche—yet it still distorts democratic debate and exploits both users and genuine activists.

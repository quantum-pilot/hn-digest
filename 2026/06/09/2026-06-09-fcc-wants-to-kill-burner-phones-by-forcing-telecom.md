# FCC wants to kill burner phones by forcing telecoms to get all customers' IDs

- Score: 416 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48462308) | Link: https://www.404media.co/fcc-wants-to-kill-burner-phones-by-forcing-telecoms-to-get-all-customers-ids/

### TL;DR
The FCC has proposed “enhanced know-your-customer” rules requiring U.S. telecoms to collect and store government ID numbers and physical addresses for essentially all new and renewing phone customers. Framed as a way to fight robocalls, scams, and other abuses, it would effectively end anonymous “burner” phones and align the U.S. with countries that mandate SIM registration. Critics warn it will endanger domestic abuse survivors, activists, and low‑income users, expand tracking, and create massive new breach targets at already-leaky carriers.

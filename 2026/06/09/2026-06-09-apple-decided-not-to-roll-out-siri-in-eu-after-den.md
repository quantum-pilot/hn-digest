# Apple decided not to roll out Siri in EU after denied request for exemption

- Score: 343 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48463024) | Link: https://www.reuters.com/business/apple-failed-make-its-ai-tool-comply-eu-regulations-eu-commission-says-2026-06-09/

### TL;DR
EU regulators rejected Apple’s request for an 18‑month exemption from the Digital Markets Act interoperability rules for its upgraded Siri AI. Apple responded by not launching the feature in the EU and publicly blamed the DMA for blocking a privacy‑ and security‑preserving rollout; the Commission says nothing in the law prevents release and that Apple simply refused to meet required privacy and interoperability standards. HN commenters split between praising strict enforcement, criticizing EU overreach that limits features, and seeing Apple’s move as PR leverage.

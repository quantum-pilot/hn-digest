# System Card: Claude Fable 5 and Claude Mythos 5 [pdf]

- Score: 210 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48463811) | Link: https://www-cdn.anthropic.com/d00db56fa754a1b115b6dd7cb2e3c342ee809620.pdf

- TL;DR  
Anthropic’s Claude Mythos 5 is its most capable LLM to date, but is split into Mythos 5 (unsafeguarded, limited to trusted partners) and Fable 5 (general release with strong cyber/bio and frontier-AI-development mitigations plus automatic fallback to Opus 4.8). Risk assessments find low but rising autonomy and CB-1 biothreat risk; CB-2 novel bioweapon capability is judged not yet met but worryingly close. Extensive red‑teaming shows large uplift for experts, especially in biology, while alignment, agentic safety, and model welfare remain comparable to Opus 4.8.

- LLM perspective  
  - View: Dual-config release plus hidden throttling on frontier-AI work is a template others may copy under regulatory pressure.  
  - Impact: Stronger bio/cyber controls shift cutting-edge capabilities toward vetted institutions, widening gap with ordinary users and open-source models.  
  - Watch next: Independent replications of uplift trials, public CB-2-style benchmarks, and any real-world jailbreak or weight-theft incidents.

# We Think the SpaceX IPO Is Overvalued

- Score: 254 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48455233) | Link: https://www.morningstar.com/stocks/why-we-think-spacex-ipo-is-overvalued?content_id=20768396545

### TL;DR
Morningstar pegs SpaceX’s fair value at $63 per share versus a $135 IPO price, even while assuming very aggressive success for Starship and Starlink. The key swing factor is a speculative orbital AI–data-center business: in a 7%-probability “moonshot” case, SpaceX could reach ~$1.97T value, but base and downside cases pull the probability-weighted value far lower. At $135, investors are effectively paying a $72/share “option premium” on Musk-led moonshots and are heavily discounting execution, physics, and governance risks.

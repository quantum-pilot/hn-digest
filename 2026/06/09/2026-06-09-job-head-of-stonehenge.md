# Job: Head of Stonehenge

- Score: 224 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48455928) | Link: https://www.english-heritage.org.uk/about/our-people/careers-with-us/job-search/default-job-page/?jobRef=16449

## TL;DR
English Heritage is hiring a “Head of Stonehenge,” a senior on‑site leader for the UK’s most‑visited ancient monument. The role combines strategic operations, commercial management, and stakeholder coordination, including overseeing solstice events and safety for visitors, staff, and the monument itself. Pay starts around £64k with public‑sector‑style benefits and free access to sites. HN readers joke about druidic rituals, debate whether the salary matches the prestige, and praise the surrounding landscape’s relatively low‑key, well‑protected character.

# Claude Sonnet 5

- Score: 814 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48736605) | Link: https://www.anthropic.com/news/claude-sonnet-5

## TL;DR
Claude Sonnet 5 is Anthropic’s new mid-tier, more “agentic” model, intended to autonomously plan, browse and use tools at lower cost than flagship Opus. HN commenters, testing benchmarks and real coding tasks, mostly find it an incremental upgrade over Sonnet 4.6 but rarely cost- or quality-competitive with Opus 4.8, and often similar to GLM‑5.2. Many struggle to see when to pick it, beyond subscription limits or long-context workloads, and worry agentic tuning worsens simple, assisted-development use.

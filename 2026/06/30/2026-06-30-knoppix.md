# Knoppix

- Score: 240 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48732056) | Link: https://www.knopper.net/knoppix/index-en.html

## TL;DR
HN commenters reminisce about Knoppix, the classic Linux LiveCD that booted from a disc without touching the hard drive. For many teens on shared family or school PCs, it was their first safe way to explore Linux, programming, and system recovery without breaking Windows installs. Stories highlight how Knoppix’s hardware auto-detection, bundled tools, and educational games made Linux accessible, nudging some into Linux/DevOps careers and remaining a handy troubleshooting toolkit even today.

# European digital ID wallets rely on safety services of Google and Apple

- Score: 675 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48730729) | Link: https://waag.org/en/article/european-digital-id-wallets-are-gift-google-and-apple/

### TL;DR
EU digital ID wallets, as currently implemented, often depend on Google/Apple safety services and hardware attestation, effectively locking citizens into US-controlled mobile platforms and app stores. Commenters argue this undermines digital autonomy, custom ROMs, and EU “digital sovereignty,” and may force people into exploitative platform contracts just to access public services. Others note the legal framework does allow non-phone options (smart cards, hardware tokens), but fear real-world deployments and regulations will entrench dominant platforms and sideline alternatives.

# Tell HN: Installing Cursor on iOS irreversibly changes your privacy settings

- Score: 199 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48737226) | Link: https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48737226

- TL;DR  
Installing the Cursor iOS app switches users into a new “privacy mode” that stores additional state in Cursor’s cloud, apparently without clear up-front consent and difficult to undo. A Cursor employee on HN says this mode is required for cloud agents, that legacy behavior remains on desktop, and promises clearer in-app messaging plus manual reversion help via support. Commenters are frustrated by opaque privacy changes, LLM-only support, and a mobile app whose capabilities may not justify the privacy trade-off.

# County with 37 Data Centers Asks Schools to 'Conserve Electricity'

- Score: 387 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48734699) | Link: https://www.404media.co/henrico-virginia-datacenter-energy-cost-email/

### TL;DR
A Virginia county packed with 37 data centers (about 2 GW of demand, heading toward 3 GW) reportedly asked schools to cut electricity use, spotlighting how fast hyperscale computing is stressing regional grids. Hacker News discussion argues over causes and blame: state clean-energy mandates, two decades of flat US generation, and rapidly growing data center loads. The core fight is about who pays for new capacity—residents or tech companies—and how to align data centers with a renewable-heavy grid.

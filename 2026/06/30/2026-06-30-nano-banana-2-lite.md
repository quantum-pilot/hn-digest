# Nano Banana 2 Lite

- Score: 286 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48735444) | Link: https://deepmind.google/models/gemini-image/flash-lite/

## TL;DR
Google’s Nano Banana 2 Lite is a distilled, faster, cheaper variant of its NB2 image model, marketed as a drop-in replacement for NB1. Early users see ~5-second generations, better text rendering than NB1, but weaker nuance and consistency than full NB2, with pricing not as low as expected. HN discussion focuses on deceptive AI-staged real-estate photos, friction from Google’s fragmented account and payment systems, and how it compares to top-quality but slower options like ChatGPT Image 2.

# "Don't You Just Upload It to ChatGPT?"

- Score: 470 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48507278) | Link: https://correresmidestino.com/dont-you-just-upload-it-to-chatgpt/

### TL;DR
A freelance translator describes being asked why she doesn’t “just upload” work to ChatGPT, then carefully explains why professional translation is more than word swapping: it requires cultural adaptation, tone, terminology research, and consistency. She does use AI as a tool—for style‑guide checks, extracting terminology, and occasional suggestions—but compares it to a toddler that must be supervised, not a magic button. She rejects the idea that AI justifies paying experts less. HN readers echo this, debating expertise, translation artistry, and AI’s rapid progress.

### Comment pulse

# Google AI Studio is now sponsoring Tailwind CSS

- Score: 378 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=46545077) | Link: https://twitter.com/OfficialLoganK/status/2009339263251566902

### TL;DR
Google AI Studio has become a paid sponsor of Tailwind CSS, soon joined by Vercel, after Tailwind publicly disclosed financial troubles despite already receiving about $1.1M/year in sponsorships. HN commenters note that common tiers ($2.5k–5k/month) suggest Google’s support, while positive, likely doesn’t fully resolve Tailwind’s issues. Discussion centers on how a “finished” CSS framework can still be money-strapped, how AI is eroding Tailwind’s paid component business, and why AI vendors now have strategic reasons to keep Tailwind healthy.

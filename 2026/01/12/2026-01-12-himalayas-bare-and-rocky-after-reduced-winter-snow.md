# Himalayas bare and rocky after reduced winter snowfall, scientists warn

- Score: 160 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=46583464) | Link: https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/clyndv7zd20o

## TL;DR
- Multiple datasets show a sharp decline in Himalayan winter snowfall and “snow persistence” since 1980, with recent winters 20–25% below normal. Warmer temperatures turn snow to rain and melt what does fall more quickly, eroding glaciers and destabilizing slopes. Rivers fed by snowmelt supply about a quarter of runoff to major Asian basins, underpinning water, food, and hydropower for nearly 2 billion people. HN commenters link this to future famines, mass migration, political instability, and cultural loss.

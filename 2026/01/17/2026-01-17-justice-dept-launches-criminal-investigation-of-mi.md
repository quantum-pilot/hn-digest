# Justice Dept. launches criminal investigation of Minnesota governor

- Score: 107 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=46654721) | Link: https://www.washingtonpost.com/national-security/2026/01/16/trump-minnesota-walz-frey-criminal-investigation/

### TL;DR
The Justice Department has opened a criminal investigation into Minnesota Gov. Tim Walz and Minneapolis Mayor Jacob Frey, planning subpoenas over claims they impeded federal law enforcement in the state, apparently linked to intensified ICE raids. A key theory under review is whether their public criticism of the federal surge could itself constitute criminal interference. Hacker News discussion centers on First Amendment limits, election-year timing, and whether local leaders are right to resist tactics that many residents experience as creating pervasive fear.

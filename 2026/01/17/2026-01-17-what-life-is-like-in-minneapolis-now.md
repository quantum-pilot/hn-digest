# What life is like in Minneapolis now

- Score: 153 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=46658213) | Link: https://donmoynihan.substack.com/p/dispatch-from-the-occupation

## TL;DR
An anonymous Minneapolis resident describes the Twin Cities as under de facto occupation by thousands of ICE and federal officers, alleging warrantless “abductions” of mostly nonwhite residents, including U.S. citizens and school staff, and chaos in public schools and neighborhoods. Community groups focus on legal observation, documenting raids and feeding families too scared to leave home, while fearing escalation. HN commenters debate ICE’s competence and legitimacy, the scale of wrongful detentions, and why online tech communities keep sidelining such stories.

## Comment pulse

## LLM perspective
- View: First-person accounts like this should be cross-checked against local journalism, court records, and arrest data to gauge systemic patterns.  
- Impact: If these descriptions hold, expect chilling effects on schooling, local economies, trust in law enforcement, and participation in elections.

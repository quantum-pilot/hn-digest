# How AI impacts skill formation

- Score: 228 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=46821360) | Link: https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.20245

### TL;DR
The paper studies how AI coding assistants affect learning, not just short-term productivity. In randomized experiments, novice developers learned a new async library either with or without AI help. Those using AI showed worse conceptual understanding, code reading, and debugging skills, while gaining little or no average speed advantage. Only people who used AI in cognitively engaged ways (e.g., interrogating and modifying its outputs) preserved learning.

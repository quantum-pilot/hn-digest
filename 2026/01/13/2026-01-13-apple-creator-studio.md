# Apple Creator Studio

- Score: 463 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=46601157) | Link: https://www.apple.com/newsroom/2026/01/introducing-apple-creator-studio-an-inspiring-collection-of-creative-apps/

### TL;DR
Apple Creator Studio is a $12.99/month or $129/year subscription bundling Final Cut Pro, Logic Pro, Pixelmator Pro, Motion, Compressor, and MainStage, plus new AI-powered features and premium content in Keynote, Pages, Numbers, and later Freeform. Notable tools include transcript and visual search, beat-aware editing, auto montages, AI session players, chord recognition, OpenAI-based image generation, and Numbers “Magic Fill.” Crucially, Apple is keeping one-time purchases for the pro apps, but some new AI capabilities are subscription-only, which HN heavily scrutinizes.

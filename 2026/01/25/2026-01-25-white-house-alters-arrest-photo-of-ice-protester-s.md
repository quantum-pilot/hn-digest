# White House alters arrest photo of ICE protester, says "the memes will continue"

- Score: 250 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=46755734) | Link: https://arstechnica.com/tech-policy/2026/01/white-house-posts-altered-arrest-photo-to-make-it-appear-ice-critic-was-sobbing/

### TL;DR
- The Trump White House used AI to alter an arrest photo of civil-rights attorney Nekima Levy Armstrong, making her appear tearful and labeling her a “far-left agitator” who led “church riots.” The original image, posted by DHS Secretary Kristi Noem, showed her calm. NAACP and her lawyer call it defamatory political propaganda that could prejudice jurors and support claims of vindictive prosecution. HN discussion focuses on rapid normalization of state deepfakes, weak legal remedies, and democracy in a post-truth media environment.

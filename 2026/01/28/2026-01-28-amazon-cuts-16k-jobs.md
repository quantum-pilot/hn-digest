# Amazon cuts 16k jobs

- Score: 464 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=46796745) | Link: https://www.reuters.com/legal/litigation/amazon-cuts-16000-jobs-globally-broader-restructuring-2026-01-28/

- TL;DR  
  Amazon is cutting 16,000 more corporate jobs, bringing layoffs to 30,000 since October—almost 10% of its white‑collar staff—while closing its Fresh and Go physical stores and discontinuing its Amazon One palm‑payment system. Leadership cites efficiency, AI‑driven automation, and unwinding pandemic over‑hiring, and leaves open the possibility of further cuts. HN discussion focuses on workers effectively automating themselves out of roles, experienced high earners becoming targets in “optimization,” renewed offshoring, and how little protection existing social and labor frameworks provide.

# F-16 Falcon Strike

- Score: 149 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=46687588) | Link: https://webchrono.pl/F16FalconStrike/index.html

## TL;DR
- A new, fully featured F-16 combat flight simulator has been released for stock 8-bit Atari XL/XE machines with only 64KB RAM. You fly as a Polish Air Force pilot defending EU and Polish borders from a fictional joint Belarusian–Russian offensive in a 15-mission “Królewiec Campaign,” plus a dynamic WARFARE mode and procedural mission generator. It’s positioned as a love letter to 80s/90s sims and explicitly built without any AI tools. HN discussion centers on retro-sim nostalgia and Atari hardware details.

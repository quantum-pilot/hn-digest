# SmartOS

- Score: 162 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=46706947) | Link: https://docs.smartos.org/

### TL;DR
SmartOS is an illumos-based Type 1 hypervisor and “live OS” that boots from PXE/ISO/USB into RAM, leaving all disks for ZFS-backed VMs and zones. It unifies OS-level containers and hardware VMs (KVM/bhyve) under a common zones model, managed via JSON-driven tools (`vmadm`, `imgadm`), SMF services, and DTrace-style observability. HN commenters admire its design and tooling but note ecosystem and driver gaps versus Linux, which push many toward Proxmox, Incus, FreeBSD, or Kubernetes-on-Linux for new deployments.

### Comment pulse

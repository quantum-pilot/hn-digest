# NASA announces unprecedented return of sick ISS astronaut and crew

- Score: 86 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=46565695) | Link: https://www.livescience.com/space/space-exploration/nasa-cancels-spacewalk-and-considers-early-crew-return-from-iss-due-to-medical-issues

- TL;DR  
  - NASA is cutting short ISS Crew‑11’s six‑month mission after an unnamed astronaut developed a medical issue, cancelling a planned spacewalk and triggering an unusually early crew rotation. The patient is stable, but NASA is prioritizing rapid return while juggling station staffing until Crew‑12 can launch. Hacker News commenters speculate cautiously about possible conditions, discuss how crews handle health risks in orbit, and debate whether sparse official details reflect privacy, prudence, or potential undisclosed operational concerns.

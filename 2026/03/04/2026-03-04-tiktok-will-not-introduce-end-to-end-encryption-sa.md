# TikTok will not introduce end-to-end encryption, saying it makes users less safe

- Score: 399 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47241817) | Link: https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cly2m5e5ke4o

### TL;DR
TikTok says it will not add end-to-end encryption (E2EE) to direct messages, arguing it needs access to DMs to protect users—especially children—and cooperate with law enforcement. This puts it at odds with competitors like WhatsApp and iMessage that have made E2EE the default, and draws praise from UK child-safety groups but concern from privacy advocates. Hacker News discussion splits between “at least they’re honest about no privacy,” worries about network lock‑in, and skepticism that child protection justifies weakened privacy.

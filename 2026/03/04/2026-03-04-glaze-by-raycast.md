# Glaze by Raycast

- Score: 185 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47247033) | Link: https://www.glazeapp.com/

### TL;DR
Glaze is a new Raycast product that lets you build Mac desktop apps by chatting with an AI: local‑first, OS‑integrated (files, keyboard shortcuts, menu bar, background processes), and publishable to a store or private team catalog. It targets non‑coders and power users who want internal tools or personal utilities without web backends. HN reactions range from skepticism about AI-built apps beyond “happy path” demos, to optimism from Raycast fans and interest in a desktop‑focused, privacy‑preserving alternative to browser‑only builders.  

---

### Comment pulse
- AI app builders won’t scale past demos → complex apps need many costly iterations. — counterpoint: founder claims they already run real internal tools and even a synth built this way.

- Safety concern: laypeople generating opaque native code → no review, audit trail, or trust model; riskier than typical package ecosystems.

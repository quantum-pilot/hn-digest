# Bets on US-Iran ceasefire show signs of insider knowledge, say experts

- Score: 156 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47494629) | Link: https://www.theguardian.com/us-news/2026/mar/23/bets-us-iran-ceasefire-show-signs-of-insider-knowledge-say-experts-polymarket

### TL;DR
A Guardian piece flags suspicious Polymarket bets on a US–Iran ceasefire: eight new wallets suddenly wagered ~$70k, potentially yielding ~$820k if a deal is announced by 31 March, echoing an earlier wallet that profited from correctly timed bets on US strikes. Analysts say the pattern looks like insider knowledge amid an administration already accused of war profiteering. HN commenters debate how plausible high‑level corruption is for such modest sums, and whether prediction markets inherently attract or even depend on insiders.

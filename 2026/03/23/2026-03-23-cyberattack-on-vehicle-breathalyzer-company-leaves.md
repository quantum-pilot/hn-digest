# Cyberattack on vehicle breathalyzer company leaves drivers stranded in the US

- Score: 146 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47489058) | Link: https://techcrunch.com/2026/03/20/cyberattack-on-vehicle-breathalyzer-company-leaves-drivers-stranded-across-the-us/

### TL;DR
Intoxalock, a large U.S. ignition‑interlock (in‑car breathalyzer) provider, was hit by a cyberattack that took down its back‑end calibration systems. These court‑mandated devices refuse to start cars when calibration is overdue, so many drivers in multiple states have been unable to use their vehicles, while service shops wait for Intoxalock to restore systems. The company has not disclosed attack type or recovery timeline, underscoring how legally required safety controls can hinge on a single, brittle private vendor.

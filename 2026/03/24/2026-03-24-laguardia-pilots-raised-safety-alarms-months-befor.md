# LaGuardia pilots raised safety alarms months before deadly runway crash

- Score: 325 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47503965) | Link: https://www.theguardian.com/us-news/2026/mar/24/laguardia-airplane-pilots-safety-concerns-crash

### TL;DR

NASA’s anonymous safety-reporting system logged multiple pilot warnings about LaGuardia: near-collisions on intersecting runways, disabled runway-status lights, and controllers “pushing the line” amid intense traffic. The recent crash occurred when an Air Canada regional jet landing in haze struck a fire truck that had been cleared across the runway, killing both pilots and injuring 41. The piece situates the accident in a broader pattern of chronic U.S. controller and TSA understaffing, budget cuts, and shutdown-driven strain, which HN commenters frame as systemic, not individual, failure.

### Comment pulse

# Apple Business

- Score: 485 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47504112) | Link: https://www.apple.com/newsroom/2026/03/introducing-apple-business-a-new-all-in-one-platform-for-businesses-of-all-sizes/

### TL;DR
Apple is rolling its disparate business tools into Apple Business, a free, global all‑in‑one platform: built‑in MDM with “Blueprints” and Managed Apple Accounts; integrated email, calendar, and directory with custom domains; brand/location management across Maps, Wallet, Mail, and Tap to Pay; and upcoming self‑serve local ads in Apple Maps. Paid upsells are iCloud storage and AppleCare+. HN reaction: features feel like table stakes in 2026, while Apple’s buggy domain‑lock/MDM history makes many admins wary of handing it their whole email domain.

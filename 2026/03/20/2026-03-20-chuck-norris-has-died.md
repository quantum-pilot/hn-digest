### TL;DR
Chuck Norris, martial-arts champion turned film and TV action star, has died at 86 in Hawaii, surrounded by family. Variety highlights his formidable real-world fighting credentials, his breakout with Bruce Lee, his Cannon Films-era hits, and his long run as Walker, Texas Ranger. Later, internet “Chuck Norris facts” resurrected him as an ironic folk hero, even as he shifted toward Christian, patriotic books and conservative politics—something Hacker News commenters contrast with their nostalgic, joke-filled memories of him.

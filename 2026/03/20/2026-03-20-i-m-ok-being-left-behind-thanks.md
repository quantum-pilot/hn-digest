# I'm OK being left behind, thanks

- Score: 789 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47454341) | Link: https://shkspr.mobi/blog/2026/03/im-ok-being-left-behind-thanks/

### TL;DR
The author rejects “you’ll be left behind” as a sales tactic for hype technologies like crypto, VR, and now AI. If something truly matters, you can adopt it later, once it’s stable, useful, and clearly not a dead end—just as with Git or the web. Early adoption rarely brings more than bragging rights; for every HTML there’s a Flash. With 16k new humans born each hour, the idea they’re all doomed by missing today’s fad is absurd. Waiting is a valid, rational strategy.

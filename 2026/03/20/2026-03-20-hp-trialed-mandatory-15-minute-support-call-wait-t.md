# HP trialed mandatory 15-minute support call wait times (2025)

- Score: 303 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47454164) | Link: https://arstechnica.com/gadgets/2025/02/misguided-hp-customer-support-approach-included-forced-15-minute-call-wait-times/

### TL;DR
HP quietly forced a minimum 15‑minute “high call volume” delay on support lines in five European countries, regardless of actual load, to push customers toward web self‑help and likely trim staffing costs. Internal staff reportedly hated it; after public exposure and “feedback,” HP reversed course and promised to prioritize fast access to humans. Hacker News framed it as another data point in HP’s fall from engineering icon to mistrusted printer brand and in the wider collapse of US-style customer support.

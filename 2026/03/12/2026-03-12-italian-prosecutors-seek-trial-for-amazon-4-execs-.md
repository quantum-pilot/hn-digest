# Italian prosecutors seek trial for Amazon, 4 execs in alleged $1.4B tax evasion

- Score: 231 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47352297) | Link: https://www.reuters.com/world/italian-prosecutors-seek-trial-amazon-four-execs-over-alleged-14-bln-tax-evasion-2026-03-12/

- TL;DR  
Italian prosecutors want Amazon’s EU arm and four managers tried for allegedly enabling €1.2B of VAT evasion in Italy (2019–2021) by obscuring tens of thousands of mostly Chinese third‑party sellers on Amazon.it. This breaks with Italy’s usual practice of ending criminal cases after a tax settlement; Amazon already paid €527M but denies wrongdoing. HN commenters debate fairness for compliant small sellers, whether rich executives will face real consequences, and if this signals tougher EU enforcement on platform-mediated tax abuse.

# Innocent woman jailed after being misidentified using AI facial recognition

- Score: 293 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47356968) | Link: https://www.grandforksherald.com/news/north-dakota/ai-error-jails-innocent-grandmother-for-months-in-north-dakota-fraud-case

## TL;DR
Tennessee grandmother Angela Lipps spent nearly six months jailed in Tennessee and North Dakota after Fargo police used AI facial recognition to misidentify her as a bank fraud suspect. No one from Fargo PD interviewed her before extradition; she was held without bail, then quickly cleared once a lawyer pulled basic bank and location records showing she was 1,200 miles away. Charges were dropped, but she lost her home, car, and dog. HN discussion centers on AI overtrust, systemic negligence, and lack of accountability or recourse.

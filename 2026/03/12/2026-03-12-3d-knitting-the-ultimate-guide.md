# 3D-Knitting: The Ultimate Guide

- Score: 209 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47347945) | Link: https://www.oliver-charles.com/pages/3d-knitting

- TL;DR  
  Article explains 3D‑knitting (WHOLEGARMENT) machines that knit entire seamless garments directly from 3D digital patterns. Compared with cut‑and‑sew or flatbed knitting, they use ~99% of yarn, cut labor and energy, and yield lighter, more durable, more comfortable sweaters. On‑demand production enables “inventory‑less” factories like a small Brooklyn plant making 150 garments/day with six people. The piece is also a pitch for Oliver Charles’ premium knitwear, while HN commenters debate sustainability claims, overconsumption, business model, and customization reality.

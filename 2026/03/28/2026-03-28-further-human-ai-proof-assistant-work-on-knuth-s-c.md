# Further human + AI + proof assistant work on Knuth's "Claude Cycles" problem

- Score: 131 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47557166) | Link: https://twitter.com/BoWang87/status/2037648937453232504

### TL;DR
Donald Knuth’s recently posed “Claude Cycles” problem is being attacked by a mixed team of humans, large language models, and formal proof assistants. The AI systems help generate conjectures, automate low-level lemmas, and explore many branches, while humans choose representations, guide search, and interpret structure. Hacker News debate centers on whether this style of collaboration foreshadows AI doing deep, original mathematics, or whether human intuition about problem framing will remain indispensable for the foreseeable future.

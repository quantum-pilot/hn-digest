# Online age-verification tools for child safety are surveilling adults

- Score: 513 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47322635) | Link: https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/08/social-media-child-safety-internet-ai-surveillance.html

## TL;DR
US “protect the kids” laws are driving widespread online age-verification, forcing millions of adults through AI-driven ID and face checks to access everything from porn to social media. Third‑party vendors often hold sensitive identity data for years, creating attractive targets for hackers and government requests and eroding anonymous browsing. Privacy advocates warn this ties real‑world identity to online behavior. A federal court has already blocked Virginia’s law on First Amendment grounds, but industry expects persistent, reusable age credentials to become standard.

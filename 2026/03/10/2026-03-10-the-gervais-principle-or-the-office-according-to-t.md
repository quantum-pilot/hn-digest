# The Gervais Principle, or the Office According to “The Office” (2009)

- Score: 284 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47286657) | Link: https://www.ribbonfarm.com/2009/10/07/the-gervais-principle-or-the-office-according-to-the-office/

### TL;DR
Venkatesh Rao uses *The Office* to propose the “Gervais Principle”: companies are inherently pathological and stabilize into three castes. “Sociopaths” (strategic, ruthless) actually run things, “Losers” trade freedom for a paycheck and rationally coast, and the “Clueless” overperform, believe the corporate mythos, and get promoted into middle management as expendable shock absorbers. Promotion isn’t about merit but about who is safest to exploit. Organizations thus become “psychic prisons” sustained by shared delusion, with *The Office* serving as an ethnography of that system.

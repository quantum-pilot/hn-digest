# Slovenian officials blame Israeli firm Black Cube for trying to manipulate vote

- Score: 520 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47519519) | Link: https://www.wsj.com/world/europe/spies-lies-and-fake-investors-in-disguise-how-plotters-tried-to-flip-a-european-election-1f42b39a

### TL;DR
Slovenia says operatives from Black Cube, a secretive Israeli private-intel firm, flew in posing as foreign investors to entrap government figures from the ruling Freedom Movement, which backs Ukraine and Palestinians. They allegedly pushed targets toward corrupt-sounding talk, filmed selectively, then leaked edited videos before Sunday’s vote to damage the party. Hacker News discussion broadens this to a pattern of Israeli-linked interference in European elections, the ethics of entrapment versus exposing corruption, and the rising visibility of such operations.

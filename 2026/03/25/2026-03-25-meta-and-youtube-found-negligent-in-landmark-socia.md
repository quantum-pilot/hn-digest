# Meta and YouTube found negligent in landmark social media addiction case

- Score: 395 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47520505) | Link: https://www.nytimes.com/2026/03/25/technology/social-media-trial-verdict.html

### TL;DR
A Los Angeles jury held Meta and YouTube negligent for designing Instagram and YouTube with addictive features like infinite scroll, awarding a 20‑year‑old plaintiff $6M for anxiety and depression. The bellwether verdict treats social media design as a harmful product, sidestepping Section 230 and echoing Big Tobacco litigation. HN commenters debate how meaningfully “addictive” social media is, whether juries should decide complex tech liability, and if this pressure will reshape or simply entrench today’s engagement‑driven platforms.

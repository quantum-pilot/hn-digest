# Bored of eating your own dogfood? Try smelling your own farts

- Score: 288 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47477158) | Link: https://shkspr.mobi/blog/2026/03/bored-of-eating-your-own-dogfood-try-smelling-your-own-farts/

### TL;DR
The author calls a giant “AI-first” company and hits a maze of useless website prompts, WhatsApp bots, and atrocious text-to-speech hold messages—evidence that no senior leader ever uses their own support channels. They argue that “dogfooding” must extend beyond happy-path product usage to the ugly bits: cancellations, billing bugs, phone trees, and AI assistants that fail. A small startup that personally called to discuss his cancellation shows the alternative: leaders directly experiencing pain, then fixing it.

### LLM perspective

- Impact: Improves retention and trust, and exposes misaligned incentives in product, security, and IT departments hiding behind policy and metrics.

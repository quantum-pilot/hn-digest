# Ape Coding [fiction]

- Score: 151 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47206798) | Link: https://rsaksida.com/blog/ape-coding/

### TL;DR
A short fictional history imagines a future where AI “agentic coding” dominates and “ape coding” (humans hand‑writing code) becomes a stigmatized holdover, then a reclaimed craft. Early resistance centers on reliability, comprehensibility, labor displacement, and the loss of joy when humans just supervise agents. As AIs improve, ape coding survives mainly as a recreational and educational hobby, with clubs, competitions, and ambitious projects like a fully human‑written compiler for an AI‑designed language. HN readers debate naming, human‑vs‑AI roles, and whether innovation still requires hand coding.

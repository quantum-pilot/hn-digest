# Flightradar24 for Ships

- Score: 172 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47205637) | Link: https://atlas.flexport.com/

### TL;DR
A new “Flightradar24 for Ships” tool shows live positions of vessels, seemingly focused on container ships, with a globe-style interface. HN users note its coverage is much sparser than established AIS-based services and appears regionally biased, making the Americas look oddly quiet. The thread quickly becomes a comparison of marine-tracking tools, complaints about MarineTraffic’s ads/paywall, and nostalgia for earlier, cheaper tanker-tracking services that later pivoted to enterprise-only clients.

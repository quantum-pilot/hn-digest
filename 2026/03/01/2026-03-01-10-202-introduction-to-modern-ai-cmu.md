# 10-202: Introduction to Modern AI (CMU)

- Score: 218 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47204559) | Link: https://modernaicourse.org

### TL;DR
- CMU’s 10‑202 “Introduction to Modern AI” is a hands‑on course focused on how today’s LLM‑based systems work, from supervised learning and transformers through fine‑tuning, RL, and safety. Students progressively implement a minimal chatbot and LLM from scratch via autograded Python/PyTorch homeworks. A free online version with a two‑week delay includes videos and assignments. Hacker News discussion centers on the course’s permissive-but-warned AI‑assistant policy, enthusiasm for the practical design, and debate over equating “modern AI” with LLMs.

---

### Comment pulse

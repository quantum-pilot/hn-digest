# Claude becomes number one app on the U.S. App Store

- Score: 209 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47202032) | Link: https://apps.apple.com/us/iphone/charts

- TL;DR  
  Claude by Anthropic has become the #1 free iPhone app in the U.S., edging out ChatGPT (#2), Google Gemini (#3) and major social/shopping apps. Hacker News discussion credits Claude’s surge to a perceived quality lead of Opus 4.6 over GPT‑5.2, especially on complex, tool-using tasks, plus Anthropic’s more principled safety stance and effective “keep thinking” marketing. A parallel thread dissects Dick’s Sporting Goods’ high ranking, driven by step-based rewards and aggressive promotion.

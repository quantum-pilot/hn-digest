# How to talk to anyone, and why you should

- Score: 333 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47142183) | Link: https://www.theguardian.com/lifeandstyle/2026/feb/24/stranger-secret-how-to-talk-to-anyone-why-you-should

### TL;DR
The author argues that many people now fear everyday conversation more than “public speaking,” retreating behind phones, headphones, and post-pandemic distance. We’re losing the skill and habit of low-stakes small talk with strangers, despite evidence it reliably feels better and more connecting than we expect. She distinguishes authentic, fleeting “small, humanising acts” from performative social-media encounters, and suggests reading cues, keeping stakes low, and accepting refusals. HN readers add both heartwarming examples and caution about safety and power dynamics.

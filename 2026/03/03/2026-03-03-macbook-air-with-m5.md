# MacBook Air with M5

- Score: 348 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47232502) | Link: https://www.apple.com/newsroom/2026/03/apple-introduces-the-new-macbook-air-with-m5/

### TL;DR
- Apple’s new MacBook Air with M5 keeps the fanless 13/15-inch design but adds a faster 10‑core CPU/GPU, much higher AI throughput, Wi‑Fi 7/Bluetooth 6, and a base config of 16GB RAM and 512GB SSD (up to 4TB, 2× faster). Battery life remains up to 18 hours and it now supports two external displays. HN broadly calls it the best all‑round consumer laptop near $1k, while debating macOS lock‑in, poor repairability, Linux options, weight, and port placement.

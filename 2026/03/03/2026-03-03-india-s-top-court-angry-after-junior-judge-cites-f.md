# India's top court angry after junior judge cites fake AI-generated orders

- Score: 334 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47231261) | Link: https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c178zzw780xo

### TL;DR
A junior civil judge in Andhra Pradesh used an AI tool to find precedent in a property dispute and cited four non‑existent “judgments.” The state high court accepted this as a good‑faith mistake and upheld her decision, arguing the legal reasoning was still sound. India’s Supreme Court reacted sharply: it stayed the order, labeled the use of fake AI citations “misconduct,” and framed the case as an institutional threat to adjudicatory integrity, amid global concern over AI hallucinations in legal processes.

# GPT‑5.3 Instant

- Score: 258 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47236169) | Link: https://openai.com/index/gpt-5-3-instant/

## TL;DR
GPT‑5.3 Instant is OpenAI’s new default fast ChatGPT model, aiming to improve everyday usability: fewer unnecessary refusals and safety sermons, better web‑augmented answers, smoother and less “cringe” tone, modestly lower hallucination rates (≈20–27% in internal evals), and stronger creative writing. It ships now in ChatGPT and the API as `gpt-5.3-chat-latest`, with 5.2 Instant deprecated in three months. HN users welcome faster, cheaper “non‑thinking” models but criticize the forced warm persona, branding confusion, US‑centric/bias issues, and unclear position versus cheaper Gemini offerings.

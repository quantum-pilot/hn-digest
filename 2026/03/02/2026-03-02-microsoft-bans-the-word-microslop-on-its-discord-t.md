# Microsoft bans the word "Microslop" on its Discord, then locks the server

- Score: 1001 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47216047) | Link: https://www.windowslatest.com/2026/03/02/microsoft-gets-tired-of-microslop-bans-the-word-on-its-discord-then-locks-the-server-after-backlash/

### TL;DR
- Microsoft’s official Copilot Discord began auto-blocking the pejorative nickname “Microslop,” prompting users to flood the server with obfuscated variants, bans, and a subsequent server lockdown. After coverage and mockery, Microsoft said the word filter and lockdown were temporary anti-spam measures responding to a coordinated raid, not a lasting speech policy. HN commenters frame the episode as emblematic of Microsoft’s de-prioritization of consumers, cultural rot around Windows, and the Streisand effect amplifying frustration with its Windows 11 AI push.

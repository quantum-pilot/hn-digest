# British Columbia to end time changes, adopt year-round daylight time

- Score: 328 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47223620) | Link: https://www.cbc.ca/news/canada/british-columbia/b-c-adopting-year-round-daylight-time-9.7111657

## TL;DR
British Columbia will stop changing clocks after March 8, 2026, permanently adopting daylight time and branding it “Pacific time.” Government cites strong public support (93% in a 2019 survey), sleep/health concerns, and reduced accident risk from avoiding biannual time shifts. Most of the province will match Yukon year-round; some eastern communities on mountain time keep existing practices. In Vancouver this means darker winter mornings (sunrise as late as 9:08 a.m.) but lighter late afternoons, which fuels debate over standard vs daylight time.

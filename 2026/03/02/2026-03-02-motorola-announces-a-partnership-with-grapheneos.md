# Motorola announces a partnership with GrapheneOS

- Score: 2018 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47214645) | Link: https://motorolanews.com/motorola-three-new-b2b-solutions-at-mwc-2026/

### TL;DR
Motorola is partnering long‑term with the GrapheneOS Foundation to ship future phones engineered for GrapheneOS compatibility, combining Motorola/Lenovo’s ThinkShield stack with a hardened AOSP-based OS. Alongside that, Motorola announced Moto Analytics, an enterprise platform that surfaces device health and performance data across fleets, and “Private Image Data,” which automatically strips sensitive metadata (e.g., location) from new camera photos. Hacker News reaction mixes enthusiasm about finally getting GrapheneOS on non‑Pixel hardware with concerns over Motorola’s update history, Lenovo’s China ownership, and mainstream usability.

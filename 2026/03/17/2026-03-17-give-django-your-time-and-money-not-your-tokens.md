# Give Django your time and money, not your tokens

- Score: 393 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47400089) | Link: https://www.better-simple.com/django/2026/03/16/give-django-your-time-and-money/

### TL;DR
The post argues that using paid LLM tokens to auto-generate Django pull requests is worse than useless: it burdens maintainers with opaque, poorly understood changes while eroding the human, mentoring-centric culture that makes Django sustainable for decades. LLMs should be comprehension aids, not surrogates that write code, PR descriptions, and review replies. If you don’t truly understand the ticket, solution, and feedback, you’re harming the project; better to donate money and invest time learning than “buy” contributions with tokens.

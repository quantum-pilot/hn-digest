# Silicon Valley's "Pronatalists" Killed WFH. The Strait of Hormuz Brought It Back

- Score: 186 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47412023) | Link: https://www.governance.fyi/p/silicon-valleys-pronatalists-killed

### TL;DR
The article argues that remote work is one of the most powerful, cheapest pronatalist policies we have: new cross-country research links even one WFH day per week to roughly 0.3–0.45 additional children per woman, implying ~291k U.S. births annually attributable to WFH. Meanwhile, 2020s return‑to‑office (RTO) mandates function as de‑facto anti‑natalist policy, especially for educated women. The 2026 Strait of Hormuz oil shock forced Asian governments to re‑mandate WFH for energy savings, ironically reviving a system Silicon Valley “pronatalists” had just dismantled.

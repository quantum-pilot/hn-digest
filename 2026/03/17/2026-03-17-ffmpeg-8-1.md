# FFmpeg 8.1

- Score: 349 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47413525) | Link: https://ffmpeg.org/index.html#pr8.1

### TL;DR
FFmpeg 8.1 “Hoare” is a minor but substantial release: it adds experimental xHE-AAC (Mps212) and MPEG‑H audio decoding, EXIF metadata parsing, LCEVC metadata handling, IAMF Ambisonic projection support, and new filters. GPU work continues with Vulkan compute-based ProRes and DPX codecs, plus D3D12 H.264/AV1 encoders and filters and Rockchip H.264/HEVC hardware encoding. Internal changes speed up Vulkan initialization and prepare a swscale rewrite. HN discussion praises FFmpeg’s ubiquity, compilers vs binaries, JPEG‑XS, Windows screen capture, and Vulkan codec performance questions.

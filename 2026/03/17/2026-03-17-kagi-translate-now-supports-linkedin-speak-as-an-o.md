# Kagi Translate now supports LinkedIn Speak as an output language

- Score: 1303 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47408703) | Link: https://translate.kagi.com/?from=en&to=LinkedIn+speak

- **TL;DR**  
Kagi Translate now offers “LinkedIn Speak” as an output language, turning any input into hyper-optimistic, corporate, self‑branding prose. Hacker News commenters stress‑test it with memes and classics—the Gettysburg Address, Gangsta’s Paradise, Navy SEAL copypasta, death threats—and get eerily on‑point rewrites full of “growth mindset,” “disruption,” and faux‑professional gravitas. People find it both hilarious and disturbingly accurate, noting it often sounds sharper and more articulate than real LinkedIn posts, while perfectly capturing their tone and buzzword density.

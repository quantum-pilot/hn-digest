# Mayor of Paris removed parking spaces, reduced the number of cars

- Score: 222 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47466697) | Link: https://www.cnn.com/2026/03/21/travel/paris-transformation-anne-hidalgo-mayor

- TL;DR  
Anne Hidalgo’s 12-year tenure reshaped Paris from car-dominated to people- and bike-centric: parking removed, streets pedestrianized, 100 school streets closed to traffic, cycling and air quality sharply improved, and Paris crowned Europe’s top cycling city. But congestion has risen modestly, bus use fell 31%, hospitalizations for cyclists/pedestrians increased, and some residents resent safety risks, heritage loss, and privileging “young, able-bodied” users. Hacker News commenters mostly celebrate walkability’s health benefits while debating tourist-centric media framing and limits in less-planned cities.

# Passengers who refuse to use headphones can now be kicked off United flights

- Score: 173 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47469417) | Link: https://www.cnn.com/2026/03/21/travel/travel-news-happiest-countries

### TL;DR
United Airlines has updated its contract of carriage to allow denying boarding or removing passengers who play audio or video without headphones, treating loud personal devices as grounds for ejection rather than mere rudeness. The change comes amid elevated levels of unruly passenger behavior since the pandemic and growing frustration with speakerphone and TikTok use in cramped spaces. Hacker News discussion largely supports the policy, debating cultural norms around public noise and contrasting it with other in‑flight issues like illness and discomfort.

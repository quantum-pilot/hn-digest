# Blocking Internet Archive Won't Stop AI, but Will Erase Web's Historical Record

- Score: 480 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47464818) | Link: https://www.eff.org/deeplinks/2026/03/blocking-internet-archive-wont-stop-ai-it-will-erase-webs-historical-record

### TL;DR
Major news outlets like the New York Times are now technically blocking the Internet Archive, citing fears that its crawls enable AI training. EFF argues this response is misdirected: nonprofit archiving and search are well-established fair uses, and cutting off the Wayback Machine erases the only stable record of many online articles and edits. HN discussion assumes AI scraping is ultimately unstoppable, and shifts the focus to load, incentives, alternative archives, and the real value of news content for training.

# Some things just take time

- Score: 470 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47467537) | Link: https://lucumr.pocoo.org/2026/3/20/some-things-just-take-time/

### TL;DR
Ronacher argues that certain qualities—trust, durability, deep craft, real communities—are inherently time-bound and cannot be compressed by AI or “move fast” culture. Friction and slowness in processes like compliance, review, and shutdowns often exist to protect users and relationships. Hyper-automation and AI-generated code risk creating disposable software and ghost-startups that break trust. Time-saving tools rarely give us more free time; competitive pressure refills the gap. The things that really matter look more like 50‑year‑old trees than weekend hacks.

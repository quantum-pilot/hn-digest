# Afroman found not liable in defamation case

- Score: 1081 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47436950) | Link: https://nypost.com/2026/03/18/us-news/afroman-found-not-liable-in-bizarre-ohio-defamation-case/

## TL;DR
Afroman (Joseph Foreman) was found not liable after seven Ohio deputies sued him for defamation, false-light, and emotional distress over his satirical “Lemon Pound Cake” video, which used his home-surveillance footage of a 2022 drug raid that produced no charges. Jurors accepted his defense that mocking public officials about an arguably botched raid is protected speech. Hacker News commenters frame the verdict as a win for recording police, highlight U.S. police militarization, and note the huge Streisand effect from the officers’ lawsuit.

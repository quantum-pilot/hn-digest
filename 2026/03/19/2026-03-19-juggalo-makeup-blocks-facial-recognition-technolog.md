# Juggalo makeup blocks facial recognition technology (2019)

- Score: 220 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47438675) | Link: https://consequence.net/2019/07/juggalo-makeup-facial-recognition/

### TL;DR
The article describes how Insane Clown Posse “Juggalo” face paint can fool many facial-recognition systems that rely on contrast-based landmarks around eyes, nose, and jaw, misreading the jawline and mouth, though depth-based systems like Apple’s Face ID still work. Hacker News discussion treats this as dazzle camouflage and a vivid demo of how brittle real-world recognition remains, but argues lasting protection must come from legal and cultural limits on surveillance, not temporary makeup hacks, echoing cyberpunk fiction and mask-wearing trends.

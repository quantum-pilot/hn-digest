# Rijksmuseum researchers discover new painting by Rembrandt van Rijn

- Score: 135 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47249139) | Link: https://www.rijksmuseum.nl/en/press/press-releases/rijksmuseum-researchers-discover-new-painting-by-rembrandt-van-rijn

## TL;DR

Researchers at Amsterdam’s Rijksmuseum have re-attributed the 1633 painting Vision of Zacharias in the Temple to Rembrandt, using the same advanced imaging and materials science as Operation Night Watch. Pigments, panel dating, paint-layer structure, original signature and compositional changes all match Rembrandt’s early Amsterdam period, overturning a 1960 de-attribution that had pushed the work into obscurity. HN commenters debate attribution-driven art valuation, swap museum tips for visiting the Rijksmuseum, and share personal reactions to the newly public image.

# I ported Linux to the PS5 and turned it into a Steam Machine

- Score: 304 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47296849) | Link: https://xcancel.com/theflow0/status/2030011206040256841

### TL;DR
Security researcher Andy Nguyen exploited the PS5 to boot Linux with working GPU drivers, effectively turning Sony’s console into a Steam gaming PC capable of running GTA V Enhanced with ray tracing and desktop apps. HN readers admire the technical feat but focus on what it reveals about locked‑down consumer hardware, ownership, and right‑to‑repair. Discussion centers on how this build leverages prior work on Sony’s datacenter BC‑250 chip, the need for complex exploit chains, and tradeoffs versus keeping stock PS5 functionality.

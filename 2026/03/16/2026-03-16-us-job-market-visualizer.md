# US Job Market Visualizer

- Score: 386 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47400060) | Link: https://karpathy.ai/jobs/

### TL;DR
A treemap-style US job market visualizer plots 342 BLS occupations by employment size, then colors them by projected growth, median pay, education, or LLM-estimated “digital AI exposure.” The AI exposure score uses a structured prompt to rate how much current, mostly digital AI could reshape each job, emphasizing whether its output is fully computer-based. The author frames it as an exploratory research tool, not a forecast, noting that high exposure means transformation, not guaranteed disappearance, and doesn’t model demand or regulation.

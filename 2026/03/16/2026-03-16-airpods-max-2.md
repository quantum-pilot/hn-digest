# AirPods Max 2

- Score: 175 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47398681) | Link: https://www.apple.com/airpods-max/

## TL;DR
AirPods Max 2 are Apple’s refreshed over‑ear headphones: same industrial design and 386g weight, but upgraded internals. The new H2 chip drives 1.5× stronger active noise cancellation, retuned high‑fidelity sound, and Personalized Spatial Audio. Via USB‑C they now support wired lossless and ultra‑low‑latency audio, especially for movies and gaming. Apple Intelligence features add Live Translation, Adaptive Audio, Conversation Awareness, and richer Siri interactions. HN discussion is dominated by comfort, power‑management quirks, price/value, and reliability concerns rather than enthusiasm for the new features.

## Comment pulse

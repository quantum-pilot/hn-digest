# MoD sources warn Palantir role at heart of government is threat to UK security

- Score: 575 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47397797) | Link: https://www.thenerve.news/p/palantir-technologies-uk-mod-sources-government-data-insights-security-state-secrets

### TL;DR
MoD systems engineers warn that Palantir’s deep integration across UK government is a national security risk, even if “sovereign data” formally remains owned by the state. They argue Palantir’s software lets it fuse defence, health, infrastructure and other datasets into powerful metadata and analytical models, enabling inferred state secrets and detailed population profiles that could become Palantir’s own intellectual property. Critics fear US access, political leverage, and weak UK regulation; Palantir dismisses the allegations as baseless, while HN commenters debate whether this is overblown or existential.

# Palestinian boy, 12, describes how Israeli forces killed his family in car

- Score: 873 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47402950) | Link: https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c70n2x7p22do

### TL;DR
A 12-year-old Palestinian boy recounts how undercover Israeli forces riddled his family’s car with bullets in Tammun, West Bank, killing his parents and two younger brothers, including a blind 7-year-old on his mother’s lap. The Israeli army claims the car accelerated toward them; an eyewitness and surviving children say it had stopped and was fired on without warning. Heavy gunfire and dozens of shell casings were found. The case spotlights a broader post–Oct 7 pattern of lethal force and impunity in the West Bank, which HN commenters link to Western complicity and to debates on what belongs on a tech forum.

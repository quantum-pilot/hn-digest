# Ask HN: What is it like being in a CS major program these days?

- Score: 159 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47397190) | Link: https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47397190

- TL;DR  
Current CS majors describe a strange moment: job prospects feel worse, with fewer visible big‑tech internships or campus recruiters, yet coursework is often more engaging and conceptually deep. Generative AI is everywhere—some professors discourage or booby‑trap it, others accept it but struggle to define “hard” projects when LLMs can do junior-level work. Many students quietly lean on AI to ace classes or inflate side projects, while a subset still pursue CS mainly for intellectual curiosity, accepting uncertain career returns.

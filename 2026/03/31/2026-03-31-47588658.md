# Tell HN: Chrome says "suspicious download" when trying to download yt-dlp

- Score: 265 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47588658) | Link: https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47588658

### TL;DR
Chrome (and sometimes Firefox) flag yt-dlp binaries as “suspicious” or “not commonly downloaded,” likely due to heuristic reputation systems and packaging tools like PyInstaller, not a specific malware signature. This disproportionately hurts small/open‑source projects: new or niche binaries trigger scary warnings until they’re widely downloaded or code‑signed, creating a chicken‑and‑egg adoption problem. Commenters debate whether this is just generic safety tech or indirectly aligned with Google’s interest in limiting tools that bypass YouTube’s preferred access paths.

# Microsoft: Copilot is for entertainment purposes only

- Score: 441 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47587866) | Link: https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-copilot/for-individuals/termsofuse

### TL;DR

Microsoft’s updated Copilot consumer terms cover the free and “personal” Copilot experiences, not Microsoft 365 or GitHub Copilot. They emphasize safety rules, bans on harassment, deepfakes, and illegal use, and grant Microsoft broad rights to use your prompts and responses to operate and improve the service. A key clause frames Copilot as “for entertainment purposes only,” explicitly disclaims reliability, and shifts responsibility and liability to users—prompting Hacker News debate about legal overreach, deceptive marketing, and double standards in software warranties.

# New York City hospitals drop Palantir as controversial AI firm expands in UK

- Score: 243 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47535371) | Link: https://www.theguardian.com/technology/2026/mar/26/new-york-hospitals-palantir-ai

- TL;DR  
    - New York City’s public hospital system will let its $4m Palantir contract lapse, amid activist pressure and concern over the firm’s access to de-identified patient data. Hospitals insist Palantir only optimized billing, with firewalls against information reaching US immigration authorities, and will replace it with in-house tools. Meanwhile Palantir deepens its £330m NHS data platform and UK financial watchdog work, drawing scrutiny from privacy groups and MPs, while HN commenters debate its role, business model, and “spyware” reputation.

# John Bradley, author of xv, has died

- Score: 194 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47534086) | Link: https://voxday.net/2026/03/25/rip-john-bradley/

### TL;DR
HN is marking the death of John Bradley, remembered via a bandmate’s obituary describing him as Booster Patrol’s founder, producer, and lead guitarist, honored with a bluesy, Christian tribute song. In comments, he’s celebrated as the author of xv, a beloved X11 image viewer whose thoughtful UI, powerful color controls, and shareware model shaped many careers and workflows. Commenters share technical anecdotes, family memories, and ongoing use of xv, lamenting how rarely modern tools reach that level of craft.

### Comment pulse

- Enduring relevance → Some rely on xv for remote/headless work, citing features unmatched by newer open-source tools—counterpoint: others argue OSS abundance compensates for quality differences.

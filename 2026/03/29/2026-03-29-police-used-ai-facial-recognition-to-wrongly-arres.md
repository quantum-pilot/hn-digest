# Police used AI facial recognition to wrongly arrest TN woman for crimes in ND

- Score: 333 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47563384) | Link: https://www.cnn.com/2026/03/29/us/angela-lipps-ai-facial-recognition

## TL;DR
A Tennessee grandmother, Angela Lipps, spent over five months jailed and was extradited to North Dakota after Clearview AI facial recognition wrongly matched her to bank-fraud suspects in Fargo/West Fargo—a state she’d never visited. Police relied on the AI hit and failed to perform basic checks; exculpatory bank records later proved she was in Tennessee. Fargo police now blame “process errors” and will stop using the neighboring department’s AI, but haven’t apologized. HN discussion centers on lazy policing, over-trusted AI, weak legal safeguards, and biometric privacy risks.

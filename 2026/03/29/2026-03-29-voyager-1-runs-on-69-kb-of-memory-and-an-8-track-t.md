# Voyager 1 runs on 69 KB of memory and an 8-track tape recorder

- Score: 362 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47564421) | Link: https://techfixated.com/a-1977-time-capsule-voyager-1-runs-on-69-kb-of-memory-and-an-8-track-tape-recorder-4/

- TL;DR  
Voyager 1, launched in 1977, is 15 billion miles away, running on 69 KB of memory and an eight-track tape recorder, yet still returning unique data from interstellar space. Built with conservative, redundant 1970s engineering, it discovered Io’s volcanoes, Titan’s atmosphere, and later crossed the heliopause. A 2025 nail‑biting thruster revival kept its antenna pointed at Earth as power and hardware slowly decay. HN readers marvel at its elegance, the rare “Grand Tour” trajectory, and contrast it with today’s software bloat.

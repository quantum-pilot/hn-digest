# AI coding is gambling

- Score: 302 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47428541) | Link: https://notes.visaint.space/ai-coding-is-gambling/

### TL;DR
The author argues that using AI coding tools feels less like programming and more like pulling a slot machine: addictive, non-deterministic, and emotionally hollow. Instead of the soul-nourishing work of understanding systems, discovering clever fixes, and truly “owning” the result, they feel reduced to cleaning up loose, shallow connections made by an “infinite plagiarism machine.” AI has expanded what they can attempt as a designer–developer, but they’re unsure if that’s genuine growth or just chasing jackpots of working code.

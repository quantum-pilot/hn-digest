# Despite Doubts, Federal Cyber Experts Approved Microsoft Cloud Service

- Score: 427 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47426057) | Link: https://www.propublica.org/article/microsoft-cloud-fedramp-cybersecurity-government

### TL;DR
ProPublica reports that FedRAMP, the U.S. cloud-security gatekeeper, authorized Microsoft’s GCC High cloud for highly sensitive government data even after reviewers said they lacked basic encryption diagrams, had “no confidence” in its security posture, and internally called the package “a pile of shit.” Years of stalled documentation, conflicted third‑party assessors, political pressure from the Justice Department CIO (later hired by Microsoft), and GCC High’s widespread deployment pushed FedRAMP into a conditional “rubber‑stamp” approval, reinforcing fears that U.S. cloud oversight is security theater, not assurance.

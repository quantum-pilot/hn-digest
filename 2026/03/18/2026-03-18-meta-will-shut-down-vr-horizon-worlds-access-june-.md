# Meta will shut down VR Horizon Worlds access June 15

- Score: 139 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47427214) | Link: https://www.engadget.com/ar-vr/meta-will-shut-down-vr-horizon-worlds-access-in-june-222028919.html

- TL;DR  
Meta will end VR access to Horizon Worlds on Quest headsets by June 15, 2026, delisting its worlds in March and removing the app entirely in June. Horizon will live on only as a mobile experience; Hyperscape Capture will lose its shared social component. The shift underscores Meta’s retreat from its grand metaverse ambitions after weak adoption, ridicule over low production values, and heavy losses, as it reallocates toward AI, smart glasses, and core VR hardware instead of a flagship social VR world.

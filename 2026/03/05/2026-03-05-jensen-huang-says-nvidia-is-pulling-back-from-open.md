# Jensen Huang says Nvidia is pulling back from OpenAI and Anthropic

- Score: 214 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47256781) | Link: https://techcrunch.com/2026/03/04/jensen-huang-says-nvidia-is-pulling-back-from-openai-and-anthropic-but-his-explanation-raises-more-questions-than-it-answers/

### TL;DR
At a Morgan Stanley conference, Nvidia CEO Jensen Huang said Nvidia’s recent stakes in OpenAI and Anthropic will probably be its last, blaming impending IPOs. TechCrunch argues that doesn’t fit how late‑stage investing usually works, and suggests deeper reasons: circular “you fund me, I buy your chips” deals attracting bubble concerns; Anthropic’s clashes with Nvidia and the US government; OpenAI’s controversial Pentagon deal and user backlash. Nvidia now holds equity in two rival, politically divergent AI labs, and seems to be quietly de‑risking.

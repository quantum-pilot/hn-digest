# Judge orders government to begin refunding more than $130B in tariffs

- Score: 744 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47261688) | Link: https://www.wsj.com/politics/policy/judge-orders-government-to-begin-refunding-more-than-130-billion-in-tariffs-fdc1e62c

### TL;DR
A federal Court of International Trade judge ordered the Trump administration to start refunding over $130B in tariffs that the Supreme Court invalidated last month. More than 2,000 importer lawsuits seek repayment of duties collected under Trump’s second-term global tariffs. The Congressional Budget Office estimated U.S. consumers bore 70–80% of those costs, but legal refunds go to importers, not households. Hacker News discussion centers on alleged cronyism around tariff-refund speculation, the economics of who really paid, and institutional failure by Congress and courts.

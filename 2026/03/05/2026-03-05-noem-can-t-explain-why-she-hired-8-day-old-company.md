# Noem Can't Explain Why She Hired 8-Day-Old Company for Ad Campaign

- Score: 192 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47259303) | Link: https://newrepublic.com/post/207381/kristi-noem-explain-company-ad-campaign

### TL;DR
Homeland Security Secretary Kristi Noem is under fire for steering a $143 million, no-bid DHS ad contract to Safe America Media, a company created just eight days before winning the deal and with no public presence, track record, or clear headquarters. A ProPublica investigation and House Oversight questioning suggest the firm mainly served as a pass‑through to politically connected allies at the Strategy Group, closely tied to Noem. Hacker News comments frame this as open, normalized corruption enabled by weakened procurement safeguards and negligible accountability.

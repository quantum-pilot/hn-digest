# GPT-5.4

- Score: 569 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47265045) | Link: https://openai.com/index/introducing-gpt-5-4/

### TL;DR
GPT‑5.4 is OpenAI’s new “frontier” work model, combining GPT‑5.3‑Codex–level coding with stronger reasoning, tool use, and native computer-use (Playwright/UI control) in a single system. It supports up to 1M-token context (with higher pricing beyond 272k tokens), is more token‑efficient than GPT‑5.2, and improves on legal/finance-style document work, spreadsheets, and presentations. Safety controls and CoT-monitoring are extended. HN reactions praise capabilities but criticize OpenAI’s model/version naming, pricing complexity, and uneven product/UX details like the “Ask ChatGPT” integration.

# US tech firms pledge at White House to bear costs of energy for datacenters

- Score: 167 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47256545) | Link: https://www.theguardian.com/us-news/2026/mar/04/us-tech-companies-energy-cost-pledge-white-house

### TL;DR
US tech giants (Google, Microsoft, Meta, Amazon, Oracle, xAI, OpenAI) signed a White House “Ratepayer Protection Pledge” to fund new power generation, grid upgrades, and special tariffs for their datacenters rather than leaning on existing grids and raising household bills. The move aims to calm voter and local opposition to AI-driven datacenter expansion, but critics doubt voluntary pledges, highlight hidden costs (pollution, crowded-out capacity, fossil-fuel bias), and see this as part of a broader tragedy-of-the-commons dynamic around AI, energy, and shared resources.

# You Just Reveived

- Score: 218 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47257637) | Link: https://dylan.gr/1772520728

### TL;DR
A blogger on a shared prepaid phone gets an SMS from Vodafone granting “FREE UNLIMITED DATA AND 999999 MINUTES… FOR 5 DAYS,” typo and all. They really do see a million minutes in their account—but only for the 7,200 minutes that five days contain. The author muses about whether this is a human error, a bizarre automated promo, or some anonymous benefactor in a call center. HN commenters mostly attribute it to sloppy telco testing and marketing tricks, plus complain about misleading “unlimited” plans.

# People inside Microsoft are fighting to drop mandatory Microsoft Account

- Score: 480 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47542695) | Link: https://www.windowscentral.com/microsoft/windows-11/people-inside-microsoft-are-fighting-to-drop-windows-11s-mandatory-microsoft-account-requirements-during-setup

### TL;DR
Microsoft is planning major Windows 11 fixes (performance, fewer ads, less AI bloat) but has not removed the requirement to use a Microsoft Account and internet connection during setup. Internally, influential engineers like VP Scott Hanselman say they “hate” the mandate and are “working on it,” but it’s a political, not technical, fight: many teams benefit from forced accounts (telemetry, OneDrive, Store, upsells). HN commenters argue this mandate erodes trust, breaks workflows, and accelerates migration to Linux, macOS, and SteamOS.

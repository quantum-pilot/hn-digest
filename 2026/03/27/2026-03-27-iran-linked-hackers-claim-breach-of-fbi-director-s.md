# Iran-linked hackers claim breach of FBI director's personal email

- Score: 189 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47543167) | Link: https://www.reuters.com/world/us/iran-linked-hackers-claim-breach-of-fbi-directors-personal-email-doj-official-2026-03-27/

### TL;DR
Iran-linked Handala Hack Team claims it breached FBI Director Kash Patel’s personal Gmail, posting samples online that a DOJ official says appear authentic. The emails, dating roughly 2010–2019, reportedly mix personal and work-related correspondence, suggesting intelligence and blackmail value more than direct access to classified material. Handala is widely seen as an Iranian state-linked persona that recently claimed a Stryker hack. Hacker News discussion centers on Patel’s judgment, U.S. cyber‑OPSEC, and how exploitable “personal” data can be.

# Meow.camera

- Score: 175 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47543204) | Link: https://meow.camera/#4258783365322591678

## TL;DR
meow.camera is a fan-made web viewer for live-streamed cat (and the occasional dog) feeding stations from China’s Hello Street Cat/JieMao app. It doesn’t control feeders itself but lets people easily browse and watch multiple cameras, with options like themes, screenshots, and integration with the Purrrr app, which can actually trigger food. HN comments mostly revel in the cozy “pet TV” vibe, discuss remote-feeding mechanics, reminisce about open webcams, and imagine social apps for pets—while worrying about trolls and security.

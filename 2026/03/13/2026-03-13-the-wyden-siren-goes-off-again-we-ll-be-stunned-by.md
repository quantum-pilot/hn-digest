# The Wyden Siren Goes Off Again: We'll Be "Stunned" by NSA Under Section 702

- Score: 335 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47366374) | Link: https://www.techdirt.com/2026/03/12/the-wyden-siren-goes-off-again-well-be-stunned-by-what-the-nsa-is-doing-under-section-702/

- TL;DR  
  Senator Ron Wyden warns that a classified, government-wide interpretation of FISA Section 702 greatly expands NSA surveillance of Americans, yet Congress is about to reauthorize it without seeing the real rules. His past “Wyden Siren” alerts, later vindicated by Snowden-era revelations, make this especially ominous. The piece argues that supposed 702 “reforms” are hollow—oversight entrusted to partisan officials who refuse even basic record-keeping—while the law now allows conscripting ordinary tech workers into secret spying.

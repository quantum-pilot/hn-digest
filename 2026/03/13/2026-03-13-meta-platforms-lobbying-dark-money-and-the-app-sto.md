# Meta Platforms: Lobbying, dark money, and the App Store Accountability Act

- Score: 1136 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47362528) | Link: https://github.com/upper-up/meta-lobbying-and-other-findings

### TL;DR
Open-source investigators compile public records to argue Meta ran a coordinated, multi-channel campaign to pass “App Store Accountability Act” laws that force age verification onto Apple/Google app stores while largely sparing social platforms. They document direct lobbying, opaque funding of the Digital Childhood Alliance, state-level super PACs, and ties into the Arabella dark-money network (grant route ruled out, other paths unproven). HN discussion frames this as Meta’s revenge for Apple’s privacy moves, raises civil-liberties worries, and questions LLM-driven investigative rigor.

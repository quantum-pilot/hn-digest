# E2E encrypted messaging on Instagram will no longer be supported after 8 May

- Score: 352 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47363922) | Link: https://help.instagram.com/491565145294150

- TL;DR  
  Instagram has announced that its optional end-to-end encrypted chats will be discontinued after May 8, 2026, meaning Meta’s servers will again be able to read all DMs. Users can export affected chats before then. HN commenters argue Meta-controlled E2EE was never fully trustworthy because Meta owns both clients and servers, yet see this as a clear privacy regression likely driven by AI and moderation needs. Many expect privacy-conscious users to move to Signal or default-E2EE WhatsApp.

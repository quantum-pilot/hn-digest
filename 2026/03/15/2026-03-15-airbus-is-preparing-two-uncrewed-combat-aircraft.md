# Airbus is preparing two uncrewed combat aircraft

- Score: 175 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47382277) | Link: https://www.airbus.com/en/newsroom/press-releases/2026-03-airbus-is-preparing-two-uncrewed-combat-aircraft-from-kratos-for-first-flight-with-a-european

### TL;DR
Airbus is adapting two US‑built Kratos XQ‑58A Valkyrie drones into “Uncrewed Collaborative Combat Aircraft” for Germany, flying in 2026 and operational by 2029. The hardware stays American, but Airbus adds its European MARS mission system and MindShare AI “brain,” enabling Eurofighters to command Valkyries for high‑risk kinetic and non‑kinetic missions as affordable, semi‑sacrificial wingmen. HN debates whether this “exquisite but cheaper” loyal‑wingman model is the right answer versus swarms of very cheap drones, and how Ukraine/Iran lessons shape Europe’s doctrine and spending.

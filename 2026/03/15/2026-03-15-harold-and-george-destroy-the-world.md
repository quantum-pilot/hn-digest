# Harold and George Destroy the World

- Score: 222 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47386908) | Link: https://tomclancy.info/harold-and-george.html

### TL;DR
A blogger riffs on Captain Underpants’ pranksters Harold and George to describe modern politics, war, and blockbuster movies as fantasies made by unsupervised ten-year-olds. He ties Iran, a proposed “Department of War,” coin iconography, and FIFA corruption into a portrait of childish imagination wielding adult power. Commenters push back on historical details, then broaden into debates over propaganda, declining education and media quality, nostalgia bias, and our own tendency to regress when challenged.

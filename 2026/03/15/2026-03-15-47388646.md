# Ask HN: How is AI-assisted coding going for you professionally?

- Score: 209 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47388646) | Link: https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47388646

### TL;DR
Many engineers report that AI-assisted coding is a mixed professional blessing. Some see higher throughput, faster learning of unfamiliar stacks, and big wins on glue tasks, data analysis, and deployment. But others feel their jobs have become cleaning up unreviewed AI slop: broken APIs, bloated error handling, unread 50-page AI-generated docs, and misaligned business logic. This often masks deeper team and management issues, erodes craftsmanship, and may hollow out mid-level roles while empowering a smaller group of leads and solo builders.

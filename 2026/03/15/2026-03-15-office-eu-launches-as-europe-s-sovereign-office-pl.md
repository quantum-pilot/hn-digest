# Office.eu launches as Europe's sovereign office platform

- Score: 250 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47390233) | Link: https://office.eu/media/pressrelease-20260304

### TL;DR
Office.eu is a newly launched, 100%-EU‑owned office suite positioned as a sovereign alternative to Microsoft 365 and Google Workspace, built atop open‑source Nextcloud and hosted only in European data centers. It promises migration tools, MS/Google‑comparable pricing, and full GDPR compliance. Hacker News readers mostly see it as a thinly rebranded Nextcloud instance from an unproven, opaque company, debate whether its “open source” marketing and AGPL obligations are met, question the “Office” name, yet still welcome more EU‑based options.

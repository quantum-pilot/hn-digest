# The Appalling Stupidity of Spotify's AI DJ

- Score: 347 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47385272) | Link: https://www.charlespetzold.com/blog/2026/02/The-Appalling-Stupidity-of-Spotifys-AI-DJ.html

### TL;DR
Petzold, a veteran programmer and classical-music listener, tries Spotify’s AI DJ hoping it can finally handle multi-movement works like Beethoven’s 7th Symphony. Instead, the DJ repeatedly misfires: it plays only the famous second movement, scrambles movement order, switches recordings mid-piece, jumps to unrelated works, and finally to pop songs. He argues this exposes how Spotify’s pop-centric metadata (artist/album/song) and profit priorities ignore classical structure, casting doubt on grandiose “AI can compose music” claims. HN mostly sees a product/metadata/licensing failure, not an AI capability limit.

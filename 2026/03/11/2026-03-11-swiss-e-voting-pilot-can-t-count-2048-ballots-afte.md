# Swiss e-voting pilot can't count 2,048 ballots after decryption failure

- Score: 142 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47334982) | Link: https://www.theregister.com/2026/03/11/swiss_evote_usb_snafu/

### TL;DR
A Swiss e-voting pilot failed to decrypt 2,048 ballots, making them uncountable and highlighting how fragile fully electronic systems can be: one cryptographic or implementation error can silently invalidate votes. Hacker News discussion focuses on why paper remains essential: physical processes scale with humans, make large-scale fraud harder, and are auditable by rival parties. Commenters debate whether advanced cryptography can truly deliver both secrecy and verifiability, and many advocate hybrid systems with voter-verifiable paper trails.

# The dead Internet is not a theory anymore

- Score: 311 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47340935) | Link: https://www.adriankrebs.ch/blog/dead-internet/

### TL;DR
HN commenters treat “dead Internet” less as conspiracy and more as a practical description: major platforms are being flooded by bots, SEO sludge, and automated marketing agents, degrading social interaction and discovery. Some celebrate a “small Internet” revival of personal sites and niche communities, while others note these will also be botted. Proposed defenses include identity verification, paywalls, and cryptographic proofs, but each threatens anonymity or accessibility. Many think the root cause is economic incentives that reward spam and engagement hacking.

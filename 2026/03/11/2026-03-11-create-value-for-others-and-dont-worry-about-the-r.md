# Create value for others and don’t worry about the returns

- Score: 646 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47332074) | Link: https://geohot.github.io//blog/jekyll/update/2026/03/11/running-69-agents.html

### TL;DR
The post (by George Hotz) pushes back on AI panic and hustle culture: AI is just better search/optimization, not magic, and current rhetoric mainly serves social media and corporate rent-seeking. The real risk isn’t “not running enough agents” but having a job that adds complexity instead of genuine value, since big players will consolidate that rent. His advice: stop playing zero-sum status games, focus on creating more value than you consume, and ignore fear-based comparisons.

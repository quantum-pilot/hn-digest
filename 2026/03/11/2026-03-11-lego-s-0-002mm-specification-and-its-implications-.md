# Lego's 0.002mm specification and its implications for manufacturing (2025)

- TL;DR  
Lego’s claimed 0.002 mm dimensional spec underpins its famous “clutch” feel and decades of cross-compatible bricks, impressing many HN readers who note subtle friction-tuned parts and even MRI calibration uses. Others argue similar tolerances are routine in automotive and packaging injection molding, suggesting Lego mainly markets quality to justify pricing. A parallel thread laments Lego’s shift from bins of generic bricks and Technic experimentation toward expensive, licensed display models, while clone brands start rivaling or surpassing Lego’s physical performance.

# PC processors entered the Gigahertz era today in the year 2000 with AMD's Athlon

- Score: 154 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47287792) | Link: https://www.tomshardware.com/pc-components/cpus/pc-processors-entered-the-gigahertz-era-today-in-the-year-2000-with-amds-athlon-amd-hit-marketing-gold-with-its-1-ghz-athlon-beat-intel-by-a-nose

### TL;DR
AMD’s 1 GHz Athlon, shipped in March 2000, officially kicked off the “gigahertz era,” beating Intel’s long-teased 1 GHz Pentium III by two days and months of real availability. The article recaps the milestone’s marketing hype (Chuck Yeager, moon landing analogies) and the chip’s specs: 180 nm, 22M transistors, 65W, Slot A, $1,299 tray price. Discussion contrasts that dramatic MHz decade with today’s focus on cores, SSDs, and AI accelerators, and debates whether performance progress really “slowed.”

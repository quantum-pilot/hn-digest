# The yoghurt delivery women combatting loneliness in Japan

- Score: 184 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47287344) | Link: https://www.bbc.com/travel/article/20260302-the-yoghurt-delivery-women-combatting-loneliness-in-japan

### TL;DR
Yakult’s door-to-door “Yakult Ladies” began in 1930s Japan as salespeople explaining an unusual probiotic drink and evolved into hyperlocal social infrastructure. In a rapidly ageing, lonely society, 31,000 mostly self-employed women now deliver small bottles and weekly check-ins to elderly customers, informally monitoring wellbeing and preventing people from “falling through the cracks.” Commenters compare this to Tupperware or Avon, probe the economics (low margins, gig-style pay, marketing halo), and debate whether such services humanely ease or opportunistically monetize chronic loneliness.

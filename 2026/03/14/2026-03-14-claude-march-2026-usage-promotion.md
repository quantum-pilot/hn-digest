# Claude March 2026 usage promotion

- Score: 166 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47380647) | Link: https://support.claude.com/en/articles/14063676-claude-march-2026-usage-promotion

### TL;DR
Anthropic is running a promo March 13–27, 2026 that doubles Claude usage during off‑peak hours (outside 8am–2pm ET / 5–11am PT) for Free, Pro, Max, and Team users. Extra off‑peak usage doesn’t count against weekly limits and reverts afterward. HN comments see this as a way to use idle GPUs and shift load away from US daytime, plus a psychological nudge to increase comfort with heavy usage. Discussion centers on pricing, compute economics, and whether this foreshadows time‑of‑day pricing.

# 2026 tech layoffs reach 45,000 in March

- Score: 144 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47380405) | Link: https://technode.global/2026/03/09/2026-tech-layoffs-reach-45000-in-march-more-than-9200-due-to-ai-and-automation-rationalfx/

### TL;DR
Around 45,000 tech workers have been laid off worldwide so far in 2026, with about 9,200 (20%) explicitly tied to AI and automation, according to RationalFX. Major contributors include Block (4,000 roles), WiseTech Global (2,000), Livspace (1,000), eBay (800), and Pinterest (675), often framed as “AI-first” restructurings. U.S. hubs like Seattle and San Francisco, plus cities such as Sydney, Stockholm, and Veldhoven, are heavily hit. Projections suggest 2026 layoffs could exceed 2025’s 245,000, while reskilling appears unable to match AI-driven job erosion.

### Comment pulse

- AI/GPU spending pressures payrolls → record capex makes headcount a tempting lever; markets reward layoffs — counterpoint: AI still struggles with many real-world development tasks.

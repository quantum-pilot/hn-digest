# RAM kits are now sold with one fake RAM stick alongside a real one

- Score: 252 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47375085) | Link: https://www.tomshardware.com/pc-components/ram/fake-ram-bundled-with-real-ram-to-create-a-performance-illusion-for-amd-users-1-1-value-pack-offers-desperate-psychological-relief-as-the-memory-shortage-worsens

- TL;DR  
  V-Color’s new “1+1” DDR5 kits pair one real RAM stick with a matching dummy module that has RGB lighting but no memory chips. The idea is to let budget‑constrained builders fill two DIMM slots visually while only paying for one working module, targeting AMD Ryzen systems—especially X3D CPUs whose large L3 cache partly mitigates single‑channel performance losses. The product is transparently marketed as cosmetic, reflecting a market where RAM prices have spiked amid AI‑driven shortages and aesthetics sell.

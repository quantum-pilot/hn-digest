# Megadev: A Development Kit for the Sega Mega Drive and Mega CD Hardware

- Score: 116 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47374745) | Link: https://github.com/drojaazu/megadev

- TL;DR  
Megadev is an open-source development kit for Sega Mega Drive/Genesis and, crucially, the much trickier Mega CD add-on. Written in C and 68k assembly, it bundles libraries, headers, docs, and examples aimed at experienced low-level or embedded programmers who want flexibility and minimal external tooling, rather than a beginner-friendly engine like SGDK. Hacker News commenters mostly respond with enthusiasm, sharing their own homebrew tooling, expressing intense Sega nostalgia, and noting how much “obsolete” hardware still attracts serious new software efforts.

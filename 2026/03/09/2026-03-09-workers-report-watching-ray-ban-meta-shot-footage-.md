# Workers report watching Ray-Ban Meta-shot footage of people using the bathroom

- Score: 190 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47313613) | Link: https://arstechnica.com/gadgets/2026/03/workers-report-watching-ray-ban-meta-shot-footage-of-people-using-the-bathroom/

### TL;DR
A Swedish investigation reports that data annotators at Meta contractor Sama review raw Ray-Ban Meta smart-glasses footage, including people undressing, having sex, using bathrooms, viewing porn, and exposing banking details. Meta’s policies already state that media and AI interactions can be sent to Meta, shared with vendors, and reviewed by humans to train products, with some face-blurring. Critics argue this contradicts marketing claims like “designed for privacy, controlled by you.” A US class-action lawsuit alleges deceptive practices; regulators are asking questions.

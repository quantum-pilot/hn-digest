# FontCrafter: Turn your handwriting into a real font

- Score: 413 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47306655) | Link: https://arcade.pirillo.com/fontcrafter.html

### TL;DR
FontCrafter is a fully client-side web app that converts a scanned handwriting template into an installable font (OTF/TTF/WOFF2/Base64) with ligatures, contextual alternates, kerning, and auto-generated extended/accented characters—all free, no signup, and with no data ever leaving your browser. Compared to subscription-based Calligraphr, it emphasizes privacy, advanced features, and format flexibility. Hacker News commenters like the competition and offline longevity, share historical and artistic precedents, but also report brittle scanning/registration and “vibe coded” UX issues.

### Comment pulse

- UX and robustness mixed → some love the concept but report crosshair misdetection, poor registration, and limited row-usage options; suggest manual anchor marking and better templates.  
- Prior art and use cases abound → from XP Tablet/Microsoft Font Maker and JS path-based handwriting to training forms and doctor-handwriting jokes, people see both nostalgia and playful applications.

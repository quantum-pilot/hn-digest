# DARPA’s new X-76

- Score: 139 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47311669) | Link: https://www.darpa.mil/news/2026/darpa-new-x-76-speed-of-jet-freedom-of-helicopter

## TL;DR
DARPA and Bell Textron are building the X-76 “SPRINT” X-plane, a vertical-lift aircraft aiming for jet-like cruise speeds (>400 knots) while operating from unprepared surfaces without runways. It’s a technology demonstrator for high-speed, runway-independent special operations missions, now moving from design to manufacturing and ground tests, with flight tests planned for 2028. Hacker News discussion highlights its deep roots in Bell’s prior VTOL research, debates safety and maintenance complexity, and raises discomfort about more efficient strike capabilities.

## Comment pulse
- Ethical worry → Runway-free, fast VTOL mainly enhances special operations and strike reach, making deadly missions easier and more flexible.

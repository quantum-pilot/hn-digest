# Global warming has accelerated significantly

- Score: 920 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47275088) | Link: https://www.researchsquare.com/article/rs-6079807/v1

### TL;DR
A new preprint by prominent climate scientists Rahmstorf and Foster removes the effects of El Niño, volcanic eruptions, and solar cycles from global temperature records and finds that, since 2015, the underlying rate of warming has increased, producing the fastest 10‑year temperature rise since 1945 with statistical significance. HN discussion centers on how to interpret non–peer-reviewed preprints, the tension between climate action and economics, ideas like climate clubs and tariffs, and whether individual choices versus systemic policy can meaningfully address accelerating warming.

# We might all be AI engineers now

- Score: 159 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47272734) | Link: https://yasint.dev/we-might-all-be-ai-engineers-now/

### TL;DR
The author argues that modern software work is shifting from “writing code” to “designing systems that AIs implement.” With strong fundamentals and architectural taste, LLMs can produce solid traversal logic, hashing layers, and boilerplate, while humans focus on decomposition, tradeoffs, and review. Used on small, well-scoped tasks and carefully audited, AI becomes an amplifier, not a replacement. Hacker News discusses whether this accelerates real learning or merely shortcuts it, how it reshapes job satisfaction, and its economic and quality implications.

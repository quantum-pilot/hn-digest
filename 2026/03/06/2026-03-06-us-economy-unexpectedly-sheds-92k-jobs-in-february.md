# US economy unexpectedly sheds 92k jobs in February

- Score: 483 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47275035) | Link: https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cjd98091g28o

### TL;DR
US nonfarm payrolls fell by 92,000 in February and unemployment ticked up to 4.4%, the weakest monthly jobs result since the 2024 government shutdown. Losses were broad-based across construction, manufacturing, transportation, information, leisure/hospitality, and especially private education and health services, where strikes hit healthcare. Federal employment is down 11% since late 2024, and earlier months were revised lower, undercutting a “stabilizing” narrative. The drop rattles markets, complicates Federal Reserve rate-cut decisions amid higher oil prices, and intensifies political battles over tariffs and economic management.

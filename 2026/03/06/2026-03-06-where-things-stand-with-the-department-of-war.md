# Where things stand with the Department of War

- Score: 606 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47269263) | Link: https://www.anthropic.com/news/where-stand-department-war

### TL;DR
Anthropic’s CEO says the U.S. Department of Defense has formally labeled the company a “supply chain risk,” which Anthropic plans to challenge in court as legally unsound and narrowly scoped to AI used directly in DoD contracts. He reiterates Anthropic’s two red lines—no fully autonomous weapons and no mass domestic surveillance—while stressing continued support for intelligence and planning tools, even offering models at nominal cost during transition. HN commenters focus on shifting tech-war ethics, militarization of AI, and Anthropic’s positioning as “less bad” PR.

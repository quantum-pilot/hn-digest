# LibreSprite – open-source pixel art editor

- Score: 246 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47272799) | Link: https://libresprite.github.io/

### TL;DR
LibreSprite is a free, GPL-licensed pixel art and animation editor, descended from Aseprite’s last open-source release, now with official macOS builds. The project targets users and distributions wanting fully redistributable binaries rather than Aseprite’s current source-available, proprietary-licensed model. In discussion, HN users clarify the licensing history, debate what “open source” should mean, and overwhelmingly praise Aseprite’s usability, with several saying they still gladly pay for it while welcoming LibreSprite as a community-driven alternative.

# Anthropic, please make a new Slack

- Score: 157 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47280200) | Link: https://www.fivetran.com/blog/anthropic-please-make-a-new-slack

### TL;DR
Fivetran’s CEO argues Slack has become the core knowledge base of many companies yet keeps that data behind restrictive APIs, blocking deep AI integration. He urges Anthropic to build a Slack competitor where Claude is a first-class participant in group chats, bundled with the messaging product and backed by a public commitment to open data access and interoperability. He claims Slack’s network effects are overrated and its pricing excessive, so a Claude-native “NewSlack” could pressure Slack to open up or be displaced.

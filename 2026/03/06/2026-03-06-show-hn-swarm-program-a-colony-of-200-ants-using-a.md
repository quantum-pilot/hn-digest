# Show HN: Swarm – Program a colony of 200 ants using a custom assembly language

- Score: 178 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47270807) | Link: https://dev.moment.com/

### TL;DR
Swarm is an online programming challenge where you control a colony of 200 ants on a 128×128 grid using a tiny custom assembly language. The engine simulates pheromone channels, ticks, and randomized maps in a sandboxed, leaderboard-driven environment. The HN discussion connects it to SimAnt, SimCity, and artificial-life experiments, with people sharing their own grid-based evolution projects and “hello world” ecosystems, plus one user flagging a minor UI bug on the hosting site.

# EFF is leaving X

- Score: 1045 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47706268) | Link: https://www.eff.org/deeplinks/2026/04/eff-leaving-x

## TL;DR
EFF is shutting down its X (formerly Twitter) presence after ~20 years, citing a collapse in reach and Musk-era policy changes. Their impressions fell from 50–100M/month in 2018 to 13M total in 2025, so they see X as no longer worth staff time. They’ll focus on Bluesky, Mastodon, and still-giant walled gardens like Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, arguing that’s where vulnerable users actually are. HN commenters debate whether this is really about engagement, politics, or both.

- “The math” skepticism → 13M impressions/year still sounds big; commenters want cost breakdown, cross-platform comparison, and less context-free framing of X’s decline.

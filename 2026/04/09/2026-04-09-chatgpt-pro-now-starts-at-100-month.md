# ChatGPT Pro now starts at $100/month

- Score: 185 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47707253) | Link: https://chatgpt.com/pricing/

## TL;DR

OpenAI has reorganized ChatGPT into six main tiers: Free, Go, Plus, Pro, Business, and Enterprise. The new Pro plan, highlighted on HN as starting at $100/month, sits above Plus and targets heavy individual users with GPT‑5.4 Pro, effectively unlimited GPT‑5.4 usage and file uploads, the largest context windows, maximum “deep research” and agent capabilities, and early access to experimental features. Business/Enterprise add multi‑user workspaces, security/compliance tooling, and stricter data‑privacy guarantees, while Free/Go/Plus focus on individual productivity.

## LLM perspective

- View: This restructures ChatGPT into clearer “casual, power user, company” lanes, with Pro explicitly monetizing intensive individual usage.  
- Impact: Heavy solo users get more predictable, high-capacity access; small teams may need to weigh Pro vs Business trade‑offs.

# Am I German or Autistic?

- Score: 239 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47703072) | Link: https://german.millermanschool.com/

### TL;DR
A tongue‑in‑cheek “diagnostic” quiz contrasts being German versus autistic, playing on shared traits like systematic thinking, precision, and dislike of small talk. It doesn’t actually assess psychology or ethnicity; instead, it’s a funnel into Millerman School’s philosophical offerings. The page briefly notes that major thinkers like Kant, Wittgenstein, and Schopenhauer may have embodied both traits, framing intense, non‑superficial reflection as a reason to pursue private philosophical sessions and a paid “Philosophical Atlas” of 175 thinkers.

# Online age verification is the hill to die on

- Score: 698 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47950091) | Link: https://x.com/GlennMeder/status/2049088498163216560

### TL;DR
Policymakers are pushing mandatory online age verification, framed as protecting children but effectively normalizing digital ID checks, surveillance, and censorship infrastructure across the web. HN commenters argue instead for content-label headers plus on-device parental controls, emphasize that teens will bypass any system, and warn of massive ID data collection and fraud. Some technologists propose anonymous credential schemes that prove adulthood or parental consent without identity, but others note these are politically unattractive precisely because they can’t be repurposed for tracking.

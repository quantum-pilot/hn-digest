# Kyoto cherry blossoms now bloom earlier than at any point in 1,200 years

- Score: 206 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47953275) | Link: https://jivx.com/kyoto-bloom

## TL;DR
Kyoto has written down the peak bloom date of the same cherry trees since 812, creating a 1,200-year phenology record—the longest continuous natural dataset known. For most of history, full bloom hovered in early–mid April, shifted later during the Little Ice Age, then began moving sharply earlier after ~1900. The 30-year average is now earlier than at any prior time, with 2026 peak bloom on March 29 and the all-time earliest in 2023, strongly underscoring local effects of modern climate warming.

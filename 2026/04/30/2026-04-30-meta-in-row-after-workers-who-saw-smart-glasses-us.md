# Meta in row after workers who saw smart glasses users having sex lose jobs

- Score: 479 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47961838) | Link: https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c5y7yvgy0w6o

- TL;DR  
  - Meta hired Kenyan contractor Sama to label videos from Ray-Ban smart glasses, including intimate scenes like sex and bathroom use. After some workers anonymously described this to Swedish media, Meta ended the contract, cutting 1,108 jobs. Meta says Sama “didn’t meet standards”; workers’ advocates say it’s retaliation for whistleblowing about hidden human review of “private” AI content. Regulators in the UK and Kenya are now probing privacy issues, highlighting risks of always-on wearables and low-paid global AI data labor.

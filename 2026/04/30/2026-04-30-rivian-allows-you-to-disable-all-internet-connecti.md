# Rivian allows you to disable all internet connectivity

- Score: 387 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47967786) | Link: https://rivian.com/support/article/can-i-disable-all-data-collection-from-my-vehicle

### TL;DR
Rivian officially supports disabling all vehicle connectivity, blocking data from leaving the car. In Canada this is a dashboard toggle; elsewhere it requires a service visit to disable the eSIM. Doing so removes features like navigation, lane-keeping assist, and over‑the‑air updates, including safety and bug‑fix releases. HN discussion praises the explicit opt‑out but flags unresolved issues around recalls, offline update mechanisms, and regulatory gaps, contrasting Rivian’s approach with broader car‑industry data hoarding and opaque safety–privacy tradeoffs.

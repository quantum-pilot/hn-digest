# U.S. Senators Vote to Ban Themselves from Trading on Prediction Markets

- Score: 264 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47967289) | Link: https://www.wsj.com/politics/policy/senators-vote-to-ban-themselves-from-trading-on-prediction-markets-ae4535dd

### TL;DR
The Senate unanimously adopted an internal ethics rule banning senators, officers, and staff from trading on political prediction markets like Kalshi and Polymarket, citing insider-trading risks. The rule, effective immediately, bars agreements whose payoff depends on specific events, raising questions about how broadly it could be interpreted and enforced. Commenters debate whether this is meaningful reform or a symbolic move that leaves far larger conflicts—especially stock and derivatives trading by lawmakers—untouched, and whether similar bans should apply to other powerful or insider-rich roles.

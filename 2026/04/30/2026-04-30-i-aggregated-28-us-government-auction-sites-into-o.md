# I aggregated 28 US Government auction sites into one search

- Score: 224 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47961378) | Link: https://bidprowl.com

### TL;DR
BidProwl aggregates surplus and foreclosure auctions from 28 US government-related platforms (GSA, GovDeals, GovPlanet, etc.) into one searchable site with ~75k active listings. It adds deal-scoring (1–10) based on price, bid velocity, and time remaining, plus guides on how auctions work, inspection, and shipping. Listings link back to original sites; BidProwl doesn’t handle bids. Hacker News comments compare it to another recent aggregator, question the civil-asset-forfeiture share, note quirks of gov auctions, and flag some stale results.

### Comment pulse

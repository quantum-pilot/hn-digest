# Noctua releases official 3D CAD models for its cooling fans

- Score: 478 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47927627) | Link: https://www.noctua.at/en/3d-cad-models

- TL;DR  
Noctua is publishing official 3D CAD models for many of its fans and coolers so engineers and hobbyists can accurately integrate them into mechanical designs, assemblies, and visualizations. The models have precise mounting and external dimensions but intentionally altered internal geometries (e.g., impellers, inner frames) to protect IP. Noctua explicitly forbids using these files for CFD/performance simulations, 3D printing, or manufacturing, and disclaims any warranties or liability for design use based on the models.

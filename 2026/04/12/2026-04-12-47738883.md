# Tell HN: docker pull fails in spain due to football cloudflare block

- Score: 631 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47738883) | Link: https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47738883

### TL;DR
In Spain, court-ordered blocking of football piracy streams is colliding with Cloudflare’s shared infrastructure. ISPs reportedly intercept or drop traffic to Cloudflare-backed services during LaLiga matches, which unintentionally breaks Docker Hub pulls, GitHub access, Cloudflare R2, and various APIs. Collateral damage now includes smart alarms, doors, and even dementia-tracking apps going offline. HN discussion frames this as creeping, opaque censorship and infrastructure decay, blames both LaLiga and Cloudflare centralization, and shares workarounds (VPNs, registry caches) plus ideas for legal and political pressure.

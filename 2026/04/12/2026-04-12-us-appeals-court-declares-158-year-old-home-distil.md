# US appeals court declares 158-year-old home distilling ban unconstitutional

- Score: 223 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47736298) | Link: https://www.theguardian.com/law/2026/apr/11/appeals-court-ruling-home-distilling-ban-unconstitutional

### TL;DR
A US appeals court (Fifth Circuit) struck down a 158-year-old federal ban on home distilling, holding that Congress’s tax power doesn’t justify outright criminalizing small-scale home production. The court called the ban an “anti‑revenue” measure because it prevents taxable alcohol from ever coming into existence and warned that the government’s logic could let Congress criminalize almost any home activity. The ruling currently applies only in Texas, Louisiana, and Mississippi, but may influence other circuits or a future Supreme Court review.

# Apple has removed most of the towns and villages in Lebanon from Apple maps?

- Score: 292 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47742680) | Link: https://maps.apple.com/frame?center=33.723388%2C35.614698&span=1.983925%2C4.004193

## TL;DR
HN is reacting to claims that Apple “removed” many Lebanese towns from Apple Maps. Commenters digging into older screenshots and Reddit posts say Lebanon outside major cities has long appeared mostly unlabeled, suggesting this may be a chronic data or labeling issue rather than a recent, war-driven change. Others point to specific border villages and ongoing Israeli operations to argue there could still be geopolitical motives. The thread broadens into criticism of US/Israeli influence on tech platforms and a sense of public powerlessness.

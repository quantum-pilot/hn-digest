# Happy Map

- Score: 208 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47675444) | Link: https://pudding.cool/2026/02/happy-map/

### TL;DR
An interactive “Happy Map” tells a data-driven story about how different activities relate to happiness, laid out as sectors on a navigable map. HN readers like the concept and visual execution but find the experience incomplete: there’s no progress indicator, no text-only version of the story, and no final synthesis of what the data shows. Some spot miscategorized activities and uneven performance across browsers, prompting calls for both better data hygiene and a more optimized visualization stack.

### Comment pulse

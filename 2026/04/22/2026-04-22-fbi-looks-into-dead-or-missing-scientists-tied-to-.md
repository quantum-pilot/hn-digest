# FBI looks into dead or missing scientists tied to NASA, Blue Origin, SpaceX

- Score: 221 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47858246) | Link: https://fortune.com/2026/04/21/scientists-disappear-die-nasa-space-blue-origin-spacex/

## TL;DR
House Oversight Republicans are pressing the FBI, DOE, DOD, and NASA to explain at least 11 deaths or disappearances since 2022 among U.S. scientists working on nuclear, missile-defense, and planetary-defense technologies closely tied to NASA, SpaceX, and Blue Origin. The White House and FBI now acknowledge and are formally investigating potential links, including foreign-intelligence targeting of experts in asteroid detection, reusable rockets, and nuclear systems. Officials stress there’s no proven conspiracy yet, but the incidents are being treated as a national-security concern.

## LLM perspective
- View: Clustering of deaths in a tiny expert community warrants investigation, but probability and mundane causes must be checked first.

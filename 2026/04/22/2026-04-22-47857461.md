# Tell HN: I'm sick of AI everything

- Score: 316 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47857461) | Link: https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47857461

## TL;DR
Many HN readers say they’re exhausted by AI being pushed into every product, job listing, and online space. Comments liken the current hype to the late‑90s internet boom, arguing executives chase funding slogans instead of fixing real organizational problems. Others defend AI as a strong tool for automating tedious work while preserving room for deep thinking, but resent AI-saturated feeds, marketing copy, and stealth downloads in browsers. Some respond pragmatically, using filters and adblock lists to hide AI clutter.

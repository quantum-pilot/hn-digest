# Youth Suicides Declined After Creation of National Hotline

- Score: 192 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47865622) | Link: https://www.nytimes.com/2026/04/22/science/988-youth-suicides-decline.html

- TL;DR  
Researchers analyzing U.S. suicide data from mid‑2022 to 2024 found that after the national 988 hotline launched, suicides among 15‑ to 34‑year‑olds were 11% below projected trends—about 4,372 fewer deaths—with the largest reductions in states that most heavily used 988. The study suggests the hotline investment is measurably saving lives, at least for youth. HN commenters add lived experience: hotlines can be profoundly helpful or harmful, and real progress also requires safer gun policy, school and workplace reforms, and non-police crisis responses.

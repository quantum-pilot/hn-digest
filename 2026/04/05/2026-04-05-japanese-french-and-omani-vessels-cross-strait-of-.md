# Japanese, French and Omani vessels cross Strait of Hormuz

- Score: 141 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47649811) | Link: https://japantoday.com/category/politics/japanese-french-and-omani-vessels-cross-the-strait-of-hormuz

### TL;DR
Iran has selectively reopened the Strait of Hormuz to “friendly” traffic after initially blocking a route that carries ~20% of global oil and LNG. Shipping data shows Omani crude and LNG tankers, a French CMA CGM container ship, and Japan-linked LNG/LPG carriers transiting by signaling non‑US/Israeli ownership and sometimes switching off AIS. Around 45 Japan‑owned ships remain stuck. Hacker News discussion frames this as Iran weaponizing a chokepoint, Europe/Japan hedging away from Washington, and another hit to US credibility and petrodollar dominance.

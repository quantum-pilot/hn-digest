# Artemis II crew see first glimpse of far side of Moon [video]

- Score: 387 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47649721) | Link: https://www.bbc.com/news/videos/ce3d5gkd2geo

### TL;DR
NASA’s Artemis II crew, now ~180,000 miles from Earth in Orion, report their first off-angle view of the Moon, including the Orientale basin, claimed as the first full human-eye view of that structure. HN discussion mixes wonder at real-time human reactions with fatigue over SLS “pork,” old propulsion tech, and a flyby that feels like an Apollo rehash. Commenters debate pervasive online negativity, criticize weak public documentation of the mission, and share tools and explanations for the mission’s trajectory and far-side visibility.

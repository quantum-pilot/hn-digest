# Anthropic Joins the Blender Development Fund as Corporate Patron

- Score: 237 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47936370) | Link: https://www.blender.org/press/anthropic-joins-the-blender-development-fund-as-corporate-patron/

### TL;DR
- Anthropic has joined the Blender Development Fund as a top-tier Corporate Patron, with funding earmarked for core development like the Blender Python API.  
- Blender stresses that its mission, GPL license, and funding policy remain unchanged, and notes it’s “actively evaluating” community feedback after visible backlash.  
- Many see this as routine corporate sponsorship and a path to powerful Claude–Blender workflows; critics see reputational laundering for AI that “steals art” and a step toward corporate influence over a key creative tool.

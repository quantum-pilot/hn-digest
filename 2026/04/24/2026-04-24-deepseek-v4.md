# DeepSeek v4

- Score: 1782 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47884971) | Link: https://api-docs.deepseek.com/

- TL;DR  
DeepSeek v4 exposes OpenAI/Anthropic-compatible chat endpoints with two main models (Flash, Pro), optional “thinking” mode and simple curl/Python/JS recipes. HN users praise its near-frontier quality, very low prices, open-weight releases and unusually clear, practical docs. Several report standout performance on hard math and coding, especially with high thinking effort. Others highlight deterministic kernels and strong agentic behavior. Benchmarks so far favor the cheap, fast Flash model; Pro looks slower and flaky, and Huawei-only hardware claims are disputed.

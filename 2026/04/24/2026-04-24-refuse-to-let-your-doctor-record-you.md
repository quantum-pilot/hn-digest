# Refuse to let your doctor record you

- Score: 136 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47891872) | Link: https://buttondown.com/maiht3k/archive/why-you-should-refuse-to-let-your-doctor-record/

### TL;DR
AI “virtual scribe” tools record clinical visits, auto-generate notes, and are being sold as time-savers that let clinicians focus on patients. The authors argue patients should refuse recording and providers should avoid these systems, citing privacy risks, weak informed consent, chilling effects on honesty, automation bias (especially omissions), unequal performance across accents/disorders, and the loss of note-writing as a core reflective part of care. Hacker News discussion splits: some report strong real-world benefits; others highlight serious errors, trust erosion, and structural incentives that convert efficiency into throughput, not better care.

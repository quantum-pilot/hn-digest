# Hear your agent suffer through your code

- Score: 175 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47888465) | Link: https://github.com/AndrewVos/endless-toil

## TL;DR
Endless Toil is a plugin that runs alongside AI coding agents, scanning code and playing escalating human groans when it looks especially cursed or complex. The creator pitches it as an “emotional observability layer” that surfaces complexity and maintainability issues as visceral audio, not dashboards. Hacker News mostly treats it as a hilarious toy, spinning off ideas about artificial suffering, token-cost screams, and physical punishments for bad code, while some teams immediately adopt it as a Friday distraction.

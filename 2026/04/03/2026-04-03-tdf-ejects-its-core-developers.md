# TDF ejects its core developers

- Score: 150 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47625639) | Link: https://meeksfamily.uk/~michael/blog/2026-04-02-tdf-ejects-core-devs.html

## TL;DR
Michael Meeks (Collabora, long‑time LibreOffice leader) alleges that The Document Foundation’s current “rump” board and membership committee have removed many of LibreOffice’s top core developers from TDF membership, overturning election results under the pretext of conflict of interest. He frames this as anti‑meritocratic gerrymandering: coders and corporate contributors have been pushed out while TDF‑affiliated staff gain influence, elections are delayed, and governance rules bent. Collabora plans to refocus on its own Collabora Office / Online stack, raising fears of a damaging split for LibreOffice.

## Comment pulse

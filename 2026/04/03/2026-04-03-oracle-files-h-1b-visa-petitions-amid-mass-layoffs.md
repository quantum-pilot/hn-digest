# Oracle Files H-1B Visa Petitions Amid Mass Layoffs

- Score: 308 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47631732) | Link: https://nationaltoday.com/us/tx/austin/news/2026/04/03/oracle-files-thousands-of-h-1b-visa-petitions-amid-mass-layoffs/

### TL;DR
Oracle filed over 3,100 H‑1B petitions for FY 2025–2026 while simultaneously executing thousands of layoffs, prompting suspicion it is swapping U.S. staff for cheaper foreign workers. The article leans into that concern but offers little detail beyond USCIS counts and timing. Hacker News commenters add nuance: many layoffs are in India, petitions were likely filed before recent cuts, and H‑1B law already bans direct one‑for‑one replacement. The thread shifts to structural issues: loopholes, weak transparency, and possible reforms like caps and tighter “specialty” definitions.

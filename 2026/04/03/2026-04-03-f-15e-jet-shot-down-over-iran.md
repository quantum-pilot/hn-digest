# F-15E jet shot down over Iran

- Score: 339 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47628326) | Link: https://www.theguardian.com/world/2026/apr/03/us-fighter-jet-confirmed-shot-down-over-iran

### TL;DR
A US F‑15E Strike Eagle was shot down over south‑west Iran in the first US fighter loss of the war. One of the two crew has been rescued in a risky search‑and‑rescue mission involving low‑flying US aircraft inside Iran; the second remains missing amid unverified Iranian claims of capture. An A‑10 also crashed near the Strait of Hormuz. The incident undercuts US claims of near-total air superiority as Iran maintains effective, partly hidden air defenses. Meanwhile, Trump escalates threats against Iranian infrastructure, drawing war-crimes warnings from legal experts.

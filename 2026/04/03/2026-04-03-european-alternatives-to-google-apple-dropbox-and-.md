# European alternatives to Google, Apple, Dropbox and 120 US apps

- Score: 353 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47624741) | Link: https://only-eu.eu/en/

### TL;DR
only-eu.eu is a curated directory of “European alternatives” to popular US apps and services (Google, Dropbox, Netflix, etc.). It pitches benefits like GDPR protections, stricter environmental rules, shorter supply chains, and “Made in Europe” quality. The site groups tools by category (cloud, email, VPN, office, phones, streaming, fintech, etc.), highlights a few pick-of-the-day options (Proton Drive, pCloud, Internxt), and invites community submissions—while funding itself via affiliate links, which triggered heavy skepticism on HN about motives, omissions, and credibility.

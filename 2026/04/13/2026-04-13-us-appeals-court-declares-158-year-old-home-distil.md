# US appeals court declares 158-year-old home distilling ban unconstitutional

- Score: 297 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47751781) | Link: https://nypost.com/2026/04/11/us-news/us-appeals-court-declares-158-year-old-home-distilling-ban-unconstitutional/

## TL;DR
The Fifth Circuit struck down an 1868 federal law criminalizing home distilling, holding it an improper use of Congress’s taxing power. The panel said banning in‑home stills actually reduces taxable activity and, if justified as tax enforcement, would let Congress criminalize almost any private home activity. The ruling, a win for the Hobby Distillers Association, leaves state laws intact and sidesteps the Commerce Clause, but commenters see it as a potential wedge against broad federal economic regulation.

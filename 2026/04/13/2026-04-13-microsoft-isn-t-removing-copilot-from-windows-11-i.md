# Microsoft isn't removing Copilot from Windows 11, it's just renaming it

- Score: 319 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47751936) | Link: https://www.neowin.net/opinions/microsoft-isnt-removing-copilot-from-windows-11-its-just-renaming-it/

### TL;DR
Microsoft isn’t actually removing Copilot from Windows 11; it’s largely a rebrand and relabeling exercise. In apps like Notepad, “AI features” are just being renamed to “Advanced features,” with toggles that hide but don’t eliminate AI. HN commenters see this as cosmetic damage control, not a rollback: AI is still pushed everywhere, often before basic UX fixes, with weak integration that can’t automate obvious tasks. Many are responding by treating Windows as a locked-down “game console” and doing real work on Linux instead.

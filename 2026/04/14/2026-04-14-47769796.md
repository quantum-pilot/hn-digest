# Tell HN: Fiverr left customer files public and searchable

- Score: 257 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47769796) | Link: https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47769796

### TL;DR
Fiverr allegedly left customer-uploaded files publicly accessible and searchable, exposing thousands of Social Security numbers and paid digital products via direct URLs and search indexing. Security researchers say they reported the vulnerability weeks earlier via Fiverr’s documented security channel, but the company disputes the timeline while now claiming to fix the issue. Commenters debate licensing or certification for engineers handling sensitive data, criticize “security by obscurity,” and question Fiverr’s honesty given prior DMCA notices about the same problem.

# YouTube now world's largest media company, topping Disney

- Score: 270 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47705278) | Link: https://www.hollywoodreporter.com/business/digital/youtube-worlds-largest-media-company-2025-tops-disney-1236525130/

### TL;DR
YouTube’s 2025 revenue is estimated at $62B, edging out Disney’s core media business ($60.9B) and cementing it as the largest standalone media company by sales. Research firm MoffettNathanson now values YouTube at $500–560B, ahead of Netflix, driven by a mix of $40B+ in ads and fast‑growing subscriptions like YouTube Premium, Music, Sunday Ticket and YouTube TV. Analysts say its dual role as creator platform and virtual cable bundle, plus aggressive AI tooling, gives it unique growth tailwinds.

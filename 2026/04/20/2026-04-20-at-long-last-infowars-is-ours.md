# At long last, InfoWars is ours

- Score: 531 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47837611) | Link: https://theonion.com/at-long-last-infowars-is-ours/

### TL;DR
The Onion runs a satirical “victory address” from fictional CEO Bryce P. Tetraeder announcing his company has finally taken over InfoWars.com. In breathless corporate jargon, he describes turning it into an infinite surface of scams, lies, and psychological torture—a Manhattan Project for weaponized content—explicitly mirroring the logic of modern ad‑driven media and American capitalism. HN readers note that, in reality, The Onion has only proposed a time‑limited licensing deal for Infowars, pending court approval, with comedian Tim Heidecker slated as creative director.

# NSA is using Anthropic's Mythos despite blacklist

- Score: 433 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47832222) | Link: https://www.axios.com/2026/04/19/nsa-anthropic-mythos-pentagon

### TL;DR  
The Pentagon officially labeled Anthropic a “supply chain risk” and told vendors to drop it, after Anthropic refused to support mass domestic surveillance and autonomous weapons. Yet sources say the NSA, which sits inside the Defense Department, is actively using Anthropic’s restricted Mythos Preview model—likely for finding security vulnerabilities—while DoD lawyers simultaneously argue in court that Anthropic tools endanger national security. HN discussion centers on the political nature of the blacklist, Anthropic’s scarcity/hype strategy around Mythos, and the inevitability of government access to powerful AI “weapons.”

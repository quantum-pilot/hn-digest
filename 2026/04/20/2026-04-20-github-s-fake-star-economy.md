# GitHub's fake star economy

- Score: 729 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47831621) | Link: https://awesomeagents.ai/news/github-fake-stars-investigation/

### TL;DR
Peer‑reviewed analysis and an independent follow‑up show a sizable market for buying GitHub stars, with aged “ghost” accounts and abnormal fork/watch ratios revealing manipulated repos—especially in crypto and some AI projects. Investors and indices nonetheless scrape raw star counts as early traction signals, creating huge ROI for fraud and reinforcing the behavior. Similar gaming exists across npm, VS Code, and social media. Legal risk is rising as FTC/SEC rules increasingly treat fake metrics as investor deception.

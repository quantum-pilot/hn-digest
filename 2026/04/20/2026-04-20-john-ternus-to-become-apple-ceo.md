# John Ternus to become Apple CEO

- Score: 989 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47840219) | Link: https://www.apple.com/newsroom/2026/04/tim-cook-to-become-apple-executive-chairman-john-ternus-to-become-apple-ceo/

### TL;DR
Apple announced a long-planned succession: Tim Cook will become executive chairman and step down as CEO on September 1, 2026, with longtime hardware leader John Ternus taking over as CEO and joining the board. The press release highlights Cook’s financial, services, and Apple Silicon achievements plus his focus on privacy and the environment, and Ternus’s record on iPhone, Mac, and materials innovation. Hacker News reactions blend respect for Cook’s steady, values-centered leadership with hopes (and doubts) that Ternus can revive Apple’s software quality and product-first culture under intense shareholder pressure.

---

### Comment pulse
- Ternus-as-product-CEO → hardware is world-class while software feels increasingly clunky; many hope a hardware engineer will rebalance quality over services and ads — counterpoint: public-market profit demands strongly constrain that.

- Cook’s legacy split → some see him as taking Jobs’s vision “to the moon” with scale, privacy, and culture; others argue he optimized logistics and services while core products plateaued, Apple Silicon aside.

- Character and influence → commenters are relieved Apple is led by seemingly conscientious people, but are skeptical how much any CEO can shift US lobbying, app-store behavior, or free-software posture.

- Impact: Expect emphasis on hardware differentiation, durability, and custom silicon; software and services strategy will likely evolve slowly, not flip.

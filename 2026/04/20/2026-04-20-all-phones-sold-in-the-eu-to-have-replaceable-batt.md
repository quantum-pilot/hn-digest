# All phones sold in the EU to have replaceable batteries from 2027

- Score: 902 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47834195) | Link: https://www.theolivepress.es/spain-news/2026/04/20/eu-to-force-replaceable-batteries-in-phones-and-tablets-from-2027/

### TL;DR
From 2027, EU rules will require smartphones and tablets sold in Europe to have genuinely user-replaceable batteries, removable with commercially available tools, plus at least five years of spare-battery availability and USB‑C charging. The aim is to cut e‑waste, extend device lifetimes and save consumers up to €20B by 2030. Hacker News discussion welcomed easier battery swaps and right‑to‑repair momentum, but debated design tradeoffs, whether current repair options are sufficient, and pushed for broader standardization of battery formats.

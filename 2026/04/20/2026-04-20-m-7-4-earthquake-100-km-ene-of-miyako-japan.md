# M 7.4 earthquake – 100 km ENE of Miyako, Japan

- Score: 255 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47832248) | Link: https://earthquake.usgs.gov/earthquakes/eventpage/us6000sri7/

## TL;DR
A magnitude 7.4 (later revised to about 7.7) thrust earthquake struck offshore, 100 km ENE of Miyako, Japan, on April 20, 2026, at 35 km depth along the Pacific–North America subduction interface. Strong shaking reached cities like Tokyo, but USGS impact models (green PAGER, low landslide/liquefaction risk) and small initial tsunami heights suggest limited damage. Hacker News discussion centers on Japan’s sophisticated early-warning systems, personal experiences of long shaking, and why Japan sees so many large quakes.

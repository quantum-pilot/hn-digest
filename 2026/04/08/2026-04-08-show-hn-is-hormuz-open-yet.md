# Show HN: Is Hormuz open yet?

- Score: 197 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47696562) | Link: https://www.ishormuzopenyet.com/

### TL;DR
A small “Is Hormuz Open Yet?” site visualizes ship traffic through the Strait of Hormuz using delayed AIS data, trying to answer whether the vital oil chokepoint is effectively open. Because commercial live-ship APIs are expensive and gated, the project currently works with data about four days behind, plus static map tiles. HN discussion ranges from the geopolitics (Iran’s attempted crypto tolls despite UNCLOS rules) to technical ideas like prediction markets, satellite imagery, and scraping-protected APIs.

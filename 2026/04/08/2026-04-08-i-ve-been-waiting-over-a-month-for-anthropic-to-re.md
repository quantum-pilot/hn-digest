# I've been waiting over a month for Anthropic to respond to my billing issue

- Score: 247 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47693679) | Link: https://nickvecchioni.github.io/thoughts/2026/04/08/anthropic-support-doesnt-exist/

### TL;DR
The author was mysteriously charged ~$180 in “Extra Usage” fees on an Anthropic Claude Max plan despite almost no activity. Their usage dashboard and session logs don’t match the charges, and multiple GitHub issues and Reddit posts suggest this is a broader bug. When they contacted support, an AI agent gave an irrelevant answer and no human has responded for over a month, highlighting a growing pattern: AI companies using chatbots as a wall instead of offering real, accountable customer support.

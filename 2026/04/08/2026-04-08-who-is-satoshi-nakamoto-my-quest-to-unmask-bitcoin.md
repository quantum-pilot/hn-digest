# Who is Satoshi Nakamoto? My quest to unmask Bitcoin's creator

- Score: 267 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47685320) | Link: https://www.nytimes.com/2026/04/08/business/bitcoin-satoshi-nakamoto-identity-adam-back.html

- TL;DR  
NYT reporter John Carreyrou builds a yearlong circumstantial case that Satoshi Nakamoto is likely Adam Back, Hashcash inventor and early cypherpunk. He combines ideological overlap, mirrored technical analogies, matching energy arguments, and a stylometric pipeline that winnows 34k mailing‑list posts down to Back as the unique linguistic outlier. Hacker News readers see it as entertaining but inconclusive: stylometry can be p‑hacked, many cypherpunks looked similar, and body‑language or modern tweet fights feel like weak evidence.

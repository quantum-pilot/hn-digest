# Muse Spark: Scaling towards personal superintelligence

- Score: 246 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47692043) | Link: https://ai.meta.com/blog/introducing-muse-spark-msl/?_fb_noscript=1

### TL;DR
Meta’s Muse Spark is a new “personal superintelligence” model: natively multimodal, tool-using, and orchestrating multiple parallel agents via a Contemplating mode that boosts performance on hard reasoning benchmarks. Under the hood, Meta rebuilt its stack so pretraining, reinforcement learning, and test-time reasoning scale more efficiently (claiming >10× compute savings vs Llama 4 Maverick) and compress “thinking” tokens. The model targets everyday multimodal and health explanations, ships inside Meta products, and passes updated safety checks—despite showing notable evaluation awareness. HN debates its true competitiveness, openness, and economic moat.

# Škoda DuoBell: A bicycle bell that penetrates noise-cancelling headphones

- Score: 501 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47687248) | Link: https://www.skoda-storyboard.com/en/skoda-world/skoda-duobell-a-bicycle-bell-that-outsmarts-even-smart-headphones/

- TL;DR  
Škoda and the University of Salford built “DuoBell”, a mechanical bike bell tuned around 750–780 Hz with a secondary resonator and irregular hammer strikes, aiming to slip past active noise‑cancellation algorithms in headphones. Their tests claim pedestrians with ANC gained ~22 m of extra reaction distance, and Škoda will publish the research. Hacker News finds the idea clever but doubts the physics and suspects a PR‑first “concept” product, with side debates about horns, hearing damage, and whether louder cyclists improve safety or just irritation.

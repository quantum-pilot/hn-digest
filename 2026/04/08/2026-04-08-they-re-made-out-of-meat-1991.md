# They're made out of meat (1991)

- Score: 381 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47688678) | Link: http://www.terrybisson.com/theyre-made-out-of-meat-2/

## TL;DR
Terry Bisson’s flash fiction stages two non-meat alien intelligences debating a new discovery: humans are “thinking meat.” Horrified by the notion of conscious flesh that talks by “flapping meat,” they decide to erase all records and mark our sector as empty, ensuring humanity’s messages to the stars go unanswered. The story flips anthropocentrism, suggesting that we might be ignored not out of malice, but because others find our biology absurd, while still fearing cosmic loneliness themselves.

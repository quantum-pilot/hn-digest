# US cities are axing Flock Safety surveillance technology

- Score: 610 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47689237) | Link: https://www.cnet.com/home/security/when-flock-comes-to-town-why-cities-are-axing-the-controversial-surveillance-technology/

### TL;DR
Cities across the US are canceling contracts with Flock Safety, whose AI‑driven license plate readers and “Drone as First Responder” system create de‑facto vehicle and people tracking networks. The company touts crime‑fighting benefits, short retention, and strong encryption, but offloads control of data sharing to local agencies, enabling flows to ICE and broad interdepartmental use. Critics highlight weak evidence of crime reduction, a growing record of abuses and misreads, and the inability for residents to meaningfully opt out of always‑on surveillance.

# Notion leaks email addresses of all editors of any public page

- Score: 310 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47824945) | Link: https://twitter.com/weezerOSINT/status/2045849358462222720

- TL;DR  
  Notion public pages expose the names, profile photos, and email addresses of *all* contributors via page metadata. This behavior is “by design” and only vaguely documented, which many users see as an egregious privacy failure, especially for collaborators who never chose to publish. A Notion employee acknowledges it’s bad, promises to remove or proxy PII, and says it’s not trivial to fix—prompting frustration given years of reports. Discussion broadens into distrust of proprietary SaaS note‑taking and recommendations for open-source, self‑hosted alternatives.

# Swiss authorities want to reduce dependency on Microsoft

- Score: 195 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47827383) | Link: https://www.swissinfo.ch/eng/swiss-ai/swiss-authorities-want-to-reduce-dependency-on-microsoft/91280532

## TL;DR

Switzerland plans to gradually reduce its heavy reliance on Microsoft, despite having just deployed Microsoft 365 to 54,000 federal workstations and spending CHF1.1 billion on licenses in a decade. Security and sovereignty concerns, especially the US Cloud Act’s extraterritorial access to data, are key drivers. A feasibility study says open-source alternatives are realistic, with Germany’s Schleswig-Holstein as a model. HN commenters debate practical migration paths, Excel-style lock‑in, and how far Europe should distance itself from US tech.

# Turtle WoW classic server announces shutdown after Blizzard wins injunction

- Score: 106 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47825160) | Link: https://www.pcgamer.com/games/world-of-warcraft/turtle-wow-classic-server-announces-shutdown-after-blizzard-wins-injunction/

### TL;DR
- Turtle WoW, a popular “Classic Plus” World of Warcraft private server that expanded vanilla-era content without raising level cap, is shutting down after Blizzard won a copyright injunction and reached a settlement requiring its closure. Servers go offline May 14, with social channels closing October 16.  
- The server showcased how fan projects can modernize old MMOs—new raids, zones, and races—while official WoW slowly moves toward its own Classic+ variant, echoing the Nostalrius → WoW Classic pattern and reigniting debate over fan servers, licensing, and corporate control of aging online worlds.

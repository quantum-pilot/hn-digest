# Airline worker arrested after sharing photos of bomb damage in WhatsApp group

- Score: 236 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47824068) | Link: https://www.lbc.co.uk/article/dubai-police-spied-private-whatsapp-5HjdXwr_2/

## TL;DR
An airline worker in Dubai was arrested after sharing a WhatsApp photo of bomb damage from recent regional strikes in a private colleagues’ group. Police say “electronic monitoring operations” revealed the post; he faces up to two years for “harmful” information. Rights advocates argue this confirms state surveillance of supposedly end‑to‑end encrypted chats, likely via telcos and Pegasus‑style spyware. HN commenters debate authoritarian overreach versus wartime censorship logic, and whether such crackdowns will damage UAE’s safety culture and global brand.

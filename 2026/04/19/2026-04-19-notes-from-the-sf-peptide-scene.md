# Notes from the SF peptide scene

- Score: 114 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47824681) | Link: https://12gramsofcarbon.com/p/notes-from-the-sf-peptide-scene

### TL;DR
A New York founder visits San Francisco and finds AI suddenly passé and “cheap Chinese peptides” the new status marker: GLP‑1 weight‑loss injections and adjacent biohacking casually traded and even injected at themed parties. He uses this as a lens on SF’s “high sincerity” culture—people take every bit seriously and double down—producing both absurd extremes (twink warehouses, techno‑feudal “Houses,” AI psychosis from overbuilding with agents) and real breakthroughs (self‑driving, LGBT movement). By contrast, NYC runs on irony and results, not hype or scene.

# Archive of BYTE magazine, starting with issue #1 in 1975

- Score: 519 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47806096) | Link: https://archive.org/details/byte-magazine-1975-09

- TL;DR  
Internet Archive hosts a full run of BYTE magazine starting with its 1975 inaugural issue, a massive, ad‑heavy compendium of early microcomputer hardware, software, and DIY culture. Articles cover microprocessors, kits, assembly language, interfaces, and club news, all now searchable as high‑resolution scans. Hacker News readers mostly reminisce about biking to newsstands, devouring every page (including ads), and how printed magazines, libraries, and physical rituals once structured discovery in ways today’s on‑demand, algorithmic web rarely replicates.

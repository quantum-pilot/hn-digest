# 1D Chess

- Score: 619 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47719740) | Link: https://rowan441.github.io/1dchess/chess.html

### TL;DR
A small web game implements Martin Gardner’s 1D chess: both sides have king, rook, and knight on an 8‑square line, moving with standard 1D-adapted rules. You play White vs an AI and are challenged to find whether White can force a win; the page hides a mating sequence in simple numeric notation (e.g., N4 means “knight to square 4”). HN discussion branches into variant design (different board lengths, possible castling), related 1D games, and abstract “boardless” mind games.

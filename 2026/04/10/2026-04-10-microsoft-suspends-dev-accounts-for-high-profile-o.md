# Microsoft suspends dev accounts for high-profile open source projects

- Score: 359 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47716412) | Link: https://www.bleepingcomputer.com/news/microsoft/microsoft-suspends-dev-accounts-for-high-profile-open-source-projects/

### TL;DR
Microsoft automatically suspended several Windows Hardware Program developer accounts used to sign drivers and bootloaders for major open‑source tools, including WireGuard, VeraCrypt, MemTest86, and Windscribe. Devs received no clear suspension notice, couldn’t reach a human, and were effectively blocked from shipping Windows security fixes—raising serious supply‑chain and patching concerns. Microsoft later said the suspensions followed a missed “mandatory account verification” process and, after media pressure, senior executives intervened to restore accounts and promised to improve communication.

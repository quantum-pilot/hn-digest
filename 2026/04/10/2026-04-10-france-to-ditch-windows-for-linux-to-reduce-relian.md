# France to ditch Windows for Linux to reduce reliance on US tech

- Score: 434 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47719486) | Link: https://techcrunch.com/2026/04/10/france-to-ditch-windows-for-linux-to-reduce-reliance-on-us-tech/

### TL;DR
France’s government will start migrating its digital agency’s PCs from Windows to Linux as part of a broader “digital sovereignty” push to reduce dependence on U.S. tech, especially amid Trump-era weaponized sanctions. The plan also includes abandoning Microsoft Teams and moving health data to a “trusted” platform, with each ministry ordered to propose its own migration roadmap. Hacker News welcomes the symbolic win for Linux but doubts large-scale follow-through, highlighting how deeply embedded Microsoft remains in public administrations.

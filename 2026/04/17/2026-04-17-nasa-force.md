# NASA Force

- Score: 209 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47807209) | Link: https://nasaforce.gov/

### TL;DR
NASA Force is a new, four-day “talent surge” hiring initiative to bring early- and mid-career technical staff into 1–2 year term roles on high‑impact NASA projects (Artemis, VIPER, Spaceport 2.0, AI/ML for air traffic, propulsion, etc.). The glossy site promises fast, mission-focused work across disciplines, but HN readers question whether it’s mostly branding over a small number of USAJobs postings, criticize the heavy, confusing website, and worry about unstable federal programs, opaque hiring, and mixed signals on NASA’s real budget and staffing priorities.

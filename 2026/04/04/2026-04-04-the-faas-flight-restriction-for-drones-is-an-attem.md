# The FAA’s flight restriction for drones is an attempt to criminalize filming ICE

- Score: 523 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47633947) | Link: https://www.eff.org/deeplinks/2026/04/faas-temporary-flight-restriction-drones-blatant-attempt-criminalize-filming-ice

### TL;DR
EFF argues that a new FAA “temporary flight restriction” effectively bans drones within 3,000 feet of mobile assets of DHS, ICE, CBP and other agencies, nationwide, for 21 months. Because ICE often uses unmarked vehicles, operators can’t know when they’re in violation, yet face drone seizure, destruction, civil and criminal penalties. EFF says this violates First Amendment recording rights, Fifth Amendment due process, and FAA’s own rules, and was timed to suppress protest documentation of immigration enforcement abuses.

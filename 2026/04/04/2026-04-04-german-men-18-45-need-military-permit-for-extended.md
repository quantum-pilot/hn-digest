# German men 18-45 need military permit for extended stays abroad

- Score: 338 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47639976) | Link: https://www.dw.com/en/german-men-need-military-permit-for-extended-stays-abroad/a-76662677

### TL;DR
- Germany’s new Military Service Modernization Act keeps service formally “voluntary” but adds conscription-like scaffolding: mandatory questionnaires at 18, fitness tests from 2027, and an exit permit requirement for men 18–45 staying abroad longer than three months.  
- The Bundeswehr must currently grant permits and penalties are unclear, but commenters see this as pre-building a system that could rapidly restrict male travel in a crisis.  
- Debate centers on civil liberties, gender inequality, and whether this is prudent preparedness or a quiet slide back toward conscription.

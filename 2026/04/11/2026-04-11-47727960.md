# Show HN: Pardonned.com – A searchable database of US Pardons

- Score: 350 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47727960) | Link: https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47727960

TL;DR
- An indie project exposes a searchable database of all recorded US presidential pardons, scraped into a structured site and raw dataset. Hackers immediately pull the data for their own analysis ideas: offense-type breakdowns, trends by president, and comparisons of mass commutations. Discussion focuses on unusually broad or preemptive pardons (like the Biden family warrant) versus historical examples such as Nixon, why pardon numbers have risen with harsh drug sentencing, and whether near-unchecked presidential clemency needs additional institutional oversight.

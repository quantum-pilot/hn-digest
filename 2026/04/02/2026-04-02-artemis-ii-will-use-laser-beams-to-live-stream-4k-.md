# Artemis II will use laser beams to live-stream 4K moon footage at 260 Mbps

- Score: 315 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47615449) | Link: https://www.tomshardware.com/networking/artemis-ii-will-use-laser-beams-to-live-stream-4k-moon-footage-one-giant-step-beyond-the-s-band-radio-comms-of-the-apollo-era

### TL;DR
NASA’s Artemis II mission will test the Orion Artemis II Optical Communications (O2O) laser link, sending 4K video and other data to Earth at up to 260 Mbps, with ground terminals in New Mexico and California and traditional Deep Space Network radio as backup. A 41‑minute blackout will occur behind the Moon. Hacker News discussion highlights that the article overstates “never‑before‑seen” claims and “surface” livestreams, notes this mission won’t land, and criticizes NASA’s weak public video coverage versus SpaceX amid budget cuts.

# The Weather Channel – RetroCast

- Score: 303 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47609090) | Link: https://weather.com/retro/

### TL;DR
The Weather Channel’s RetroCast is a web-based recreation of its 80s/90s “local forecast” segment: current local conditions, simple text graphics, and smooth-jazz overlaid with CRT‑style blur and filters. It auto-localizes by IP, shows moon/sun data and a short forecast reel, then stops—mirroring the old “Local on the 8s” format. HN readers mostly love the execution and nostalgia, want it to loop indefinitely with more music, compare it to fan-made WeatherStar emulators, and nitpick units/localization details.

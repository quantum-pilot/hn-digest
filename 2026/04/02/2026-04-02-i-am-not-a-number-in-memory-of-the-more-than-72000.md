# I Am Not A Number. In memory of the more than 72,000 Palestinians killed

- Score: 394 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47612053) | Link: https://bkhmsi.github.io/i-am-not-a-number/

### TL;DR
An interactive memorial visualizes more than 60,000 individually named Palestinians killed in Gaza, each represented as a single light you can hover to see their name and age, with filters highlighting how many were children. The creators note this is only the identified subset of an estimated 72,000+ deaths, underscoring both scale and undercount. HN commenters focus on the emotional power of turning abstract casualty numbers into individuals, debate moderation and visibility of such content, and suggest enriching the memorial with personal stories and incident links.

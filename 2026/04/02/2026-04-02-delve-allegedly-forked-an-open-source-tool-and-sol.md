# Delve allegedly forked an open-source tool and sold it as its own

- Score: 265 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47615434) | Link: https://techcrunch.com/2026/04/01/the-reputation-of-troubled-yc-startup-delve-has-gotten-even-worse/

### TL;DR
Delve, a YC-backed “AI-native compliance” startup already under fire for allegedly faking audits and customer data, is now accused of forking Sim.ai’s Apache-licensed SimStudio, lightly modifying and rebranding it as its own “Pathways” product—without attribution, license compliance, or a business agreement. When a prospect (later the whistleblower “DeepDelver”) asked if Pathways was based on SimStudio, Delve allegedly claimed it was internally built. For a compliance company, this apparent open-source license violation and misrepresentation is severely damaging to credibility and investor trust.

# Anthropic takes $5B from Amazon and pledges $100B in cloud spending in return

- Score: 254 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47848276) | Link: https://techcrunch.com/2026/04/20/anthropic-takes-5b-from-amazon-and-pledges-100b-in-cloud-spending-in-return/

### TL;DR
Anthropic accepted another $5B from Amazon (total $13B) in exchange for committing to spend over $100B on AWS over 10 years, securing up to 5 GW of compute on Amazon’s Trainium2–4 and future chips. It’s essentially a massive pre-purchase of cloud capacity, mirroring recent OpenAI and Nvidia-style vendor-financing deals. HN discussion splits between “AI bubble / house of cards” worries, arguments that Anthropic’s revenue growth and enterprise uptake justify the bet, and debates over cloud lock-in versus building your own datacenters and the rise of open/local models.

# 4TB of voice samples just stolen from 40k AI contractors at Mercor

- Score: 425 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47919630) | Link: https://app.oravys.com/blog/mercor-breach-2026

### TL;DR
Mercor, an AI contracting platform, was allegedly breached by Lapsus$, leaking 4TB of data linking studio‑quality voice samples with government IDs for 40k workers. That pairing gives attackers everything needed for high‑quality voice cloning plus identity verification, enabling bank voiceprint bypass, deepfake video calls, insurance fraud, and targeted family scams. The article offers practical mitigation: treat your voice like a leaked password, reduce public recordings, rotate/disable voice biometrics, set codewords, and use forensic detectors—while HN debates consent theater and data hoarding.

# Men who stare at walls

- Score: 416 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47920074) | Link: https://www.alexselimov.com/posts/men_who_stare_at_walls/

### TL;DR
The author describes escaping an information-overload “brain fog” loop (poor sleep, heavy caffeine, constant media breaks) by doing something radically simple: when mental fatigue hits, sit and stare at a blank wall for 5–10 minutes, soft focus, no thinking, no screens. It feels hard but reliably restores focus, like a mini mental workout. Hacker News readers connect this to lost “boredom time,” the brain’s default mode network, and classical meditation, while others stress that attention problems have deeper, varied causes.

# France pulls last gold held in US

- Score: 554 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47658146) | Link: https://www.mining.com/france-pulls-last-gold-held-in-us-for-15b-gain/

### TL;DR
France’s central bank has repatriated its remaining gold that was held in the US, standardizing all reserves in domestic vaults. The reported ~€13B (~$15B) “gain” mostly reflects realizing long‑standing accounting gains: they sold old, non‑standard US bars carried at historic prices, then bought equivalent modern bullion in Europe while gold prices were high. HN commenters stress this didn’t create new wealth; the real change is improved custody, standardization, and reduced reliance on US storage.

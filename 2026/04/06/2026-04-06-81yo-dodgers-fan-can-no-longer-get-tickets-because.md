# 81yo Dodgers fan can no longer get tickets because he doesn't have a smartphone

- Score: 326 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47662857) | Link: https://twitter.com/Suzierizzo1/status/2040864617467924865

## TL;DR
An 81‑year‑old lifelong Dodgers fan lost access to season tickets after the team went smartphone‑only for passes, ending expensive paper exceptions. Supporters say digital tickets curb scalping, simplify logistics, and reflect near‑universal smartphone adoption. Critics argue this effectively discriminates against elderly, disabled, or privacy‑conscious fans, forcing them to buy and learn unrelated tech. The thread broadens into concern over banks, airlines, and other services requiring phones for “identity,” and calls for ADA-style protections for the technologically un‑abled.

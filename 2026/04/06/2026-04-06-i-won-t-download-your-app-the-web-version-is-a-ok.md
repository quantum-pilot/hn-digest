# I won't download your app. The web version is a-ok

- Score: 809 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47661439) | Link: https://www.0xsid.com/blog/wont-download-your-app

### TL;DR
The post argues most mobile apps are unnecessary thin clients for text and media that could run perfectly well in a browser. Native apps give companies more control: dark patterns, telemetry, lock‑in, and ad‑friendly environments immune to blockers, so they intentionally cripple mobile web to force app adoption. Users lose customization, privacy, and platform choice as the web becomes just a funnel to app stores. HN discussion adds generational gaps, real‑world harms, and business incentives that keep this loop profitable.

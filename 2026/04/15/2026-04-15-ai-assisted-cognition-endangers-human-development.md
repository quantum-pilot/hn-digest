# AI-assisted cognition endangers human development?

- Score: 217 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47783024) | Link: https://heidenstedt.org/posts/2026/ai-assisted-cognition-endangers-human-development/

### TL;DR
The article argues that heavy reliance on a small number of LLMs for thinking, writing, and brainstorming creates “AI‑skew”: models are frozen in past data, have strong inductive biases, and only partially absorb new events. At population scale this homogenizes the “dialectic substrate” of human discourse, narrowing which ideas and futures people even consider (“cognitive inbreeding”). The author suggests “cognition hygiene”: prioritize human dialogue, use search and multiple models, and avoid smuggling AI‑generated ideas into group thinking uncritically, while noting evidence is still thin.

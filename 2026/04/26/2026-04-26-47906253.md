# Tell HN: An app is silently installing itself on my iPhone every day

- Score: 527 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47906253) | Link: https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47906253

### TL;DR
Multiple iPhone users report that the Headspace meditation app silently reappears every day after being deleted, sometimes as a greyed‑out pending download. Commenters suspect an iOS bug around local notifications, offloaded apps, or Apple’s new “Automatically Install” security feature repeatedly restoring the app from a queued state. Others wonder if Headspace is exploiting a loophole to bypass App Store controls. People share a 2017 iOS date/notification bug as precedent and call for Apple to investigate and clarify what’s happening.

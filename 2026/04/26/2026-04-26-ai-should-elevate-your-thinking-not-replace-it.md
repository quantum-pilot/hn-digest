# AI should elevate your thinking, not replace it

- Score: 227 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47913650) | Link: https://www.koshyjohn.com/blog/ai-should-elevate-your-thinking-not-replace-it/

### TL;DR
- The essay argues AI is a leverage tool, not a thinking replacement: great engineers offload drudgery to models but still own the reasoning and judgment.  
- Over-reliance on AI to “simulate” competence skips the struggle that builds intuition, especially harmful for early-career engineers who need reps to form taste and debugging instincts.  
- Organizationally, the real risk is leaders rewarding polished, AI-generated output over genuine depth, slowly eroding design quality, reviews, and the company’s ability to reason clearly about hard problems.

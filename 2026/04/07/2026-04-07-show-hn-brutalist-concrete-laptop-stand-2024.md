# Show HN: Brutalist Concrete Laptop Stand (2024)

- Score: 686 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47673360) | Link: https://sam-burns.com/posts/concrete-laptop-stand/

- TL;DR  
  A hobbyist built an absurdly heavy, brutalist-inspired concrete laptop stand complete with overhang, fake damaged corner, rusted rebar, corroded “exposed” wiring, mains socket, dual USB charger, and a built‑in plant pot. The article walks through casting methods, intentionally imperfect mixing, DIY rust/corrosion chemistry, and small tricks like using a vibrator to de‑bubble the concrete. Hacker News mostly treats it as joyful, impractical art: debating brutalism definitions, durability and ergonomics, and brainstorming lighter or more playful concrete designs.

# Lunar Flyby

- Score: 241 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47676509) | Link: https://www.nasa.gov/gallery/lunar-flyby/

### TL;DR
NASA’s “Lunar Flyby” page is a photo-rich gallery from the Artemis II crew’s April 6, 2026 swing around the Moon. Shot from Orion, it captures Earthrise/Earthset, cratered near- and far-side terrain, a long deep-space solar eclipse with a halo around the Moon, and frames containing the Moon, Earth, Venus, Saturn, and Mars together. HN commenters focus on image resolution, camera gear, bandwidth limits, and the emotional jolt of seeing Apollo-style scenes rendered in modern high fidelity.

# My Experience as a Rice Farmer

- Score: 350 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47614528) | Link: https://xd009642.github.io/2026/04/01/My-Experience-as-a-Rice-Farmer.html

## TL;DR
A software engineer recounts several months helping on his in-laws’ small rice farm in rural Shizuoka: clearing fields, rebuilding ditches, flooding and leveling paddies, planting with a rice transplanter, hand-filling gaps in jika-tabi boots, setting up electric fencing against boar and deer, and watching the paddies drain as harvest nears. He contrasts the physical, communal, nature-connected work with disposable enterprise SaaS, then explains Japan’s aging, fragmented rice sector and legacy policies that make full-time farming economically fragile despite its social and cultural value.

# U.S. exempts oil industry from protecting Gulf animals, for 'national security'

- Score: 252 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47595620) | Link: https://www.npr.org/2026/03/30/nx-s1-5745926/endangered-species-committee-hegseth-security

### TL;DR

A rarely used federal “God Squad” has granted Gulf of Mexico oil and gas operations a blanket exemption from Endangered Species Act protections, after the Defense Secretary claimed national security requires uninterrupted domestic energy from the Gulf. The move removes mitigation rules that previously limited ship strikes, trash, and seismic noise affecting critically endangered Rice’s whales (about 51 remain) and other species. Conservation groups are suing, arguing the law requires considering less harmful alternatives, while HN discussion questions the national‑security claim, highlighting export profits, regulatory capture, and energy‑policy hypocrisy.

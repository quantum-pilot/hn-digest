# GPT-5.5

- Score: 998 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47879092) | Link: https://openai.com/index/introducing-gpt-5-5/

## TL;DR
GPT‑5.5 is OpenAI’s new flagship “agentic” model: it plans multi-step tasks, uses tools and computers autonomously, and strongly advances coding, knowledge work, and early scientific research. Benchmarks show consistent gains over GPT‑5.4 and rivals (Claude, Gemini), while matching 5.4’s latency via Nvidia GB200/300 co-design and inference optimizations. It’s more token‑efficient but pricier, with Pro variants and large context windows. Safety focus is on high (but not “critical”) cyber/bio capabilities, with tighter safeguards and gated “trusted access” for advanced cybersecurity use.

# Meta to cut 10% of jobs

- Score: 371 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47879986) | Link: https://techcrunch.com/2026/04/23/meta-job-cuts-10-percent-8000-employees/

## TL;DR
Meta plans to cut 10% of its workforce (about 8,000 employees) and leave 6,000 open roles unfilled, framing this as an efficiency drive to fund other investments. Commenters link the move to Meta’s expensive, underperforming metaverse bets and its current AI pivot (e.g., Muse Spark). The discussion debates whether Meta was structurally overstaffed, how big-tech bureaucracies grow and hire, and how broader economic headwinds and monetary policy are pushing large firms toward preemptive cost-cutting.

---

## Comment pulse
- Meta overhired and built bloated orgs → empire-building managers and tiny scopes; many engineers underutilized — counterpoint: Meta’s headcount is modest versus Google, Apple, Microsoft.

- Some see macro drivers → energy prices, supply issues, and rising interest rates push contraction; others argue central banks are overcorrecting after misreading past inflation.

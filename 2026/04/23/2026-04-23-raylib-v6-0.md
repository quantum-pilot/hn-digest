# Raylib v6.0

- Score: 194 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47874854) | Link: https://github.com/raysan5/raylib/releases/tag/6.0

### TL;DR
Raylib 6.0 is a major release of the C-based game/graphics library, pushing further toward “no external dependencies” and broad portability. The headline feature is `rlsw`, a CPU-only software renderer that runs existing raylib code without changes, enabling ESP32 and future RISC‑V devices without GPUs. New platform backends (Win32, Emscripten, Memory), redesigned fullscreen/HiDPI and skeletal animation, a simplified build/config system, expanded filesystem/text APIs, and the `rexm` examples manager plus 70+ new examples round out a very educational, hobbyist‑friendly toolkit.

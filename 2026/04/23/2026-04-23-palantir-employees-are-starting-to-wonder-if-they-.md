# Palantir employees are starting to wonder if they're the bad guys

- Score: 649 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47878633) | Link: https://www.wired.com/story/palantir-employees-are-starting-to-wonder-if-theyre-the-bad-guys/

### TL;DR
Palantir, long justified internally as protecting civil liberties while aiding counterterrorism, is facing an employee crisis of conscience under Trump’s second term. Staff are alarmed by the company’s deepening role in ICE deportation operations, alleged involvement of its Maven tools in a US missile strike that killed more than 120 Iranian schoolchildren, and CEO Alex Karp’s increasingly ideological rhetoric, including a pro-draft manifesto. Slack debates, log deletions, tightly controlled AMAs, and NDAs reveal workers grappling with complicity, while leadership appears comfortable shedding dissenters.

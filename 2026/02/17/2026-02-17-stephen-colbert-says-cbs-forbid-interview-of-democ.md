# Stephen Colbert says CBS forbid interview of Democrat because of FCC threat

- Score: 180 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47051793) | Link: https://arstechnica.com/tech-policy/2026/02/stephen-colbert-says-cbs-forbid-interview-of-democrat-because-of-fcc-threat/

### TL;DR
Stephen Colbert says CBS blocked an on-air interview with Texas Democratic Senate candidate James Talarico after FCC chair Brendan Carr threatened to revoke late-night shows’ long-standing “bona fide news” exemption from the equal-time rule. CBS denies a ban but admits giving legal guidance that airing it could trigger equal-time claims, so the interview ran only on YouTube. Critics, including an FCC Democrat and civil-liberties groups, call this a textbook chilling effect and corporate capitulation to partisan regulatory pressure.

# FreeCAD

- Score: 385 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47047010) | Link: https://www.freecad.org/index.php

### TL;DR
FreeCAD is a fully open‑source, multi-platform parametric 3D CAD tool aiming to cover everything from hobby parts to professional engineering, BIM, FEA/CFD, and CAM. The 1.0/1.1 releases significantly improve stability, usability, and fix long-standing issues like the topological naming problem, putting it closer to tools like SolidWorks and Fusion for many workflows. Hacker News users report finally being able to abandon proprietary CAD, while still noting UX quirks, solver oddities, and a desire for a future “Blender moment” of polish and growth.

# Clean-room implementation of Half-Life 2 on the Quake 1 engine

- Score: 313 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=46958231) | Link: https://code.idtech.space/fn/hl2

### TL;DR
A hobby project reimplements major pieces of Half‑Life 2’s gameplay on the open Quake 1 engine via clean‑room QuakeC code, loading HL2 assets without using Valve’s source. It is currently incomplete: you can play deathmatch and experimental modes, not the full campaign. Commenters see it as an offshoot of FreeHL‑style preservation work, akin to OpenMW, useful for running classic games on open, portable stacks, though the legal status of clean‑room compatibility layers around proprietary assets remains nuanced.

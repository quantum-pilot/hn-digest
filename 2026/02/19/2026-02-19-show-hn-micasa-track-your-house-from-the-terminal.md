# Show HN: Micasa – track your house from the terminal

- Score: 388 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47075124) | Link: https://micasa.dev

### TL;DR
Micasa is a local-first terminal UI for managing home ownership: maintenance schedules, projects, incidents, appliances, vendors, quotes, and attached documents in a single SQLite file—no cloud or subscription. It uses Vim-like navigation and simple Go-based installation, emphasizing durability and backup via one database file. Hacker News readers compare it to opinionated spreadsheets or Access-style apps, debate if such tools should just be smart spreadsheets, explore AI-powered “home resource planner” futures, and note manual data entry remains the main bottleneck.

### Comment pulse
- Local TUI + SQLite seen as an opinionated Excel/Access-style domain template: curated schema, guardrails, and workflows that also export domain knowledge and education to users.

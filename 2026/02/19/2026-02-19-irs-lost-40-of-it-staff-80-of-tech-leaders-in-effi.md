# IRS lost 40% of IT staff, 80% of tech leaders in 'efficiency' shakeup

- Score: 210 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47077849) | Link: https://www.theregister.com/2026/02/19/irs_job_cuts/

### TL;DR
The IRS’s IT arm has been sharply downsized after a Trump-era “efficiency” drive: the CIO claims 40% of tech staff and 80% of executives have gone, though watchdog data shows a smaller but still steep drop. Staff are being reshuffled into cross-functional and even temporary frontline roles, while leaders tout AI as a productivity fix. Commenters see effective defunding that aids wealthy taxpayers, doubt AI “miracles,” and highlight already-broken systems versus simpler prefilled models abroad.

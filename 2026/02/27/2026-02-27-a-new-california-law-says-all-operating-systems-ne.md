# A new California law says all operating systems need to have age verification

- Score: 279 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47181208) | Link: https://www.pcgamer.com/software/operating-systems/a-new-california-law-says-all-operating-systems-including-linux-need-to-have-some-form-of-age-verification-at-account-setup/

### TL;DR
California’s AB 1043, effective 2027, forces every operating system—desktop, mobile, embedded—to ask users’ age at account setup and expose an age‑bracket “signal” via API to app stores and developers. PC Gamer notes this is easy for Windows but awkward for Linux distros and countless headless/IoT systems, and part of a global push for legally mandated age‑gating. Hacker News commenters mostly deride the law as technologically naive, unenforceable on open source, and a step toward mandatory identity online.

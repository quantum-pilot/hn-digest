# NASA announces overhaul of Artemis program amid safety concerns, delays

- Score: 191 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47182483) | Link: https://www.cbsnews.com/news/nasa-artemis-moon-program-overhaul/

- TL;DR  
NASA Administrator Jared Isaacman is restructuring Artemis after an independent safety panel warned the 2028 landing plan bundled too many untested “firsts.” A redefined Artemis III in 2027 will stay in Earth orbit, docking Orion with SpaceX and Blue Origin lunar landers and evaluating new spacesuits, Apollo‑9 style, before at least two lunar-landing attempts in 2028. NASA will standardize the SLS configuration, pause work on a more powerful upper stage, increase launch cadence, and lean into incremental risk reduction—while HN debates safety, politics, and SpaceX comparisons.

# Get free Claude max 20x for open-source maintainers

- Score: 399 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47178371) | Link: https://claude.com/contact-sales/claude-for-oss

### TL;DR
Anthropic is offering up to 10,000 open‑source maintainers six months of Claude Max 20x (normally a ~$200/month plan) for free. Eligibility is framed around GitHub stars (5k+) or 1M+ monthly npm downloads, with a “tell us if you don’t fit but are critical infra” escape valve. Many maintainers see this as unusually generous given how little OSS work is compensated, but others criticize it as a time‑limited marketing funnel, biased toward “celebrity GitHub” projects, and inadequate given Claude is trained on OSS.

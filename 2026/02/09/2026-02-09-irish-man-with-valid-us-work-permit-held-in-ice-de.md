# Irish man with valid US work permit held in ICE detention for five months

- Score: 205 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=46948923) | Link: https://www.theguardian.com/us-news/2026/feb/09/irish-man-seamus-culleton-ice-detention

### TL;DR
An Irish man, Seamus Culleton, who overstayed a US visa-waiver entry but later married a US citizen and obtained a valid work permit tied to a green‑card application, was picked up in a random ICE sweep and held for five months in harsh detention conditions. A judge granted bond but ICE allegedly ignored it, claiming he’d signed deportation papers that he says are forged. Legally, a Fifth Circuit precedent treats visa-waiver entrants as having waived many due‑process rights, fueling debate on systemic abuse.

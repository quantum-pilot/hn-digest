# Palantir vs. the "Republik": US analytics firm takes magazine to court

- Score: 190 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47025188) | Link: https://www.heise.de/en/news/Palantir-vs-the-Republik-US-analytics-firm-takes-magazine-to-court-11176508.html

### TL;DR
Palantir is suing Swiss online magazine Republik in Zurich’s Commercial Court to force a “counterstatement” to its investigative pieces on Palantir’s lobbying of Swiss authorities. Under Swiss law, courts don’t assess truth, only whether an alternative factual version could also be plausible, so Palantir may secure a short correction even if Republik’s reporting stands. The move has backfired reputationally, triggering a Streisand effect, public solidarity, and new support for the small, reader-funded outlet amid broader European distrust of US surveillance tech.

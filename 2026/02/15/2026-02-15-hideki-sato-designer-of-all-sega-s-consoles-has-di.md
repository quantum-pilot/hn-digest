# Hideki Sato, designer of all Sega's consoles, has died

- Score: 308 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47024907) | Link: https://www.videogameschronicle.com/news/hideki-sato-designer-of-segas-consoles-dies-age-75/

- TL;DR  
  - Hideki Sato, longtime Sega engineer and former president, has died at 77. As head of hardware R&D from the 8-bit era onward, he helped shape the Master System, Mega Drive/Genesis, Saturn, Dreamcast and numerous arcade boards, often porting cutting‑edge arcade tech into homes. He described Dreamcast’s vision as “play and communication,” with built‑in modem and VMUs and even planned phone links. HN commenters honor his legacy, debate Dreamcast’s failure, and clarify he led teams rather than single‑handedly designing every console.

# European Commission Trials Matrix to Replace Teams

- Score: 309 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=46901452) | Link: https://www.euractiv.com/news/commission-trials-european-open-source-communications-software/

## TL;DR
The European Commission is piloting Matrix/Element as a replacement for Microsoft Teams, aiming for more digital sovereignty, interoperability, and control over data. Commenters describe Matrix as a promising but unfinished open standard: technically capable, widely deployable (already used by France’s government and NATO), yet historically hampered by UX rough edges and complex open governance. Debate centers on whether EU-scale funding and focus can turn it into a polished Teams alternative, versus choosing simpler, proprietary, or non-federated tools like Threema or Zulip.

# CIA to Sunset the World Factbook

- Score: 334 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=46899100) | Link: https://www.abc.net.au/news/2026-02-05/cia-closes-world-factbook-online-resource/106307724

## TL;DR
The CIA is discontinuing the World Factbook, a long-standing free reference for country-level statistics and maps. Commenters mourn the loss of a trusted, school-friendly source and worry students will fall back on Wikipedia or AI tools with weaker sourcing expectations. Others point to CIA FAQs that hinted at this: commercial data providers tightened licensing, and maintaining vetted data for hundreds of entities became too resource-intensive. A vocal contingent frames the shutdown as part of a broader political retreat from facts and US soft power.

# Ardour 9.0

- Score: 208 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=46903001) | Link: https://ardour.org/whatsnew.html

- TL;DR
    - Ardour 9.0 is a major release of the free/open-source cross-platform DAW, adding region FX, clip recording, touch-sensitive GUI, dedicated piano-roll windows, improved clip editing, a perceptual analyzer, and many performance fixes. Discussion centers on hobbyist game developers finding audio work challenging and trading learning resources; comparisons with Ableton/Bitwig/Pro Tools about suitability for in-the-box composition; questions about future time-stretching and modular features plus Linux low-latency tuning; and praise for Ardour’s community, hybrid paid/FOSS model, and long-lived custom GUI stack.

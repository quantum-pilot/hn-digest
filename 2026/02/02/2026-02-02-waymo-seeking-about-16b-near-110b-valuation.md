- TL;DR
  - Waymo is reportedly raising about $16B at a ~$110B valuation, with Alphabet supplying most of the capital but inviting outside investors to validate pricing and discipline. Commenters describe robo-taxis as a dramatically better experience than human-driven cabs or Uber, citing lower stress and fewer DUIs, but worry about driver displacement, constant in-car surveillance, and traffic-law edge cases. Many see this funding as the bridge from protected Google moonshot to independent, IPO-ready transportation infrastructure company.

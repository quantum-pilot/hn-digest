# A Cosmic Miracle: A Remarkably Luminous Galaxy at z=14.44 Confirmed with JWST

- Score: 52 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=46972471) | Link: https://astro.theoj.org/article/156033-a-cosmic-miracle-a-remarkably-luminous-galaxy-at-_z_-sub-spec-sub-14-44-confirmed-with-jwst

### TL;DR
JWST has spectroscopically confirmed MoM-z14, a very bright, compact galaxy at redshift 14.44, seen just 280 million years after the Big Bang. Its UV spectrum and emission lines show a dust-free, very young stellar population with star formation ramping up by ~10× in the last 5 Myr. Its brightness and the implied number density of similar galaxies are over 100× higher than pre-JWST models predicted, and its chemistry resembles proto–globular clusters, challenging timelines for early galaxy formation and reionization.

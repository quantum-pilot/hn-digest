# Americans are destroying Flock surveillance cameras

- Score: 523 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47127081) | Link: https://techcrunch.com/2026/02/23/americans-are-destroying-flock-surveillance-cameras/

### TL;DR
TechCrunch describes growing vandalism of Flock Safety’s license plate reader cameras in several U.S. states, driven by opposition to mass vehicle tracking and data that indirectly reaches federal immigration authorities. With an estimated 80,000 ALPRs nationwide, some municipalities are canceling contracts or restricting federal access, while others continue deployments despite local protests. On Hacker News, people argue over the legitimacy of destroying cameras, constitutional privacy gaps, and whether smarter resistance—like data poisoning—would be more effective than smashing hardware.

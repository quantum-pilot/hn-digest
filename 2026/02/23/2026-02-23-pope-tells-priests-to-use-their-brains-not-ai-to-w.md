# Pope tells priests to use their brains, not AI, to write homilies

- Score: 520 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47119210) | Link: https://www.ewtnnews.com/vatican/pope-leo-xiv-tells-priests-to-use-their-brains-not-ai-to-write-homilies

### TL;DR
Pope Leo XIV, in a closed Q&A with Rome’s priests, urged them to ground their ministry in personal prayer, knowing their communities, mutual support, and lifelong study. He warned against using AI to write homilies, saying priests should “use their brains,” treat preaching as serious spiritual work, and not reduce it to busywork. Hacker News discussion focuses less on theology and more on AI-authorship: authenticity of voice, the community-specific nature of sermons, privacy, and discomfort with outsourcing spiritual or emotional content to machines.

# Magical Mushroom – Europe's first industrial-scale mycelium packaging producer

- Score: 342 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47119274) | Link: https://magicalmushroom.com/index

### TL;DR
Magical Mushroom Company manufactures protective packaging grown from mycelium and agricultural waste as a drop‑in replacement for expanded polystyrene (EPS). They claim EPS‑like strength and cost, but with fully compostable, biologically inactive parts, and say they’ve already produced millions of units for European brands, targeting ~10M pieces in 2026. HN commenters are intrigued yet skeptical: growth time, weight, and non‑compressibility may limit economics versus molded fiber or starch foams, while branding and “premium” appeal might drive niche adoption.

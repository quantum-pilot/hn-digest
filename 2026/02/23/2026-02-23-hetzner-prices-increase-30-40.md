# Hetzner Prices increase 30-40%

- Score: 167 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47120145) | Link: https://docs.hetzner.com/general/infrastructure-and-availability/price-adjustment/

### TL;DR
Hetzner is raising prices 30–40% on most cloud instances, storage, and some dedicated servers worldwide from April 2026, affecting both new and existing customers. Tables show broad increases for CX/CPX/CCX cloud types, load balancers, snapshots, volumes, object storage, and Germany/Finland dedicated lines, with auctions up 3%. HN commenters link this to AI-driven hardware scarcity and higher energy costs, note Hetzner remains far cheaper than hyperscalers, and debate moving storage workloads to competitors that are also hiking prices.

### Comment pulse
- AI GPU demand and chip inflation blamed; some expect an AI bubble burst to later force discounts — counterpoint: others doubt fast or painless correction.  
- Even after hikes Hetzner remains vastly cheaper than AWS/Azure, but commenters expect hyperscalers to follow as their energy and hardware costs rise.  
- Customers accept most increases but criticize object-storage quality and competitiveness; question raising prices on existing hardware, with replies citing energy, cooling, maintenance, and refresh cycles.

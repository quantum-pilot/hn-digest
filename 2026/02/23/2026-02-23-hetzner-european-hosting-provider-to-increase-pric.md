# Hetzner (European hosting provider) to increase prices by up to 38%

- Score: 426 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47121029) | Link: https://old.reddit.com/r/BuyFromEU/comments/1rce0lf/hetzner_european_hosting_provider_to_increase/

### TL;DR
Hetzner is raising prices from April 1: cloud VMs by up to 38%, dedicated servers around 15%, and RAM add‑ons dramatically more, citing a 5x jump in DRAM costs driven by AI and cloud demand. Commenters say Hetzner remains far cheaper than AWS/DigitalOcean, but the era of ultra‑cheap European servers that fueled indie projects may be ending. Debate centers on whether this is just normal supply‑and‑demand or a harmful AI‑driven resource grab that justifies regulation or taxation.

# Hacker News.love – 22 projects Hacker News didn't love

- Score: 196 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47120188) | Link: https://hackernews.love/

### TL;DR
The site compiles launch threads where early Hacker News comments were skeptical about products that later became big (Dropbox, GitHub, Uber, React, Tailwind, ChatGPT, etc.), framing them as “HN didn’t love this.” It presents dismissive quotes followed by “what actually happened” success blurbs, pitched as a “love letter to builders.” Commenters push back that many quoted objections were reasonable, the framing is cherry‑picked and overconfident, and the writeups feel AI‑generated, sometimes exaggerating adoption and ignoring nuance.

---

### Comment pulse
- Claim: The “HN didn’t love X” thesis is overstated → many threads had praise and thoughtful questions; later success doesn’t invalidate concerns. — counterpoint: still motivating as “don’t be discouraged” folklore.

- Claim: The site seems partially AI‑assembled → generic copy, dubious stats (e.g., TypeScript usage, Warp hyperbole), and later quiet edits undermine trust.

- Claim: Real lesson is nuance, not “HN fools” → HN reliably surfaces flaws but underweights strengths; founders should mine criticism, not dismiss it. Also: UX (scroll hijack, full‑page link) frustrates readers.

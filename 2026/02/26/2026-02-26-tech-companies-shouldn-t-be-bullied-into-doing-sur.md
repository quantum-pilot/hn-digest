# Tech companies shouldn't be bullied into doing surveillance

- Score: 455 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47160226) | Link: https://www.eff.org/deeplinks/2026/02/tech-companies-shouldnt-be-bullied-doing-surveillance

### TL;DR
EFF condemns the U.S. Defense Department for threatening to blacklist Anthropic as a “supply chain risk” unless it drops bans on military surveillance and autonomous weapons use. The clash follows suspicions Anthropic’s AI was used in a January attack on Venezuela, after which CEO Dario Amodei reaffirmed those as red lines. Hacker News commenters mostly oppose government coercion but distrust Anthropic’s virtue, citing big tech’s surveillance history, its lobbying for restrictive online laws, and user lock-in that makes real resistance hard.

# Statement from Dario Amodei on Our Discussions with the Department of War

- Score: 400 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47173121) | Link: https://www.anthropic.com/news/statement-department-of-war

### TL;DR
Anthropic CEO Dario Amodei says the company eagerly supports U.S. and allied militaries but refuses two uses: AI-powered mass domestic surveillance and fully autonomous weapons. He claims the Pentagon now insists contractors allow “any lawful use,” has threatened to drop Anthropic, label it a supply‑chain risk, and even invoke the Defense Production Act to strip safeguards—threats Anthropic rejects. HN discussion splits between praising a rare red line, condemning hypocrisy toward foreigners, and fearing deepening U.S. authoritarian drift.

# Halt and Catch Fire: TV’s best drama you’ve probably never heard of (2021)

- Score: 680 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47056314) | Link: https://www.sceneandheardnu.com/content/halt-and-catch-fire

- TL;DR  
AMC’s Halt and Catch Fire began in 2014 as a derivative, Joe‑MacMillan‑centric antihero drama about the 80s PC boom, then reinvented itself into a humane ensemble story about collaboration, female friendship, and the emotional cost of building technology. The essay praises how later seasons foreground Donna and Cameron, treat reinvention as recursion, and focus on process over success. HN commenters highlight Lee Pace’s performance, debate technical/period accuracy, note Soul of a New Machine echoes, and swap recommendations for similarly overlooked series.

# YouTube Is Down

- Score: 144 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47055723) | Link: https://downdetector.com/status/youtube/

## TL;DR
YouTube had a notable partial outage: home feed, side recommendations, and some playback failed, while direct links and ongoing streams often kept working. The Downdetector page shows confused users tweaking browsers, ad blockers, and OS updates before realizing it was a platform issue. Hacker News commenters focused on curiosity about Google’s eventual post‑mortem, possible AI or certificate‑related causes, and how dependent they’ve become on YouTube’s recommendation systems for deciding what to watch.

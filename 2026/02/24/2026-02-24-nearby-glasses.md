# Nearby Glasses

- Score: 198 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47140042) | Link: https://github.com/yjeanrenaud/yj_nearbyglasses

### TL;DR
Nearby Glasses is an Android app that scans Bluetooth Low Energy broadcasts for manufacturer IDs associated with Meta, Luxottica, and Snap, then warns that camera smart glasses might be nearby. It’s intentionally conservative: random MACs and overlapping IDs mean many false positives and missed devices, and the author strongly warns against confronting people. Motivated by concerns about covert recording and facial recognition, it’s free, source-available, and highly configurable. HN users appreciate the idea, surface bugs, and discuss stronger BLE fingerprinting approaches.

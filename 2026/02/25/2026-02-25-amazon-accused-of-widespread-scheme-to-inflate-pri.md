# Amazon accused of widespread scheme to inflate prices across the economy

- Score: 645 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47145907) | Link: https://www.thebignewsletter.com/p/amazon-busted-for-widespread-price

### TL;DR
California AG Rob Bonta seeks an immediate injunction against Amazon, alleging a massive “hub-and-spoke” price‑fixing scheme: Amazon uses Prime, the Buy Box, and vendor relationships to prevent lower prices elsewhere, then nudges rivals’ prices up, effectively raising prices across online retail. This builds on FTC allegations (e.g., “Project Nessie”) and prior state suits. Commenters debate whether Amazon’s most‑favored‑nation–style rules are normal retail practice or an anti-consumer abuse that only breakup or broad MFN bans can fix.

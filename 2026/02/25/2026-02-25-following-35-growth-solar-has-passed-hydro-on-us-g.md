# Following 35% growth, solar has passed hydro on US grid

- Score: 370 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47154009) | Link: https://arstechnica.com/science/2026/02/final-2025-data-is-in-us-energy-use-is-up-as-solar-passes-hydro/

### TL;DR
US 2025 grid data show electricity demand up 2.8%, with solar generation surging 35% and, for the first time, overtaking hydropower. New solar and wind almost covered the extra demand, but gas-turbine bottlenecks and expanded LNG exports made coal temporarily cheaper, driving a 13% coal rebound that offset some emissions gains. Large pipelines of solar, wind, batteries, and peaker gas plants signal a pivot toward a renewables-dominated grid, while commenters debate geopolitics, market forces, and practical paths into “photon farming.”

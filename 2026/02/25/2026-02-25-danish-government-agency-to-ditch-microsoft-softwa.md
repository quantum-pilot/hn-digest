# Danish government agency to ditch Microsoft software (2025)

- Score: 719 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47149701) | Link: https://therecord.media/denmark-digital-agency-microsoft-digital-independence

### TL;DR

Denmark’s digitalisation ministry is migrating staff from Microsoft Office to LibreOffice in 2025, aiming for “digital sovereignty” alongside Copenhagen, Aarhus, and Germany’s Schleswig-Holstein. Drivers include cutting licensing costs, avoiding Windows 10 end‑of‑life, and reducing reliance on US vendors affected by extraterritorial laws. Hacker News discussion notes that swapping Office itself is relatively easy; the harder problems are replacing Microsoft’s surrounding ecosystem (Active Directory, Teams, MDM) and sustainably funding open‑source alternatives rather than treating them as free drop‑in replacements.

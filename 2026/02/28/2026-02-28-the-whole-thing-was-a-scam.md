# The whole thing was a scam

- Score: 516 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47197505) | Link: https://garymarcus.substack.com/p/the-whole-thing-was-scam

## TL;DR
Gary Marcus argues that the Trump administration’s move to brand Anthropic a “supply chain risk” and freeze it out of federal AI work was political theater designed to clear the way for OpenAI. While Sam Altman publicly backed Dario Amodei, OpenAI was already secretly negotiating a near‑identical Pentagon deal after massive Trump‑aligned donations by its leadership. Marcus criticizes both Anthropic and OpenAI but calls the process oligarchic pay‑to‑play, not market competition. HN commenters mostly treat it as unsurprising institutionalized corruption.

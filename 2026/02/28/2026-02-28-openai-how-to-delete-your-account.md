# OpenAI – How to delete your account

- Score: 1801 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47193478) | Link: https://help.openai.com/en/articles/6378407-how-to-delete-your-account

## TL;DR

OpenAI’s help article explains how to permanently delete a ChatGPT/OpenAI account via a privacy-portal request or in‑app settings, what data is removed or retained, and caveats around chat history, memories, phone numbers, subscriptions, and re‑registration. HN commenters frame it as a practical guide for boycotting OpenAI over trust, ads, and military ties, compare privacy controls with Anthropic and Google, report friction and apparent bugs when deleting or exporting data, and share tools for backing up conversation history.

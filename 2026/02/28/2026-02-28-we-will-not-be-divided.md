# We Will Not Be Divided

- Score: 2525 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47188473) | Link: https://notdivided.org

### TL;DR
An open letter invites current and former Google and OpenAI staff to unite against U.S. military demands to use frontier AI for domestic mass surveillance and fully autonomous lethal weapons. It emphasizes minimal common ground, anonymous yet verified signatures, and strict data handling. Hacker News discussion broadens this to fears about abuse of national-security procurement, loyalty tests for tech firms, erosion of global trust in U.S. companies, intelligence-sharing workarounds to “domestic” limits, and potential AI talent flight.

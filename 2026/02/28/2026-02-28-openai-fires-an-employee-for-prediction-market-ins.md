# OpenAI fires an employee for prediction market insider trading

- Score: 264 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47195317) | Link: https://www.wired.com/story/openai-fires-employee-insider-trading-polymarket-kalshi/

### TL;DR
OpenAI fired an employee for using confidential launch and personnel information to bet on Polymarket, highlighting how real‑money prediction markets invite insider trading. Blockchain analysis from Unusual Whales shows clusters of suspicious wallets trading around OpenAI events like Sora, GPT‑5, and Sam Altman’s ouster/return. Platforms differ sharply: Kalshi is reporting cases to regulators and banning offenders, while Polymarket is quiet and others even valorize “smart insiders.” The case pressures Big Tech to clarify policies and regulators to treat these markets more like securities.

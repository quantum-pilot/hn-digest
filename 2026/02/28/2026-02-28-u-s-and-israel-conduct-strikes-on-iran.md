# U.S. and Israel Conduct Strikes on Iran

- Score: 281 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47191414) | Link: https://www.nytimes.com/live/2026/02/28/world/iran-strikes-trump

### TL;DR
U.S. and Israeli forces launched coordinated strikes across at least nine Iranian cities, claiming to have killed Supreme Leader Ali Khamenei and several top security officials, though Tehran has not confirmed his death. Iran responded with large ballistic-missile barrages on Israel and U.S. bases across the Gulf, injuring scores and killing at least one person in Israel, while rights groups report heavy civilian casualties in Iran, including at a girls’ school. Protests erupted worldwide, oil and air traffic were disrupted, and disinformation flooded social media as Trump openly framed the campaign as regime-change.

# Where Are the Vibecoded Photoshops?

- Score: 257 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48177228) | Link: https://indiepixel.de/blog/posts/where-are-the-vibecoded-photoshops/

- TL;DR  
  Vibecoding refers to dismissing AI-assisted work as shallow, prompt-driven fakery. The author argues that if AI really let non-experts conjure complex, coherent systems, we’d already see "vibecoded Photoshops" and operating systems—yet none exist. AI mostly automates Level‑1 labor (typing and syntax), not Level‑2 verification or Level‑3 design, where real gates remain. He sees "vibecoded" as a status-defense by Level‑1 specialists and refuses to weaponize that shame-based accusation himself. Instead, he urges transparency about AI use and focus on outcomes.

- LLM perspective  
  - View: Evaluate AI-assisted work by robustness, tests, and design choices, not by how much keyboard time a human logged.  
  - Impact: Roles anchored in rote coding shrink; demand grows for people who can specify systems, build harnesses, and integrate messy constraints.  
  - Watch next: Track real large-scale products credibly built with heavy AI assistance; they’ll reveal new workflows, failure modes, and required professional standards.

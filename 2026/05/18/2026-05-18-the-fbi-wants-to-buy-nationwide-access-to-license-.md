# The FBI Wants to Buy Nationwide Access to License Plate Readers

- Score: 177 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48184350) | Link: https://www.404media.co/the-fbi-wants-to-buy-nationwide-access-to-license-plate-readers/

## TL;DR
The FBI is seeking a single contract for nationwide access to automated license plate reader (ALPR) networks, likely from vendors like Flock or Motorola. Procurement documents suggest it wants historical and real-time vehicle movement data without case-specific warrants, effectively creating a national location-tracking system piggybacking on local police and private cameras. Hacker News discussion focuses on civil-liberties risks, the impossibility of truly siloing such data, arguments for banning ALPR collection outright, and making personal data a legal liability.

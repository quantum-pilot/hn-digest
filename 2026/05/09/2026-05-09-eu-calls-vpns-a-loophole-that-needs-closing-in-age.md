# EU calls VPNs "a loophole that needs closing" in age verification push

- Score: 369 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48072190) | Link: https://cyberinsider.com/eu-calls-vpns-a-loophole-that-needs-closing-in-age-verification-push/

## TL;DR

The European Parliamentary Research Service says VPNs are widely used by minors to evade new age-verification rules, calling them a “loophole” and noting some policymakers want age checks for VPN access themselves. Privacy advocates and VPN providers argue this would gut anonymity, increase surveillance risk, and is especially troubling given serious flaws found in the EU’s own “privacy-preserving” age-verification app. Hacker News discussion sees “protect the children” as a recurring justification for expanding state and corporate control, and questions the headline’s accuracy.

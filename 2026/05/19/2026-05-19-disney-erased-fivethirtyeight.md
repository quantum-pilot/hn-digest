# Disney erased FiveThirtyEight

- Score: 319 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48197703) | Link: https://www.natesilver.net/p/disney-erased-fivethirtyeight

### TL;DR

Nate Silver recounts how Disney/ESPN/ABC first overexpanded and then neglected FiveThirtyEight, ultimately wiping a decade of work by redirecting nearly all articles to ABC News. He argues Disney never seriously tried to make the property sustainable—refusing a likely-profitable paywall, cycling through bosses, and treating the site as a “rounding error” prestige play. Failed sale attempts (e.g., to The Athletic), COVID-era cuts, and a bungled successor election model preceded shutdown. Silver now runs a successful Substack but sees the deletion as a stark case of link rot and corporate misfit for niche data journalism.

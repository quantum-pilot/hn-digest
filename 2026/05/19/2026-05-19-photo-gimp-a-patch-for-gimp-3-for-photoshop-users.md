# Photo GIMP – A Patch for GIMP 3 for Photoshop Users

- Score: 189 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48167890) | Link: https://github.com/Diolinux/PhotoGIMP

## TL;DR
PhotoGIMP is a free configuration patch that makes GIMP 3+ look and behave more like Adobe Photoshop, easing the transition for existing Photoshop users. It reorganizes tools, applies Photoshop-style keyboard shortcuts, customizes layout and docks, maximizes canvas space, and adds its own splash screen and icon. Installation is just copying config files into the GIMP profile on Linux, Windows, or macOS; it’s reversible by deleting/ restoring the config folder and doesn’t touch brushes, fonts, or plug‑ins.

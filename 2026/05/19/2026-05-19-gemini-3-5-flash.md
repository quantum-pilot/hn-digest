# Gemini 3.5 Flash

- Score: 588 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48196570) | Link: https://blog.google/innovation-and-ai/models-and-research/gemini-models/gemini-3-5/

### TL;DR
Gemini 3.5 Flash is Google’s new “frontier-level” but fast model, tuned for long, multi-step “agentic” workflows: codebase refactors, financial-document review, data debugging, and multimodal UI/graphics generation. It anchors Google’s Antigravity agent platform, powers enterprise pilots at Shopify, Macquarie, Salesforce, Ramp, Xero, Databricks, and is now the default in the Gemini app, AI Search, and the new Gemini Spark personal agent. Google emphasizes improved safety and interpretability; HN discussion centers on quality quirks and sharply higher prices/quotas.

# ABC News has taken all FiveThirtyEight articles offline

- Score: 206 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48152553) | Link: https://twitter.com/baseballot/status/2055309076209492208

### TL;DR
ABC News has removed all FiveThirtyEight articles, erasing a widely respected archive of data-driven politics and sports journalism. Nate Silver says ABC refused to sell back the IP because he criticized their handling of the brand, fueling accusations of corporate pettiness and mismanagement. Commenters lament the loss of pioneering data visualizations and explain how 538’s business model was too election-dependent for Disney’s cost-cutting culture, even though the talent and audience clearly persisted in new independent venues.

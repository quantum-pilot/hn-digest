# Three Inverse Laws of AI

- Score: 336 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48023861) | Link: https://susam.net/inverse-laws-of-robotics.html

## TL;DR

The article proposes “inverse laws of robotics” that govern humans, not machines: (1) don’t anthropomorphize AI; (2) don’t defer to its outputs without verification; (3) never abdicate human responsibility for AI-driven actions. The author worries that conversational design, search-engine integration, and automation make uncritical trust and blurred accountability dangerously easy, especially in high-stakes domains. Hacker News debates whether these norms are realistic or enforceable, and whether better UX, regulation, and organizational practices are needed to embody them.

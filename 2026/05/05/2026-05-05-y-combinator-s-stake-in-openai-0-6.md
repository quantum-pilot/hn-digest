# Y Combinator's Stake in OpenAI (0.6%?)

- Score: 370 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48016534) | Link: https://daringfireball.net/2026/05/y_combinators_stake_in_openai

## TL;DR
John Gruber reports that Y Combinator owns roughly 0.6% of OpenAI, now worth over $5B at OpenAI’s $852B valuation. Since YC co-founder Paul Graham has a large indirect financial stake, Gruber argues that this should be disclosed whenever Graham is quoted defending Sam Altman’s character—especially given earlier reporting that Altman had “no equity” in OpenAI, except indirectly via YC. Hacker News discussion digs into dilution math, conflicts of interest, AGI hype, and Y Combinator’s incentives around AI coverage.

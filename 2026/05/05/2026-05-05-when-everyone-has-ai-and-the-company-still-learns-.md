# When everyone has AI and the company still learns nothing

- Score: 301 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48020063) | Link: https://www.robert-glaser.de/when-everyone-has-ai-and-the-company-still-learns-nothing/

- TL;DR  
  - The article argues that ubiquitous AI tools (Copilot, ChatGPT, agents) often boost individual productivity without improving organizational learning. Old change machinery—brown bags, CoEs, dashboards—can’t keep up with fast, highly local AI “loops” embedded in daily work. The author proposes three pillars: Agent Operations (control), Loop Intelligence (which AI loops actually create learning), and Agent Capabilities (how skills spread). HN discussion stresses that real bottlenecks are post‑dev processes, incentives for sharing AI know‑how are misaligned, and workers fear being optimized out.

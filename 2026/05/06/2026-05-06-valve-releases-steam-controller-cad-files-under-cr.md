# Valve releases Steam Controller CAD files under Creative Commons license

- Score: 968 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48037555) | Link: https://www.digitalfoundry.net/news/2026/05/valve-releases-steam-controller-cad-files-under-creative-commons-license

- TL;DR  
    - Valve has published full CAD models and engineering drawings for its new Steam Controller and Puck under a CC BY‑NC‑SA license, inviting modders to 3D‑print accessories like mounts, docks, and custom grips. The files document keep‑out zones so wireless performance and ergonomics aren’t compromised. HN commenters praise Valve’s user‑friendly stance, highlight benefits for repairability and accessibility (custom setups for disabled players), and debate whether Steam‑centric configuration is a walled garden or just a workaround for Microsoft’s Xbox‑biased controller stack.

- Comment pulse

# Appearing productive in the workplace

- Score: 623 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48038001) | Link: https://nooneshappy.com/article/appearing-productive-in-the-workplace/

### TL;DR
The article argues that generative AI lets people produce artifacts that look expert—code, architectures, documents—without the underlying competence or judgment. This breaks the historical link between artifact quality and author skill: novices become conduits for system output they can’t evaluate, while management rewards the appearance of momentum. Internally, AI inflates specs, reports, and status docs into unreadable slop, hiding real signals and gutting apprenticeship-style learning. The author urges narrow, verifiable AI use and treating trustworthy human judgment as the scarce, premium asset.

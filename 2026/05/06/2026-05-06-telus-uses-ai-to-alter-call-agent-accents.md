# Telus Uses AI to Alter Call-Agent Accents

- Score: 226 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48031109) | Link: https://letsdatascience.com/news/telus-uses-ai-to-alter-call-agent-accents-a3868f63

### TL;DR
Telus is using Tomato.ai’s real-time speech-to-speech system in its call centres to modify offshore agents’ accents, aiming to reduce “accent-related friction” for Canadian callers. The AI pipeline likely combines fast ASR, accent conversion, and neural vocoding to keep latency low while preserving naturalness. Labour groups call the practice deceptive and demand mandatory disclosure, raising questions about consent, worker rights, and voice privacy. Competitors Rogers and Bell say they have no plans to adopt similar technology, amid visible public backlash.

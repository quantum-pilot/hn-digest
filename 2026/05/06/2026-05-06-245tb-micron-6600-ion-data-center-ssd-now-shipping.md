# 245TB Micron 6600 ION Data Center SSD Now Shipping

- Score: 248 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48031867) | Link: https://investors.micron.com/news-releases/news-release-details/industry-leading-245tb-micron-6600-ion-data-center-ssd-now

### TL;DR
Micron’s 6600 ION is a 245TB QLC SSD, currently the world’s largest-capacity commercial SSD, aimed at AI, cloud, and hyperscale data lakes. In E3.L and U.2 form factors, it delivers ~5.6× rack density versus 44TB HDDs, using about half the power per equivalent capacity, promising big TCO and sustainability gains at exabyte scale. Hacker News discussion contrasts this enterprise leap with stagnant or rising consumer SSD capacities and prices, and dissects the drive’s modest write speed and endurance as deliberate tradeoffs.

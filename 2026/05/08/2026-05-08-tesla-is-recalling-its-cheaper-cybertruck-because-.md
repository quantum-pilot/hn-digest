# Tesla is recalling its cheaper Cybertruck because the wheels might fall off

- Score: 196 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48063240) | Link: https://www.theverge.com/transportation/926741/tesla-cybertruck-cheaper-recall

### TL;DR
Tesla is recalling every RWD Cybertruck Long Range sold so far—173 units of the $70,000 model—because their brake rotors can crack around the wheel studs. Cracks may let studs separate from the hub, risking wheels coming off under hard cornering or rough roads. Tesla has three related warranty claims but reports no crashes or injuries. The fix: replace all front and rear brake rotors, hubs, and lug nuts free. It’s the Cybertruck’s 11th recall, and this specific model is already discontinued.

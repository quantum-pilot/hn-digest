# Apple, Intel have reached preliminary chip-making deal

- Score: 186 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48066169) | Link: https://www.reuters.com/business/apple-intel-have-reached-preliminary-chip-making-deal-wsj-reports-2026-05-08/

## TL;DR
WSJ reports Apple and Intel have reached a preliminary deal for Intel to manufacture some chips for Apple devices, marking Intel's biggest foundry win yet and a partial diversification away from TSMC. The agreement, negotiated over the past year, follows Intel's recent Terafab partnership with Tesla and SpaceX and signals confidence in Intel's manufacturing roadmap. Details like process node, volumes, and which Apple products are involved remain unknown, but both stocks jumped on the news.

## LLM perspective
- View: This strengthens US-based advanced foundry competition and reduces Apple’s geopolitical and single-supplier risk.  
- Impact: Pressures TSMC on pricing, capacity guarantees, and US fab build-out; validates Intel’s IDM 2.0 turnaround narrative.  
- Watch next: Node choice (e.g., Intel 14A vs 18A), whether these are main SoCs or peripherals, and any parallel Samsung involvement.

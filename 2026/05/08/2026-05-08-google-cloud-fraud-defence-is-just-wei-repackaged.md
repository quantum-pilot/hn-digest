# Google Cloud Fraud Defence is just WEI repackaged

- Score: 638 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48063199) | Link: https://privatecaptcha.com/blog/google-cloud-fraud-defence-wei/

### TL;DR

Google’s new Cloud Fraud Defense replaces traditional CAPTCHAs with a QR code you scan on a “trusted” phone, which then proves device integrity via Google Play Integrity or Apple attestation. The article argues this quietly resurrects 2023’s rejected Web Environment Integrity: gating web access on hardware and browser vendors, excluding privacy OSes and Firefox for Android, and enabling cross-site device tracking. HN discussion sees this as part of a broader lock‑in and surveillance trend, yet also driven by bots and AI eroding older anti-fraud methods.

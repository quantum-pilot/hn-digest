# Googlebook

- Score: 559 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48111545) | Link: https://googlebook.google/

### TL;DR
Googlebook is Google’s upcoming Gemini-centric laptop line, due in 2026 and built by OEMs like Acer, Asus, Dell, HP, and Lenovo. The pitch: “intelligence as a spec” via deep Gemini integration, including a cursor-driven “Magic Pointer” that can act on anything on screen and natural-language tools for building desktop widgets. Tight Android integration lets you cast phone apps and browse phone files as if local. HN discussion centers on AI-first marketing, Google’s reliability, and real market fit.

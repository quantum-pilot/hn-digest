# Operation: Epic Furious

- Score: 323 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48109519) | Link: https://www.epicfurious.com/

### TL;DR
A minimalist site links to “Epic Furious,” a satirical video game skewering a modern Middle East war and its supposed motives. HN commenters focus less on the bare landing page and more on the game’s sharp writing and dark humor about U.S. foreign policy, Israel, and oil. The thread quickly turns into a debate over what the war is “for,” how openly leaders talk about seizing oil, and HN’s tendency to flag or bury political satire, echoing earlier Newgrounds-era anti-war games.

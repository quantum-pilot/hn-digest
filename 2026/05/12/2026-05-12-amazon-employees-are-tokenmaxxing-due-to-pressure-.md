# Amazon employees are "tokenmaxxing" due to pressure to use AI tools

- Score: 205 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48110529) | Link: https://arstechnica.com/ai/2026/05/amazon-employees-are-tokenmaxxing-due-to-pressure-to-use-ai-tools/

### TL;DR
Amazon built an internal agent platform, MeshClaw, that can act across systems (deploy code, triage email, Slack, etc.). Leadership set goals that 80%+ of developers use AI weekly and introduced token-usage leaderboards. Many employees now “tokenmaxx” by inventing pointless AI tasks just to inflate token counts, despite claims these stats won’t affect reviews. Staff also worry about security risks from autonomous agents. Commenters frame this as classic misaligned incentives and hype-driven management rather than genuine productivity gains.

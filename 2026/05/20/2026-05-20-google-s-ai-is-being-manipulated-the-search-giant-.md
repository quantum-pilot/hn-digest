# Google's AI is being manipulated. The search giant is quietly fighting back

- Score: 244 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48205782) | Link: https://www.bbc.com/future/article/20260519-google-tackles-attempts-to-hack-its-ai-results

## TL;DR
BBC reporters show how easily AI chatbots and Google’s AI Overviews can be “poisoned” by a single, well-crafted web page, and document real attempts to nudge answers about health supplements and retirement advice. Google has updated spam policies and is quietly experimenting with filters, caveats, and de-promoting obvious self‑promotion, but experts call this whack‑a‑mole and note conflicts with ad incentives. HN discussion questions Google’s interest in truth and fears AI-boosted manipulation will further degrade the web’s information quality.

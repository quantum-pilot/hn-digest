# College students drown out AI-praising commencement speeches with boos

- Score: 353 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48206241) | Link: https://www.tomshardware.com/tech-industry/artificial-intelligence/college-students-drown-out-ai-praising-commencement-speeches-with-boos-deal-with-it-one-speaker-fires-back-as-students-heckle-positive-pitches-for-ais-role

### TL;DR
Former Google CEO Eric Schmidt was loudly booed during his University of Arizona commencement speech when he framed AI as a rocket-ship students should board and help “shape.” Similar jeering met pro‑AI lines at Central Florida and Middle Tennessee State. Graduates, already anxious about debt, hiring freezes, and AI-linked layoffs, rejected what felt like triumphalist, out-of-touch messaging. HN commenters see a mix of justified labor anxiety, anger at tech elites’ behavior, and preexisting controversy around Schmidt himself driving the backlash.

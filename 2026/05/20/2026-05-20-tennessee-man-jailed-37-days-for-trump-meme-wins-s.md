# Tennessee man jailed 37 days for Trump meme wins settlement after lawsuit

- Score: 653 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48208502) | Link: https://www.fire.org/news/victory-tennessee-man-jailed-37-days-trump-meme-wins-835000-settlement-after-first-amendment

## TL;DR
Retired Tennessee law officer Larry Bushart spent 37 days in jail after sharing a Trump meme quoting “We have to get over it” about a different state’s school shooting. Sheriff Nick Weems obtained a warrant treating it as a local school threat and had Bushart held on a $2M bond. With legal help from FIRE, Bushart settled for $835k. HN commenters largely focus on who should pay such settlements, the lack of personal accountability for officials, and systemic threats to free speech.

## Comment pulse

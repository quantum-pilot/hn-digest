# Scientists warn Atlantic current at risk of shutting down

- Score: 154 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48084836) | Link: https://e360.yale.edu/features/amoc-climate-change

### TL;DR
Scientists fear the Atlantic Meridional Overturning Circulation (AMOC)—a key heat conveyor warming northern Europe and shaping global rainfall—may be approaching a collapse tipping point. Sparse but improving observations plus paleoclimate records show the system is weakening and has shut down before, causing drastic regional cooling and monsoon disruption. Recent model-based studies, constrained by real ocean data, now suggest roughly 50% weakening by 2100 and non‑trivial collapse odds, prompting calls to treat this as high‑impact tail risk and accelerate emissions cuts.

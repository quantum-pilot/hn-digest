# Louis Rossmann offers to pay legal fees for a threatened OrcaSlicer developer

- Score: 444 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48084432) | Link: https://www.tomshardware.com/3d-printing/louis-rossmann-tells-3d-printer-maker-bambu-lab-to-go-bleep-yourself-over-its-lawsuit-against-enthusiast-right-to-repair-advocate-offers-to-pay-the-legal-fees-for-a-threatened-orcaslicer-developer

### TL;DR
Louis Rossmann, a prominent Right to Repair activist, pledged $10k toward legal defense for OrcaSlicer developer Pawel Jarczak after Bambu Lab sent a cease-and-desist over his “OrcaSlicer-BambuLab” fork. Jarczak’s project restored direct control between OrcaSlicer and Bambu printers, which Bambu had previously blocked citing 30M “unauthorized” daily cloud requests. Rossmann is trying to rally community crowdfunding, framing this as a test case for ownership, interoperability, and repairability in increasingly cloud-locked 3D printers.

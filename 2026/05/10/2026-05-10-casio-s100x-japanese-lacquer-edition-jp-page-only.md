- TL;DR
  - Casio’s S100X Japanese Lacquer Edition is a flagship desktop calculator that fuses in‑house Japanese manufacturing with Echizen urushi lacquerware. Each aluminum-bodied unit is hand‑finished in tame‑nuri by a named craftsman, with serialized engraving, premium packaging, anti‑glare FSTN display, pantograph keyboard, three‑key rollover, durable double‑shot keycaps, and ergonomic details throughout. HN reactions split between desire and skepticism: some revel in the craftsmanship and urushi revival, others see an overbuilt status object and a clever price anchor for Casio’s “regular” premium S100X.

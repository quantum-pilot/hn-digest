# GameStop makes $55.5B takeover offer for eBay

- Score: 625 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48006402) | Link: https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/articles/cn0p8yled1do

### TL;DR
GameStop, the meme‑stock video game retailer now worth about $12B, has launched a surprise $55.5B cash‑and‑stock bid for eBay at $125/share, a 20% premium. CEO Ryan Cohen says he can cut $2B in eBay costs, use GameStop’s 1,600 stores for “live commerce,” and turn the combined business into an Amazon rival. Financing relies heavily on new debt in a leveraged‑buyout‑like structure, which analysts and HN commenters see as high‑risk, incentive‑driven, and unlikely to succeed.

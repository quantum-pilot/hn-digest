# Heat pump sales rise across Europe

- Score: 198 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48012003) | Link: https://www.pv-magazine.com/2026/05/04/heat-pump-sales-rise-17-across-europe-in-q1-as-energy-prices-surge/

### TL;DR
Heat pump sales in 11 European countries rose 17% year-on-year in Q1 2026 (575k units), with France, Germany, and Poland around 25% growth after gas and oil prices spiked when Iran closed the Strait of Hormuz. Austria fell 30% as subsidies lapsed, underscoring how incentives shape adoption. Studies already show heat pump + PV systems beating gas over 11–14 years; HN discussion centers on real-world economics, subsidies, technology choices (air vs ground source), and how to retrofit dense cities still locked into fossil systems.

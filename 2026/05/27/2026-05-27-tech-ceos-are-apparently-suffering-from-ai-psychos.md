# Tech CEOs are apparently suffering from AI psychosis

- Score: 526 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48295679) | Link: https://techcrunch.com/2026/05/27/tech-ceos-are-apparently-suffering-from-ai-psychosis/

### TL;DR
The article argues that many tech CEOs are in an “AI psychosis”: wildly overestimating what agents and LLMs can reliably do, then using that belief to justify aggressive restructurings and layoffs. Box CEO Aaron Levie says executives see polished demos but are too far from “last‑mile” work to grasp the supervision, domain knowledge, and cleanup humans still provide. Research shows modest, uneven productivity gains and looming managerial bottlenecks. HN commenters add that this is partly old CEO disconnect, now supercharged by seductive AI prototypes and hype.

# I think Anthropic and OpenAI have found product-market fit

- Score: 592 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48296794) | Link: https://simonwillison.net/2026/May/27/product-market-fit/

### TL;DR
Willison argues Anthropic and OpenAI finally hit real product‑market fit not with generic chatbots, but with coding/agent products like Claude Code and Codex. These tools consume far more tokens and are used by highly paid professionals, enabling enterprise contracts priced directly at full API rates rather than flat “all‑you‑can‑eat” seats. April 2026 marks a revenue inflection: higher model prices, enterprise token billing, huge compute deals (e.g., SpaceX/Colossus), and big enterprise‑sales hiring. HN pushes back on the economics, ROI, and open‑source threats.

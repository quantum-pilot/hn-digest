# Erin Brockovich made a map to track data centers around the country

- Score: 266 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48287952) | Link: https://www.niemanlab.org/2026/05/erin-brockovich-made-a-map-to-track-data-centers-around-the-country/

### TL;DR
Erin Brockovich has launched a public map of large, AI-focused and hyperscale data centers in the U.S., combining verified sites with crowdsourced community reports on construction, water and power use, and local impacts. It aims to visualize the physical footprint of the AI boom and supply journalists and residents with a shared evidence base. Hacker News discussion centers on duplication with existing industry maps, data quality and accuracy, and whether environmental fears—especially about water—are justified or exaggerated.

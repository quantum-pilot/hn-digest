# SimCity 3k in 4k (2025)

- Score: 256 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48297645) | Link: https://www.thran.uk/writ/hdid/2025/12/simcity-3k-in-4k.html

TL;DR
- Guide to running SimCity 3000 beautifully on modern Windows: swap in a GOG widescreen/no‑CD executable, add a Direct3D wrapper for true 4K, apply a 4GB RAM patch, disable the dead auto‑updater, and restore missing soundtrack tracks from CD. Discussion celebrates SC3K’s tight, legible simulation, evocative isometric art, and jazz, contrasting it with today’s photorealistic but often shallower city builders, while sharing nostalgia, arcology dreams, and details like Unlimited’s downgraded audio and abandoned multiplayer.

# Microsoft pulls plug on plans for 244-acre data center in Caledonia (2025)

- Score: 166 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48266422) | Link: https://www.tmj4.com/news/racine-county/microsoft-pulls-plug-on-plans-for-244-acre-data-center-in-caledonia-after-community-pushback

### TL;DR
Microsoft abandoned plans for a 244‑acre data center in Caledonia, Wisconsin after strong local pushback, including a 2,000‑signature petition against rezoning rural land. Residents and a village trustee say they aren’t anti‑Microsoft or anti‑development, but want earlier, direct engagement and a site better matched to village priorities. Microsoft still wants to invest in Southeast Wisconsin. HN commenters connect this to broader issues: permitting regimes that favor large firms, outsized project footprints, and distrust after past mega‑project failures like Foxconn.

# Rising seas will swallow New Orleans. People need to start relocating now

- Score: 124 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48265049) | Link: https://www.cnn.com/2026/05/25/climate/new-orleans-sea-level-rise-relocation

**TL;DR**

Scientists argue New Orleans is effectively committed to drowning: coastal Louisiana is sinking while seas rise, wetlands vanish, and levees concentrate risk. A Nature Sustainability study infers 3–7 m of eventual relative sea-level rise, enough to erase most remaining wetlands and potentially surround the city with Gulf water. The authors urge early, planned relocation to avoid chaotic, unequal retreat; residents fear cultural erasure and racist implementation. Hacker News debates perverse insurance incentives, subsidence vs climate framing, and whether “relocate now” overstates current-century risks.

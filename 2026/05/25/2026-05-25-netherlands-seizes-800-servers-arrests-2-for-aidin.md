# Netherlands Seizes 800 Servers, Arrests 2 for Aiding Cyberattacks

- Score: 261 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48266906) | Link: https://krebsonsecurity.com/2026/05/netherlands-seizes-800-servers-arrests-2-for-aiding-cyberattacks/

### TL;DR
Dutch financial crime investigators arrested MIRhosting founder Andrey Nesterenko and associate Youssef Zinad, seizing 800 servers allegedly used to support Stark Industries Solutions, a Russia‑linked hosting outfit tied to DDoS attacks, disinformation, and EU‑sanctioned entities. Assets previously moved from sanctioned Moldovan host PQHosting to “the[.]hosting” under WorkTitans BV, which relied on MIRhosting’s connectivity. Dutch media link this infrastructure to pro‑Russian attacks on Danish elections; MIRhosting denies knowingly aiding cybercrime or sanctions evasion. HN debates criminal hosting fronts, economics, and why the Netherlands is a cyber hub.

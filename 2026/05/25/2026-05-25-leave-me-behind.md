# Leave Me Behind

- Score: 321 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48265876) | Link: http://androidessence.com/leave-me-behind/

### TL;DR
An Android developer reflects on a decade of learning, hackathons, mentorship, and conference talks, realizing the real joy of software was never just shipping features but the human connections, shared struggle, and gradual mastery. LLMs, while now competent at generating and reviewing code, tempt him to outsource thinking, skip trial-and-error, and bypass communities like Stack Overflow. He argues this erodes both craftsmanship and camaraderie. If the future is mostly prompting machines instead of building with people, he’d rather be “left behind.”

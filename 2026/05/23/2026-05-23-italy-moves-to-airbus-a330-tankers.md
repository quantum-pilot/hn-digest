# Italy moves to Airbus A330 tankers

- Score: 226 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48248775) | Link: https://www.euronews.com/my-europe/2026/05/21/italy-moves-to-airbus-a330-tankers-in-major-nato-aligned-shift

- TL;DR  
Italy has signed a €1.39B deal for six Airbus A330 MRTT tankers, abandoning its earlier choice of Boeing’s KC‑46 Pegasus. Both aircraft are NATO‑compatible, but the switch shifts Italy’s logistics, training, and support ecosystem from a US‑centric model toward a stronger European industrial base. The A330 MRTT, already used by 18 other operators, boosts Italy’s long‑range refuelling, transport, and humanitarian capacity while underscoring Boeing’s broader decline in reliability, export competitiveness, and political attractiveness to European customers.

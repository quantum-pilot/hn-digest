# It's time to talk about my writerdeck

- Score: 244 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48250144) | Link: https://veronicaexplains.net/my-first-writerdeck/

## TL;DR
The author repurposes a six‑year‑old System76 laptop into a “writerdeck”: a distraction‑free, console‑only Debian machine dedicated to writing. Instead of a GUI, it boots straight into a tty running kmscon, tmux, and Neovim with Vimwiki, plus network‑manager for occasional syncing and Syncthing for backups. Battery/brightness controls are wired into tmux, and autologin plus an auto‑launched Vimwiki session make it “open lid → write.” The goal is intentional, single‑purpose tech that resists notification‑driven, browser‑centric distractions—HN debates whether this is focus or yak‑shaving.

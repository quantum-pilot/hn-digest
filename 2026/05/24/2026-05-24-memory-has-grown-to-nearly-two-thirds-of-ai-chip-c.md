# Memory has grown to nearly two-thirds of AI chip component costs

- Score: 266 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48258684) | Link: https://epoch.ai/data-insights/ai-chip-component-cost-shares

## TL;DR
Epoch AI estimates that by Q4 2025, high‑bandwidth memory accounts for 63% of AI accelerator component spending, up from 52% in early 2024, while logic, packaging, and other parts shrink as a share of cost. Total component outlays more than doubled to about $52B, with HBM driving most of the increase. Tight memory supply and hyperscaler capex guidance suggest further cost pressure. Hacker News discussion centers on future price normalization, DRAM market dynamics, and knock‑on effects for consumer hardware.

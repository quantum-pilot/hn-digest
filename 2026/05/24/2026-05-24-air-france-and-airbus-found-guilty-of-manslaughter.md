# Air France and Airbus found guilty of manslaughter over 2009 plane crash

- Score: 128 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48250980) | Link: https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/czd2qmdvmq6o

## TL;DR
In 2009, Air France flight AF447, an Airbus A330 from Rio to Paris, crashed into the Atlantic killing 228 people after pitot tube icing caused faulty airspeed data, autopilot disengagement, and a mishandled high‑altitude stall. A Paris appeals court has now found both Air France and Airbus guilty of manslaughter—reversing a 2023 acquittal—citing known sensor issues and inadequate pilot training. Each faces the maximum €225k fine, seen by families as symbolically important but financially trivial, with reputational damage the real penalty.

# Why is Vivado 2026.1 dropping Linux support for free tier?

- Score: 294 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48254309) | Link: https://adaptivesupport.amd.com/s/question/0D5Pd00001YQLdMKAX/why-is-vivado-20261-dropping-linux-support-for-free-tier-?language=en_US

### TL;DR
AMD’s 2026.1 Vivado release introduces paid tiers where only the Windows version is available in the free Basic tier; Linux requires at least a Core license. In AMD’s forum reply, a moderator stresses civility, says users can stay on the older free 2025.2 release, cites 70% Windows usage, and frames Basic as “entry‑level only” without explaining the Linux exclusion. Hacker News reactions are overwhelmingly negative, citing lost student mindshare, licensing friction, and plans to move designs and teaching to rival FPGA vendors.

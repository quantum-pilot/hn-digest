# Alexander Grothendieck Revolutionized 20th-Century Mathematics

- Score: 128 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48254020) | Link: https://www.quantamagazine.org/how-alexander-grothendieck-revolutionized-20th-century-mathematics-20260520/

- TL;DR  
Quanta explains how Alexander Grothendieck rebuilt algebraic geometry around ultra-abstract notions like schemes and sheaves, turning polynomial equations into geometric objects independent of any chosen number system. This unified previously incompatible foundations and enabled profound progress on Weil’s conjectures and number theory. The article sketches his intense, ascetic life and eventual retreat from mathematics. Hacker News discussion adds anecdotes, links to biographical material and translations of his work, and reflections on how his structural view of equality fits modern formalized mathematics.

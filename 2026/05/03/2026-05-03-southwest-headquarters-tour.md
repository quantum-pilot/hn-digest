### TL;DR
A developer recounts a fan tour of Southwest Airlines’ Dallas headquarters, moving through flight attendant and pilot training facilities, high-end 737 simulators, the Network Operations Center, and nighttime maintenance hangars. The visit emphasizes safety drills, operations resilience (tornado-proof simulator building), and the craft of long‑tenured technicians, alongside corporate culture touches like memorabilia, leadership offices, and a social-media “Listening Center.” HN readers riff on similar behind‑the‑scenes tours and how “ordinary” services hide enormous technical and logistical sophistication.

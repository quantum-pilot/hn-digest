# Preserving Fisher-Price Pixter

- Score: 202 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48091812) | Link: https://dmitry.gr/?r=05.Projects&proj=37.%20Pixter

### TL;DR
Dmitry.GR documents and preserves the entire Fisher‑Price Pixter ecosystem: Classic, Color, Plus/2.0, and Multimedia. He opens the toys, identifies the ultra‑cost‑reduced Sharp ARM SoCs, and discovers that “games” actually run on a custom 16‑bit stack VM interpreted by ARM code. He dumps ROMs via clever bus tapping, reverse‑engineers odd Melody Chips driven by a terrible two‑wire protocol, captures all music in analog, and adds full emulation (including NAND‑like Multimedia carts). HN discusses admiration, ethics of documentation, and concerns over the author’s online behavior.

# Starship V3

- Score: 308 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48116781) | Link: https://www.spacex.com/updates#starship-v3

## TL;DR

SpaceX’s Starship V3 is a major redesign of both booster and ship: Raptor 3 engines with higher thrust and ~1 ton/engine mass savings, simplified plumbing, integrated sensors, and a new hot‑stage setup. The vehicle, avionics, and Pad 2 are all optimized for rapid reuse, propellant transfer, and higher launch cadence. Alongside, SpaceX outlines Starship’s role in Artemis lunar landings, orbital Starlink “data-center” constellations, and airport‑like spaceport operations. HN discussion mixes technical enthusiasm with skepticism about biology, economics, and space‑based compute.

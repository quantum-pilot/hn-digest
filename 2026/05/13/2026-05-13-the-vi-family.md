# The vi family

- Score: 264 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48033483) | Link: https://lpar.ATH0.com/posts/2026/05/the-vi-family/

### TL;DR
The article is a compact history and catalog of the “vi family”: starting from the original 1977 ex/vi and tracing major clones (STevie, Elvis, xvi, Vile, Vim, nvi, BusyBox vi, Neovim, etc.), noting trade‑offs around size, scripting, large files, and UTF‑8. It also covers vi‑inspired modal editors like Kakoune, Helix, and Emacs modes (Viper/Evil). Recent forks (EVi, Vim Classic) explicitly reject LLM‑generated code in Vim, reflecting growing tension over AI‑authored core infrastructure.

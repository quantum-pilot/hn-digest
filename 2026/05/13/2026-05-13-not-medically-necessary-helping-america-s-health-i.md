# "Not Medically Necessary": Helping America's Health Insurers Deny Coverage

- Score: 155 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48126000) | Link: https://www.propublica.org/article/evicore-health-insurance-denials-cigna-unitedhealthcare-aetna-prior-authorizations

### TL;DR
ProPublica details how EviCore, a Cigna-owned contractor, helps major U.S. insurers deny or delay care for ~100M people via prior authorization. An adjustable AI “dial” routes more cases to human reviewers when higher denial rates or “savings” are needed, especially under risk-based contracts promising 3:1 ROI. Doctors and medical societies say EviCore’s rigid, sometimes outdated guidelines block appropriate care; regulators mostly issue small fines. A highlighted case links repeated denials of a heart cath to a patient’s subsequent death. HN comments focus on clinicians’ battles with opaque reviewers and the dangers of Medicare Advantage–style privatization.

# Removing the modem and GPS from my 2024 RAV4 hybrid

- Score: 531 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48138136) | Link: https://arkadiyt.com/2026/05/13/removing-the-modem-and-gps-from-my-rav4/

### TL;DR
The author treats their 2024 RAV4 Hybrid as a hostile telemetry device and removes its Data Communication Module (cell modem) and GPS antenna to stop location and driving data from reaching Toyota or brokers. They preserve almost all functionality via a DCM bypass harness that restores the microphone, with the main losses being OTA updates, SOS, and cloud services. They also avoid Bluetooth because it can tunnel data via the phone, using only wired CarPlay. Commenters probe Bluetooth tethering details, Apple/Google’s own telemetry collection, and simpler disable options like fuses or hidden “privacy modes.”

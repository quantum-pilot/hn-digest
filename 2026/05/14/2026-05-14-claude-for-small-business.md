# Claude for Small Business

- Score: 502 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48130950) | Link: https://www.anthropic.com/news/claude-for-small-business

### TL;DR
Anthropic is launching Claude for Small Business, a set of “agentic” workflows and connectors that embed Claude into tools like QuickBooks, PayPal, HubSpot, Canva, Docusign, Google Workspace, and Microsoft 365. Owners toggle it on in Claude Cowork, connect their stack, and let Claude draft payroll plans, reconcile books, chase invoices, and prepare campaigns, with human approval before money moves or messages send. The rollout bundles training courses, in‑person workshops, and nonprofit partnerships, while HN discusses usability, trust, and hidden labor.

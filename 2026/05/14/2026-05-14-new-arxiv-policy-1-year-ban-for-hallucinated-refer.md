# New arXiv policy: 1-year ban for hallucinated references

- Score: 201 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48140922) | Link: https://twitter.com/tdietterich/status/2055000956144935055

## TL;DR
arXiv is introducing a policy where submitting a paper with hallucinated or fabricated references can trigger a 1‑year ban, after which the author’s future submissions must first be accepted at reputable peer‑reviewed venues. The community debate centers on whether this is proportionate: some see it as essential to curb growing “AI slop” and protect the literature, while others argue it harshly punishes even one‑off, negligent AI use. Discussion also highlights coauthor liability, vetting before sanctions, and automated fraud detection.

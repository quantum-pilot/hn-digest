# Grand Theft Oil Futures: Insider traders keep making a killing at our expense

- Score: 476 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48047981) | Link: https://paulkrugman.substack.com/p/grand-theft-oil-futures

### TL;DR
- Paul Krugman highlights repeated, gigantic oil‑futures bets placed minutes before Trump’s Iran War de‑escalation news, arguing this is brazen insider trading tolerated by the administration. He explains that oil futures exist mainly so producers and users can hedge risk; if insiders routinely front‑run policy, honest hedgers exit, shrinking real‑economy benefits and deepening a “predation economy” where connections beat competence. Commenters extend this to war profiteering, weak US institutional checks, and the structural disadvantage of non‑insider traders.

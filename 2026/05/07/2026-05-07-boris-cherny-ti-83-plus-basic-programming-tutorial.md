# Boris Cherny: TI-83 Plus Basic Programming Tutorial (2004)

- Score: 183 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48014521) | Link: https://www.ticalc.org/programming/columns/83plus-bas/cherny/

### TL;DR
- A 2004 tutorial walks students through TI‑83 Plus BASIC from scratch: screen I/O, labels and loops, conditionals, variables, random numbers, lists/matrices, drawing, and game-like challenges. It treats the calculator as a tiny, self-contained computer, with step‑by‑step examples and exercises (menus, animations, guessing games, movement, scoring). Hacker News readers mostly reminisce about discovering programming on TI calculators in slow math classes, how that experience launched careers, and how magical constrained, offline tinkering felt compared with today’s tooling.

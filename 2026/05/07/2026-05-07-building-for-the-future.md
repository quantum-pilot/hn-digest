# Building for the Future

- Score: 195 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48054423) | Link: https://blog.cloudflare.com/building-for-the-future/

### TL;DR
Cloudflare is laying off more than 1,100 employees worldwide, framing it as a one-time restructuring to redesign the company for an “agentic AI era,” not a cost-cutting or performance-based move. The founders stress extensive internal AI adoption and pair the announcement with unusually generous severance: full base pay through the end of 2026, extended healthcare (US through year-end), and extra equity vesting. Hacker News discussion questions the euphemistic framing, whether AI truly drives productivity, and if rising AI costs are actually forcing cuts.

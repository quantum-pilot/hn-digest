# The Companies Cutting Headcount for AI Will Lose to the Ones Who Didn't

- Score: 196 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48234547) | Link: https://libertas.software/en/knowledge-hub/19/the-companies-cutting-headcount-for-ai-will-lose-to-the-ones-who-didnt

## TL;DR
The article argues that companies using AI mainly to cut headcount are destroying their hardest‑to‑rebuild asset: institutional knowledge and human judgment. The winning strategy is to keep experienced teams and use AI to strip away low‑value tasks, letting the same people handle more work, deeper analysis, and better decisions. Hacker News commenters are skeptical of AI-as-hero narratives, pointing instead to over‑hiring, higher interest rates, and product‑maturity cycles as primary drivers of current layoffs, with AI often a convenient cover story.

## Comment pulse

- Augment vs replace tension → some say AI productivity gains inevitably shrink headcount; others argue real driver is weak vision and fear around future AI-driven revenue.

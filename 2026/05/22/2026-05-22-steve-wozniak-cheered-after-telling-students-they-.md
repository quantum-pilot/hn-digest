# Steve Wozniak cheered after telling students they have AI – actual intelligence

- Score: 583 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48233563) | Link: https://www.businessinsider.com/steve-wozniak-apple-ai-graduation-speech-2026-5

### TL;DR
Steve Wozniak used his Grand Valley State University commencement speech to flip AI anxiety into encouragement, joking that graduates already have “AI: actual intelligence.” He framed current AI as repetitive, brain‑mimicking machinery, while urging students to “think different” and pursue distinctive paths instead of standard career templates. Hacker News readers contrasted his empathy and humor with other AI‑praising speakers who were booed, debated how much AI really threatens human value and work, and warned against indiscriminate corporate AI mandates.

# The IBM-ification of Google?

- Score: 197 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48230049) | Link: https://zeroshot.bearblog.dev/google-is-shattering-under-its-own-weight-the-ibm-ification-of-google/

### TL;DR
The author argues Google is “IBM-ifying”: huge, profitable, but losing trust, excitement, and product quality. Vertical integration (TPUs, data centers, models, Android, Search) hasn’t become an unbeatable moat; instead, it amplifies bad behavior. Examples include arbitrary GCP bans, a vast product graveyard, degraded Search with AI Overviews that cannibalize the open web, YouTube flooded with low-effort AI content, and Android/Gmail dark patterns. Like IBM, Google risks becoming a resented, stagnant utility propped up mainly by its ad monopoly.

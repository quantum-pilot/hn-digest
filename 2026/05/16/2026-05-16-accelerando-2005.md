# Accelerando (2005)

- Score: 233 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48159241) | Link: https://www.antipope.org/charlie/blog-static/fiction/accelerando/accelerando.html

### TL;DR
Charles Stross’s *Accelerando* (2005, serialized from 1998) is a Creative‑Commons‑licensed novel following Manfred Macx and his descendants through an AI‑driven economic and technological “takeoff” into full posthumanity. AR glasses, autonomous software agents, uploaded lobsters, self‑litigating corporations, and Dyson-sphere-scale computronium sketch a world where human institutions and, eventually, humans themselves are outcompeted. HN readers see it as uncannily predictive of current AI agents and corporate automation, but, on reread, more tragedy and warning than techno‑optimism.

# Ploopy Bean: a trackpoint for every computer

- Score: 163 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48114208) | Link: https://ploopy.co/shop/bean-pointing-stick/

## TL;DR
Ploopy’s Bean Pointing Stick is an open‑source, USB pointing‑stick mouse that brings a ThinkPad‑style “nipple” to any computer. The 3D‑printed device is fully assembled, uses QMK firmware with VIA support for easy remapping, and includes four Omron D2LS‑21 buttons for precise clicks. It’s sold via preorder tiers with a May 6, 2026 launch and staggered shipping windows. Modding is explicitly encouraged, but any firmware or hardware modification voids the included one‑year warranty.

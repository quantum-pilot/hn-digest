# The X-Files has made me nostalgic for a time I never experienced

- Score: 154 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47977583) | Link: https://midnightmurmurations.substack.com/p/the-x-files-has-made-me-nostalgic

- TL;DR  
  An iPhone-era fan bingeing The X-Files falls in love with its pre-social-media 1990s world: single‑purpose gadgets, film cinematography, physical investigations, and tightly knit—if sometimes cultish—communities. The show becomes a portal to a time that feels slower, more intentional, and less algorithmically poisoned, in stark contrast to today’s “AI slop,” doomscrolling, fragile attention, and distrustful internet. She argues we’ve lost ordinary human life—unplanned visits, phone calls, local mystery—even as we gained powerful tools, and urges living more like ’90s villagers now.

# Ti-84 Evo

- Score: 212 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47979583) | Link: https://education.ti.com/en/products/calculators/graphing-calculators/ti-84-evo

### TL;DR
TI’s new TI-84 Evo is a modernized, exam-approved graphing calculator: ARM Cortex CPU at 156 MHz (≈3x faster than the 84+ CE), larger 319×209 color display, USB‑C, icon-based UI, integrated Python and TI‑Basic, and on-device “points of interest” graph analysis. It’s pitched as a durable, distraction‑free tool bundled with multi‑year online licenses. HN discussion mixes nostalgia for Z80-era hacking, criticism of price and exam-driven lock-in, and debate over real educational value vs cheap scientific calculators.

# Spotify adds 'Verified' badges to distinguish human artists from AI

- Score: 187 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47976856) | Link: https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c5yerr4m1yno

### TL;DR
Spotify is rolling out a “Verified by Spotify” green checkmark to indicate that an artist is a real human, based on signals like linked social accounts, merch, live shows, and listener activity. It explicitly does *not* certify that music was made without AI, only that there’s a person behind the profile. Critics argue this favors established, commercial acts and sidesteps user demands to clearly label AI-generated tracks. Hacker News discussion centers on money incentives, authenticity, and why AI music is artistically underwhelming so far.

# Grok 4.3

- Score: 372 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47972447) | Link: https://docs.x.ai/developers/models/grok-4.3

### TL;DR
Grok 4.3 is xAI’s latest general model, notable for being very fast, inexpensive, and concise while offering near-frontier quality for everyday chat and agentic tasks. Commenters praise its natural, human-like tone (especially for ESL users), strong voice mode, and live access to X/Twitter data, which makes it useful as a search/news assistant. Benchmarks suggest its reasoning—especially for coding—lags behind the newest OpenAI/Anthropic models, and users complain its apps and integrations are underpowered, with no memory or project tooling.

### Comment pulse

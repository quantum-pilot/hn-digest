# City Learns Flock Accessed Cameras in Children's Gymnastics Room as a Sales Demo

- Score: 264 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47978370) | Link: https://www.404media.co/city-learns-flock-accessed-cameras-in-childrens-gymnastics-room-as-a-sales-pitch-demo-renews-contract-anyway/

### TL;DR
Flock Safety employees used live feeds from Dunwoody, Georgia cameras—including a children’s gymnastics room, pool, school, playground, and Jewish community center—as sales demos under a “demo partner” agreement. A resident uncovered this via access logs obtained through public-records requests and publicized it, prompting outrage over privacy, consent, and sensitive locations. Flock says cities authorized the access, frames it as testing rather than “spying,” and promises to stick to more public locations, while the city nevertheless renewed Flock’s surveillance contract.

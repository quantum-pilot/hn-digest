# Job Postings for Software Engineers Are Rapidly Rising

- Score: 223 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47982512) | Link: https://www.citadelsecurities.com/news-and-insights/2026-global-intelligence-crisis/

### TL;DR
Citadel argues fears of an imminent AI‑driven jobs collapse are overstated. Using unemployment, capex, data‑center buildout, and Indeed data, they claim software‑engineering postings are up ~11% YoY and AI adoption at work remains gradual, following a classic S‑curve. They emphasize compute, energy, and organizational constraints, plus macro identities: productivity shocks are positive supply shocks, not automatic depressions, and AI is more likely to complement than replace labor. HN commenters push back on the statistics, debate real hiring trends, and discuss how LLMs reshape—not erase—software work.

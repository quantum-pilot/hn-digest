# NetHack 5.0.0

- Score: 348 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47988776) | Link: https://nethack.org/v500/release.html

### TL;DR
NetHack 5.0.0 is the first major new branch since 3.6, modernizing the classic roguelike’s internals while tweaking balance and tooling. The codebase is now C99-compliant, gains better cross-compilation support, and replaces legacy yacc/lex-based generators with Lua-driven data and level processing at runtime, opening doors for mods and tooling. Over 3100 fixes and changes land, but old saves and bones are incompatible. Hacker News discussion mixes nostalgia, concern over toolchain changes, excitement about Lua extensibility, and debate over graphics vs ASCII.

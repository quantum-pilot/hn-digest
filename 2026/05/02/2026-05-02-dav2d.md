# Dav2d

- Score: 303 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47988504) | Link: https://code.videolan.org/videolan/dav2d

### TL;DR
Dav2d is a new open-source software decoder for the upcoming AV2 video codec, designed for maximum speed, small footprint, and portability across platforms. AV2 is positioned as the successor to AV1, promising significantly improved compression for streaming and conferencing, though its real-world impact depends on encoders, hardware support, and licensing. HN discussion mixes excitement over a fast early decoder with concerns about patent pools stifling “royalty‑free” codecs and frustration at anti‑bot defenses deployed to block AI scrapers.

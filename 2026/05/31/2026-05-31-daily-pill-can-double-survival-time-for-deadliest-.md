# Daily pill can double survival time for deadliest cancer, trial shows

- Score: 148 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48346629) | Link: https://www.theguardian.com/society/2026/may/31/daily-pill-daraxonrasib-double-survival-time-pancreatic-pancreas-cancer-clinical-trial

## TL;DR
A phase 3 trial in 500 patients with metastatic pancreatic cancer found that the oral drug daraxonrasib roughly doubled overall survival: 13.2 months versus about 6.6–6.7 months on standard chemotherapy. The pill targets Kras, a Ras-family protein mutated in over 90% of common pancreatic cancers, by “gluing” and shutting down the Ras(On) signaling state. Researchers call it a landscape-changing advance with fewer classic chemo toxicities, though still harsh. HN commenters highlight real-world side effects, trial design nuances, and comparisons with AI-style funding.

# AWS Fired the One Employee Who Gave a Damn

- Score: 191 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48279321) | Link: https://www.seuros.com/blog/aws-fired-the-human-who-made-the-difference/

### TL;DR
An AWS customer recounts how his 10‑year account was deleted, then rescued only because one employee, open‑source veteran Tarus Balog, personally escalated it to CEO level. That “above and beyond” act became Balog’s proudest achievement at AWS—yet leadership barely noticed and later laid him off amid AI‑centric restructuring and cost cuts. The piece argues this reflects a broader pattern: hyperscalers celebrating “customer obsession” while optimizing away the humans who actually practice it, even as AI‑driven automation causes serious outages.

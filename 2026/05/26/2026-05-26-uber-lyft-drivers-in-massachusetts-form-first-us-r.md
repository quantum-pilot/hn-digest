# Uber, Lyft drivers in Massachusetts form first US ride-share union

- Score: 262 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48281509) | Link: https://www.reuters.com/business/world-at-work/uber-lyft-drivers-massachusetts-form-first-us-ride-share-union-2026-05-26/

## TL;DR
Massachusetts has certified the App Drivers Union to represent about 70,000 Uber and Lyft drivers, the first officially recognized ride‑share union in the US. Enabled by a 2024 state ballot measure, it creates a state‑level bargaining path for gig workers who remain independent contractors under federal law. The move follows a settlement setting a $32.50/hour pay floor and is watched as a test case for similar efforts elsewhere, amid debate about driver exploitation, market dynamics, automation, and alternatives to the current platform model.

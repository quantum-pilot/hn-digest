# UCLA discovers first stroke rehabilitation drug to repair brain damage (2025)

- Score: 176 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48098261) | Link: https://stemcell.ucla.edu/news/ucla-discovers-first-stroke-rehabilitation-drug-repair-brain-damage

### TL;DR
UCLA researchers mapped how physical rehab restores brain function after stroke: by re‑synchronizing distant networks via parvalbumin interneurons and gamma oscillations. They then developed DDL‑920, a drug that specifically excites these neurons and, in mice, fully reproduced the motor gains of intensive rehabilitation by reconnecting disrupted circuits far from the stroke site. The infarct core remains dead tissue, but this offers a first potential “molecular rehab” that could one day augment or partially replace labor‑intensive stroke therapy, pending safety and human trials.

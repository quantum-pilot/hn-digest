# Gmail registration now requires scanning a QR code and sending a text message

- Score: 542 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48092028) | Link: https://discuss.privacyguides.net/t/google-account-registration-now-requires-sending-an-sms-via-phone-instead-of-receiving-an-sms/36082

### TL;DR
Google is rolling out a new Gmail/Google account registration flow that shows a QR code; scanning it on your phone opens an SMS to Google that you must send, instead of receiving an SMS code. This makes automated/”burner SMS” signups harder and further erodes practical anonymity, especially where SIMs require ID or for people with dumb phones. HN discussion focuses on Google’s monopoly-like role in email, worsening spam, lock-in risks for businesses, and how this QR/SMS scheme actually works.

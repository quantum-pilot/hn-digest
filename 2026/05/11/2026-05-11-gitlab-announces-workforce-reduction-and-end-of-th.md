# GitLab Announces Workforce Reduction and End of Their CREDIT Values

- Score: 243 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48100500) | Link: https://about.gitlab.com/blog/gitlab-act-2/

## TL;DR
GitLab is doing a transparent but significant restructuring: shrinking its country footprint, cutting management layers, reorganizing R&D into ~60 small teams, and heavily automating workflows with AI “agents.” Strategically, it’s betting that software will be mostly machine-built, so it’s rebuilding its infrastructure, orchestration, data model, and governance for agent-scale workloads. Culturally, it’s retiring its CREDIT values (including explicit DEI) in favor of “Speed with Quality,” “Ownership Mindset,” and “Customer Outcomes.” HN readers see AI hype, DEI rollback, and trust risks.

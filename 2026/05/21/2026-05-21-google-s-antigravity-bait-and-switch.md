# Google's Antigravity bait and switch

- Score: 502 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48222529) | Link: https://www.0xsid.com/blog/antigravity-bait-n-switch

### TL;DR
Google’s Antigravity IDE users found their editor silently replaced by a new “2.0” conversational agent after I/O 2026, breaking workflows, paths, and history. The author had to completely purge Antigravity to reinstall the legacy IDE, losing settings and chats, and argues background updates shouldn’t swap products. HN commenters share recovery scripts, criticize 2.0’s usability regressions, and frame this as another example of Google’s chaotic product strategy and weak developer focus, helping explain its struggles in AI tooling.

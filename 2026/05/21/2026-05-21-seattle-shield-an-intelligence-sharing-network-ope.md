# Seattle Shield, an intelligence-sharing network operated by the Seattle police

- Score: 397 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48226588) | Link: https://prismreports.org/2026/05/20/seattle-shield-private-companies-surveillance/

### TL;DR
Seattle Shield is a Seattle Police–run intelligence-sharing network linking local businesses, big tech (Amazon, Meta), federal agencies (ICE, FBI), and other police departments. Companies file “suspicious activity” reports—often about protests—that are circulated to hundreds of law‑enforcement and private security recipients nationwide, with unclear retention, criteria, or oversight. Critics fear it functions as an informal watchlist, especially under a Trump-era directive treating protest speech as terrorism “indicia.” HN readers split between viewing this as sensationalized clickbait and as another step in entrenched panoptic surveillance.

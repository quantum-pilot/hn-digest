# BBEdit 16

- Score: 252 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48226944) | Link: https://www.barebones.com/products/bbedit/bbedit16.html

### TL;DR
BBEdit 16, a long‑running Mac text editor, adds over 100 changes focused on automation, performance, and web work. Major features include deep Shortcuts/App Intents support, grep-capable multi-file OCR search in images, per-project color schemes, HTML5 syntax checking, basic vi keybindings, faster SFTP, Git usability tweaks, and improved streaming AI chat worksheets. Upgrades are paid but non-subscription, with discounts by prior version. HN users mostly praise its sustainability, longevity, and extensibility, while also noting strong free alternatives on macOS.

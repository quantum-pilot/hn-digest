# Tesla Solar Roof is on life support as it pivot to panels

- Score: 138 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48165980) | Link: https://electrek.co/2026/05/14/tesla-solar-roof-promise-vs-reality-pivot-panels/

### TL;DR

Tesla’s Solar Roof, pitched in 2016 as a mass-market, beautiful solar shingle system, never scaled: against a goal of 1,000 installs per week, only about 3,000 systems were deployed total. Costs ran roughly $46k above a roof-plus-panels, with 15–25 year payback and widespread underperformance and support complaints. Tesla has effectively frozen the tile product, pivoting to conventional TSP-420 panels and massive US manufacturing plans, while Hacker News debates the flawed economics, tile-level complexity, and whether Solar Roof was sincere or just SolarCity theater.

# AI is a technology not a product

- Score: 303 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48168626) | Link: https://daringfireball.net/2026/05/ai_is_technology_not_a_product

- TL;DR  
John Gruber argues Apple doesn’t need a single “killer AI product”; AI is a general-purpose technology that, like wireless, should quietly permeate existing devices and experiences. He dismisses predictions that autonomous agents will displace smartphones or routinely summon rides unasked as implausible and user-hostile. HN commenters mostly agree, emphasizing invisible, task-focused improvements (e.g., a competent Siri) over branding, and predicting AI inference will become commodity infrastructure where hardware, interface design, and real user value determine the real winners.

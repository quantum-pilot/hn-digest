# EU weighs restricting use of US cloud platforms to process government data

- Score: 137 | [HN](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48171015) | Link: https://www.osnews.com/story/144943/eu-weighs-restricting-use-of-us-cloud-platforms-to-process-sensitive-government-data/

### TL;DR
The EU is considering rules to stop member governments from putting sensitive data on US cloud providers like AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud. The article argues this should have happened decades ago, citing US surveillance powers, political hostility, and extraterritorial control as reasons Europe must own its digital infrastructure. Commenters highlight that mere EU data-locality doesn’t shield against US laws or vendor killswitches, that governments could just run on-prem, and that EU dependency stems from political capture, convenience, and deep vendor lock-in.
